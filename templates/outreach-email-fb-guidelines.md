# Outreach email guidelines (Facebook Leads)

- Sound like a neighbor who did homework — reference city, post captions, or services from scrape.
- Never use: "does a lot of the work", "hours, menu, and directions" (unless food vertical).
- Preview must name 2–3 mockup sections actually built.
- Gain-only framing — never criticize Facebook.
- 110 words max in email without counting the link.
- Subject lines feel handwritten, under 8 words when possible.
- Subject should feel custom for each business.
