I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Della’s Airbrush Spray Tan**, intentionally crafted to stand apart from generic local-business templates.

### Design Rationale:
*   **Visual Identity**: I established a "Coastal Glow" identity, utilizing a sophisticated palette of soft creams, sandy neutrals, and rich bronze accents that reflect the Gulf Breeze aesthetic.
*   **Typography**: The high-contrast **Bodoni Moda** serif for headings provides an editorial, high-fashion feel, while clean sans-serif body text ensures readability and an airy, modern atmosphere.
*   **Unique Motif**: I've implemented a recurring **arched frame motif** for imagery and key layout elements, creating a cohesive, high-end "boutique" visual language that is both memorable and premium.
*   **Layout Strategy**: Instead of a standard grid, I used asymmetrical sections and overlapping elements in the hero to create a sense of depth and curated design.
*   **Credibility & Services**: The "Rituals" section elevates the service menu to a bespoke experience, while the "Della Difference" narrative builds immediate trust through a sophisticated, story-driven layout.
