Kenneth L. Stapleton, M.A., L.P.

For over four decades, I have dedicated my career to supporting the mental health needs of the Grand Rapids community. My approach is grounded in a deep respect for the individual and a commitment to providing high-quality, professional psychological services.

As a Licensed Psychologist (L.P.) with a Master of Arts (M.A.), I combine extensive clinical experience with a compassionate understanding of the human condition. Whether you are navigating a difficult life transition or managing a chronic mental health condition, my goal is to provide a stable, professional environment where you feel heard and supported.
