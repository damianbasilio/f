Since 1954, Tegeler Wrecker & Crane has set the standard for heavy recovery in Iowa and Illinois. What began as a local service has evolved into a regional powerhouse of specialized industrial logistics.

Our philosophy is simple: precision is non-negotiable. Every lift, every tow, and every wrecking project is approached with mechanical rigor and safety as the primary directive. We are not just service providers; we are your partners in overcoming physics.
