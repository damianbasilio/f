I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for Tegeler Wrecker & Crane, moving away from generic local-business templates to create a high-impact, industrial visual identity.

### Design Highlights:
*   **"Iron & Asphalt" Visual Identity:** I established a high-contrast palette of deep charcoal and "machinery yellow" (#F2B705) to reflect the raw power of heavy-duty operations.
*   **Asymmetric Editorial Layout:** The site uses an unconventional grid and bold, condensed typography (Barlow Condensed) to create a premium, memorable first impression that feels "engineered" rather than assembled.
*   **High-Impact Industrial Imagery:** I generated a custom set of professional photographs—including a dramatic dusk shot of a 75-ton rotator and architectural close-ups of mechanical components—to anchor the "Precision Under Pressure" brand narrative.
*   **Production-Ready Structure:** The landing page includes a specialized "Industrial Capabilities" grid, a technical "Request Service" form, and the requested #visit section with a dedicated map slot.

The design is fully responsive and uses semantic HTML/CSS to ensure a fast, professional experience across all devices.
