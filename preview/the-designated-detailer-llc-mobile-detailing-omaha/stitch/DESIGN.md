I have designed a bespoke, premium landing page for **The Designated Detailer, LLC**. 

### Design Rationale:
*   **Visual Identity**: I moved away from common "car wash" aesthetics, opting for a high-contrast **"Technical Luxury"** theme. The palette uses deep slates and metallic silvers to reflect the high-end vehicles being serviced.
*   **Editorial Layout**: The design features an asymmetrical grid and large-scale, authoritative typography to create a memorable, concierge-level first impression.
*   **Bespoke Imagery**: I generated custom, moody automotive photography (paint reflections, interior stitching, and precision brushwork) to emphasize the "master craftsmanship" aspect of the business.
*   **Technical Excellence**: The page is fully responsive, features smooth-scroll navigation, and include a dedicated `#visit` section with the requested empty map slot for future integration.

### Key Sections:
*   **Hero**: A high-impact cinematic visual of automotive perfection.
*   **Credibility**: Direct links to Facebook ratings and certification status.
*   **Service Tiers**: Clearly defined phases of care, from exterior correction to recurring maintenance packages.
*   **Craftsmanship Section**: Highlights the precision and veteran-owned nature of the business.
*   **Concierge Form**: A sleek, dark-themed lead capture form tailored for high-end clients.

The site is now production-ready and uniquely tailored to the Omaha market and the business's specific brand voice.
