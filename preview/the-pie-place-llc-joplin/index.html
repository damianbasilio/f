Our Story

Born in Joplin,
Refined by Time.

The Pie Place began with a single wood-fired oven and a commitment to never take a shortcut. Twenty years later, we still use the same hand-cranked mixers and locally milled flour that defined our first batch.

"A pie is only as good as the hands that shaped it. We believe in the slow way, the right way, the Joplin way."
