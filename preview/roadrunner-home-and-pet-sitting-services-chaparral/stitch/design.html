Founded by Dale and Laura, Roadrunner Home and Pet Sitting was born from a simple observation: high-desert homeowners require more than just a visitor—they require a steward.

With over a decade of experience navigating the unique challenges of Las Cruces estates—from irrigation nuances during monsoon season to the specialized needs of desert-dwelling pets—we bring a level of professional precision that is unmatched in the region.
