I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Roadrunner Home and Pet Sitting Services**. 

This design breaks away from the typical local business template, instead leaning into a "High-Desert Editorial" aesthetic that feels premium and trustworthy.

### Design Highlights:
- **Visual Identity**: I established a palette of **Deep Terracotta, Sage Brush, and Midnight Slate**, reflecting the unique landscape of Las Cruces and Chaparral.
- **Typographic Rhythm**: High-contrast serif headings (Playfair Display) provide an authoritative yet warm editorial feel, paired with generous whitespace to ensure the brand feels premium.
- **Bespoke Layouts**: 
    - The **Hero** uses a cinematic photography style to immediately establish a sense of "home stewardship."
    - The **Services** section utilizes an asymmetrical composition to avoid a generic grid look, focusing on the "stewardship" of precious assets.
    - **Visual Motif**: Thin horizontal rules in terracotta are used throughout the page as a recurring "horizon" motif, tying the sections together with a subtle geographic nod.
- **Production Ready**: The site includes the requested map placeholder, a refined contact form, and full responsive behavior.

The code is semantic, utilizes CSS variables for the color system, and features a clean mobile navigation and smooth scroll interactions.
