I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Green Speed Towing** that breaks away from traditional local-business templates. 

### Design Decisions:
*   **Visual Identity:** I established a high-performance "Velocity Precision" brand using a deep **Midnight Forest** green and a vibrant **Electric Lime** accent. This palette avoids the generic yellow/orange associated with towing and positions the brand as a premium, elite service.
*   **The "Speed Line" Motif:** Throughout the design, you'll see sharp diagonal rules and technical borders that create a sense of forward motion and precision, reinforcing the "Green Speed" name.
*   **Editorial Typography:** I used bold, condensed headlines (Archivo Narrow) and a strict typographic hierarchy to command attention. The hero section features a massive headline that anchors the page with authority.
*   **Specialized Layout:** Instead of a standard card grid, I used a **Split List** approach for service capabilities. This layout uses large number indices (01, 02, 03) and high-contrast imagery to tell a more intentional story about their technical expertise.
*   **Technical Density:** Sections like "Precision Stats" and the "Metro Coverage" map use a minimalist, mono-spaced aesthetic to suggest logistical sophistication and rapid response capabilities.

The result is a memorable, high-fidelity first impression that feels both urgent and professional.

How does this new visual direction feel to you? We could explore adding motion to the speed lines or refining the "Specialized Response" imagery further.

## Brand & Style
The brand identity for this design system is built on the intersection of high-performance engineering and urgent, premium service. It avoids the cluttered, "budget" aesthetic of traditional roadside assistance in favor of a sophisticated, editorial approach.

The design style is **High-Contrast / Modern Minimalism** with a **Technical** edge. It utilizes a "Speed Line" motif—diagonal 45-degree accents and razor-sharp intersections—to evoke a sense of rapid movement and mechanical precision. The aesthetic is raw and powerful, yet meticulously organized, suggesting a service that is both incredibly fast and highly professional.

**Key Visual Principles:**
- **Urgency through Contrast:** High-value accents against deep, moody backgrounds.
- **Precision:** Perfect alignment and thin, technical strokes.
- **Mechanical Strength:** Use of condensed, heavy typography and sharp, unyielding corners.
- **Editorial Pace:** Generous whitespace to ensure clarity during high-stress user interactions.

## Colors
The palette is designed to stand out in the towing industry by replacing generic yellows with a high-visibility, technical "Electric Lime."

- **Midnight Forest (#062C20):** Our primary brand anchor. Used for deep backgrounds, primary buttons, and heavy headings to convey authority and stability.
- **Electric Lime (#C1FF72):** The high-visibility accelerator. Used exclusively for primary calls to action, urgent status indicators, and the "Speed Line" motif.
- **Cool Asphalt (#F2F4F7):** The main interface background. It provides a clean, neutral slate that allows the dark greens and bright limes to pop.
- **Onyx Technical (#1A1C1E):** A deep neutral used for text and thin borders to maintain a professional, architectural feel.

## Typography
Typography is the core of the "Speed" narrative. 

**Archivo Narrow** is used for all headings. Its condensed proportions and heavy weights suggest structural strength and efficiency. It should almost always be set in uppercase for headlines to maximize the "industrial" feel.

**Inter** provides a highly legible, neutral balance for body copy, ensuring that even in stressful situations, information is easy to consume.

**JetBrains Mono** is used for labels, technical data (like VIN numbers or GPS coordinates), and small captions. This adds a layer of "engineered" precision to the UI.

## Layout & Spacing
The layout follows a **Rigid Grid System** with an emphasis on verticality and diagonal accents.

- **Grid:** Use a 12-column grid for desktop and a 4-column grid for mobile. 
- **The "Speed Line":** Integrate 1px diagonal rules (at 45 degrees) that bridge sections or act as decorative "strikethroughs" behind headline text. These should be colored in `Electric Lime` or `Midnight Forest` at low opacity.
- **Whitespace:** Use aggressive whitespace (96px+ between sections) to create an "Editorial" feel. Content should never feel cramped; spacing should feel intentional and spacious to reduce user anxiety.
- **Breakpoints:** 
  - Mobile: < 768px (16px margins)
  - Tablet: 768px - 1024px (32px margins)
  - Desktop: > 1024px (48px margins, 1280px max content width)

## Elevation & Depth
This design system rejects traditional shadows in favor of **Layered Flat Design** and **Technical Outlines**.

- **Surfaces:** Depth is created through color blocking rather than shadows. Elements are placed on high-contrast backgrounds (e.g., a Midnight Forest card on a Cool Asphalt background).
- **Borders:** Use 1px or 2px solid borders for all containers. Border colors should be `Midnight Forest` (primary) or `Electric Lime` (for active/urgent states).
- **Speed Accents:** Use "offset shadows"—a solid, non-blurred block of color shifted 4px down and 4px right—to give components a heavy, physical presence without using soft glows.

## Shapes
The shape language is defined by **Absolute Sharpness**. 

- **Corners:** All primary containers, buttons, and input fields must have 0px border radius (sharp 90-degree corners). This reinforces the industrial, high-performance nature of the brand.
- **The Exception:** Tags, chips, or small status pills may use a full-round (pill) shape to provide a visual break and signify "contained" pieces of metadata.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid `Midnight Forest` fill, `Electric Lime` text, sharp corners. On hover, the background shifts to `Electric Lime` with `Midnight Forest` text.
- **Secondary/Outline:** 2px solid `Midnight Forest` border, no fill. High-speed hover effect: the button fills instantly with color from left to right.
- **Ghost:** `JetBrains Mono` text with a 45-degree arrow icon.

### Input Fields
- **Styling:** 1px `Midnight Forest` bottom border only (editorial style) or full sharp-cornered box. 
- **Focus State:** Border thickens to 2px and changes to `Electric Lime`.
- **Labels:** Always use `label-sm` (JetBrains Mono) positioned above the input.

### Cards
- Sharp 90-degree corners. 
- 1px `Onyx Technical` border.
- Header sections should often feature a `Midnight Forest` background with white or lime text to create a strong visual anchor.

### Status Chips
- Pill-shaped.
- Use `Electric Lime` for "Dispatched" or "On the Way."
- Use `Cool Asphalt` with `Midnight Forest` text for "Pending."

### Speed Lines
- Subtle 1px diagonal lines used as background separators or to underline active navigation items.
- In photography, use a dark green overlay with high-contrast highlights to maintain a moody, premium aesthetic.
