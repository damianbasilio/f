## Brand & Style

The design system is built on a foundation of **Editorial Modernism**—a blend of high-fashion print aesthetics and structured architectural precision. It targets a discerning clientele who views grooming as a ritual of status and self-respect. The identity is masculine and authoritative, eschewing traditional barbershop tropes in favor of a sophisticated, "Modern Monarchy" aesthetic.

The visual narrative is defined by:
- **Sharpness:** Every edge is intentional, reflecting the precision of a master barber’s blade.
- **Authority:** Bold, high-contrast typography conveys expertise and heritage.
- **Luxury:** A "Rule-Based" layout utilizes thin, structural lines to create a sense of order and exclusivity, reminiscent of a premium broadsheet or luxury lookbook.
- **Atmosphere:** Generous whitespace (negative space) is used to create a "gallery" feel, allowing imagery and typography to breathe.

## Colors

The palette is anchored in a high-contrast, dark-mode-first approach to evoke the atmosphere of a premium grooming lounge.

- **King’s Gold (#C5A059):** A burnished, metallic hue used for accents, hairlines, and primary actions. It represents craftsmanship and the "gold standard."
- **Deep Charcoal (#121212):** The primary canvas. It provides a deep, matte background that feels infinite and grounding.
- **Parchment (#F4F1EA):** Used for primary typography and high-end contrast. It is softer than pure white, providing an editorial, archival quality.
- **Steel Carbon (#2A2A2A):** Used for subtle UI layering and secondary containers to maintain depth without breaking the dark aesthetic.

## Typography

The typography strategy relies on the tension between the high-fashion serif and a clean, technical sans-serif.

- **Headlines:** Use **Bodoni Moda**. Its extreme stroke contrast and vertical stress communicate luxury and editorial authority.
- **Secondary Text/Labels:** Use **Hanken Grotesk**. This should be tracked out (wide spacing) for a modern, breathable feel. Use uppercase for labels to reinforce the "rule-based" structural aesthetic.
- **Watermarks:** Large-scale, low-opacity Bodoni Moda characters should be placed behind content sections to act as a subtle brand motif.

## Layout & Spacing

This design system utilizes a **Fixed Grid** system inspired by newspaper typesetting. 

- **The Grid:** A 12-column grid for desktop with 24px gutters. Elements should be strictly aligned to these columns.
- **Structural Rules:** Use 1px hairlines (King's Gold or Steel Carbon) to separate sections, create "cells" for content, and define the edges of the UI.
- **Whitespace:** Use aggressive vertical padding (80px–120px) between major sections to emphasize the premium nature of the brand. 
- **Mobile Adaption:** On mobile, switch to a 4-column grid. Horizontal rules remain essential for defining content hierarchy where vertical space is constrained.

## Elevation & Depth

To maintain a "flat editorial" look, traditional drop shadows are strictly prohibited. Depth is achieved through:

- **Tonal Layering:** Using Parchment or Deep Charcoal surfaces to distinguish foreground from background.
- **King's Gold Hairlines:** 1px borders are the primary method of defining interactive areas and section breaks.
- **Textural Contrast:** Mixing matte charcoal backgrounds with subtle "paper grain" textures in the Parchment-colored sections.
- **Overlay Panels:** Modal windows or menus should use a 100% opaque Deep Charcoal background with a 1px King's Gold border to signify importance.

## Shapes

The shape language is strictly **Sharp (0px)**. 

- **No Rounded Corners:** To maintain the authoritative and "razor-sharp" brand identity, all buttons, containers, and input fields must have square 90-degree corners.
- **Interactive Elements:** Buttons should be defined by their borders or solid fills rather than corner radii.
- **Images:** Photography should always be cropped into perfect rectangles or squares, following the grid lines.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid King's Gold fill with Deep Charcoal text. All caps, wide letter-spacing.
- **Secondary:** Transparent background, 1px King's Gold border, King's Gold text.
- **Ghost:** Parchment text with a 1px Steel Carbon underline that expands on hover.

### Inputs
- **Field Styling:** 1px Steel Carbon bottom-border only. Label floats above in Hanken Grotesk (uppercase).
- **Focus State:** Border color shifts to King's Gold.

### Cards
- **Service Cards:** Defined by a 1px border. The top-right corner should contain the price in Bodoni Moda.
- **Portfolio Cards:** Full-bleed imagery with a thin gold frame appearing only on hover.

### Lists
- **Service Menus:** Use a "Leader Line" style (Service Name ........ Price) where the dots are replaced by a 1px Steel Carbon hairline.

### Navigation
- **Header:** Minimalist. Logo centered. Links use `label-md` styling with significant horizontal tracking. Underline active states with a King's Gold hairline.
