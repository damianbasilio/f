The Chef's Philosophy

The Vision of
Maurizio Bianchi

Born in the rolling hills of Tuscany, Maurizio Bianchi brought his family's culinary heritage to the Pacific Northwest with a singular mission: to strip away the pretense of fine dining and return to the raw beauty of ingredients.

"Food should tell a story of its origin. From the flour we mill to the sheep's milk we source for our pecorino, every element is a character in our narrative. We don't just cook; we curate moments of Italian soul on the streets."

M. Bianchi
