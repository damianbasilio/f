Our Story

The spirit of "Ole Glory" isn't just a name, it's an ethos of dignity in service.

Founded on 25th Avenue North, Ole Glory was born from a desire to reclaim the slow, deliberate pace of the traditional American barbershop. We believe that grooming is a ritual, not a chore.

Our barbers are craftsmen of the highest order, dedicated to the geometry of hair and the artistry of the blade. We invite you to sit back, enjoy a pour of Nashville’s finest, and experience the standard we've set for the city.
