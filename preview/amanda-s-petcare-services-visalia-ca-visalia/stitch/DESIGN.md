I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Amanda’s Petcare Services** that captures a premium and devoted brand identity for the Visalia community.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Layout**: Moved away from generic templates to create a sophisticated, magazine-style experience with generous whitespace and intentional asymmetry.
*   **Sun-Drenched Palette**: A custom color story of **Deep Forest Green, Warm Terracotta, and Soft Oat Cream**, evoking a sense of high-end care and natural warmth.
*   **Atmospheric Photography**: I've integrated bespoke imagery—from a golden-hour hero shot of Amanda to detailed lifestyle photography of pets—to establish immediate emotional trust.
*   **Sophisticated Hierarchy**: Used elegant serif headings (Playfair Display) paired with clean sans-serif body text to balance professional authority with approachable warmth.
*   **Functional Polish**: Included an "Emergency" contact header, a structured service grid, a "Service Heart" map section for local relevance, and a minimalist contact form.

The site is fully responsive and production-ready with semantic HTML, CSS custom properties for the theme, and smooth interactive elements.
