I’ve designed a bespoke homepage for **Grants Pawtastic Pet Care** that moves away from generic local-business templates in favor of a high-end, editorial aesthetic. 

### Design Rationale:
*   **The New British Classic**: I established a visual identity rooted in heritage and luxury hospitality. The palette uses **Heritage Green** and **Antique Cream** with **Burnt Ochre** accents to evoke a sense of established trust and premium service.
*   **Editorial Layout**: The homepage features an asymmetrical, high-contrast layout. Instead of standard grids, I used **oversized typography**, **architectural whitespace**, and **organic circular masks** to create a sophisticated narrative flow.
*   **Visual Motifs**: I introduced a **thin-rule motif** and vertical line separators to guide the eye through the content, reinforcing the premium brand positioning.
*   **Bespoke Imagery**: Every section uses custom-generated, editorial-quality photography—from the library-inspired hero shot of a Golden Retriever to the minimalist grooming detail—to ensure the site feels unique and high-end.

This design transforms the business's online presence from a simple Facebook page into a memorable, premium destination.
