Meet Brandy

"Beautiful Paws" is not just a business name; it's a philosophy of care that I've cultivated over 16 years in the industry.

I believe that grooming is an essential part of an animal's well-being, not just their appearance. My approach is centered on building trust with each pet, ensuring they feel safe and loved throughout their stay with me.

Having served the Hillsboro and Portland Metro community for over a decade, I've had the pleasure of watching many of my "clients" grow from playful puppies and kittens into happy seniors. Every pet that walks through my door is treated with the same compassion and professional expertise I would give my own.
