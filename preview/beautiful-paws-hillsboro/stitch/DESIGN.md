## Brand & Style
The brand personality is rooted in "Compassionate Expertise." It targets discerning pet owners who view their animals as family members and seek a stress-free, premium grooming experience. The UI must evoke a sense of quietude, cleanliness, and clinical proficiency softened by maternal warmth.

The design style is **Modern Organic**. It utilizes generous whitespace to mimic the airy atmosphere of a high-end pet spa. While the layout remains professional and structured (reflecting 16 years of industry experience), the visual treatment uses soft transitions and natural textures to remain approachable. It avoids the cluttered, hyper-saturated look of discount pet retail in favor of a curated, editorial aesthetic.

## Colors
The palette is inspired by natural landscapes and professional wellness environments.
- **Primary (Deep Teal):** Used for primary actions, navigation links, and authoritative headers. It represents the "professional" pillar of the brand.
- **Secondary (Sage Green):** Used for accents, success states, and decorative elements. This color promotes a "calm" atmosphere.
- **Tertiary (Warm Walnut):** Reserved for grounded elements, high-end accents, and icon details. It adds an earthy, tactile warmth to the digital experience.
- **Background (Cream Off-White):** A soft, eye-straining-reducing base that feels more premium and "spa-like" than pure white.
- **Text:** Deep charcoal (nearly black) for readability, with primary teal used for subtle semantic differentiation.

## Typography
The typography pairing balances tradition with modernity. **Source Serif 4** provides an authoritative, bookish quality that underscores the 16 years of experience and the "Professional" aspect of the brand. It should be used for all major headings and editorial moments.

**Plus Jakarta Sans** is used for all functional text, body copy, and UI labels. Its slightly rounded terminals and open apertures feel friendly and welcoming, ensuring that the "Caring" side of the brand is felt even in technical details like price lists or booking forms. Use generous line-heights to maintain the "spa" feeling of breathing room.

## Layout & Spacing
This design system utilizes a **Fluid Grid** with fixed maximum containers. 
- **Desktop:** 12-column grid with 24px gutters. Use wide 64px outside margins to frame the content like a premium magazine.
- **Tablet:** 8-column grid with 24px gutters and 32px margins.
- **Mobile:** 4-column grid with 16px gutters and 16px margins.

Spacing follows an 8px rhythmic scale. To achieve the "spa-like" feel, prioritize the `lg` (48px) and `xl` (80px) tokens for vertical section padding. Information density should be kept low to avoid overwhelming the user.

## Elevation & Depth
Depth is conveyed through **Tonal Layers** and **Ambient Shadows**. 
- Surfaces should not "float" aggressively. Instead, use soft, low-opacity shadows (Color: Tertiary Brown at 8% opacity, Blur: 20px) to lift cards slightly off the neutral background.
- Background containers for secondary information should use a subtle tint of Sage Green rather than a shadow to indicate a different functional zone.
- Use backdrop blurs (12px) on navigation headers to maintain a sense of translucency and "glass-like" cleanliness as the user scrolls.

## Shapes
The shape language is consistently **Rounded**. Hard corners are non-existent in this design system to reflect the gentle nature of pet care. 
- Standard components (Buttons, Inputs) use a `0.5rem` radius.
- Large containers (Service Cards, Image Wrappers) use `rounded-lg` (1rem) or `rounded-xl` (1.5rem) to create a soft, "pillowy" appearance.
- Icons should always use a rounded cap and join style to match the UI's softness.

## Components
- **Buttons:** Primary buttons are solid Teal with white text, featuring 0.5rem rounded corners. Secondary buttons use a Sage Green stroke with Teal text.
- **Cards:** Cards use the off-white background with a 1px Sage Green border or a very soft brown-tinted shadow. Padding inside cards should be generous (24px+).
- **Input Fields:** Use a light Sage-tinted background with a bottom-only border or a full 1px border in a muted tone. Focus states transition the border to Primary Teal.
- **Chips/Badges:** Used for pet types (Dog/Cat) or service categories. These should be fully pill-shaped with a light Sage background and dark Teal text.
- **Lists:** Service menus should use elegant Serif headings for the service name and Sans-serif for the description, separated by wide vertical spacing rather than harsh lines.
- **Specialty Component - "Trust Badge":** A specific circular or badge-shaped component highlighting "16 Years Experience" or "Hillsboro, OR Local," using the Tertiary Wood Brown and Serif typography to act as a seal of quality.
