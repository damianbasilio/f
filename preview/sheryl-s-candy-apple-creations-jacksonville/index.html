The Journey

Sheryl's Legacy

"What started in a small home kitchen in Jacksonville has blossomed into a lifelong pursuit of the perfect confection."

Sheryl's passion for candy apples began with a simple family recipe and a commitment to quality. Today, that same dedication lives in every apple we dip. We source the crispest apples from sustainable orchards and use only pure cane sugar and organic dairy for our legendary caramel.

As a Jacksonville staple, we take pride in being part of your celebrations, from autumn weddings to everyday indulgences.
