I've designed a bespoke, industrial-editorial homepage for **Roadside Towing**. 

### Design Rationale
*   **Visual Identity**: I moved away from common service-business templates in favor of a "Technical Blueprint" aesthetic. This uses a high-contrast **Asphalt Black** and **Caution Amber** palette to immediately signal safety, authority, and industrial-grade reliability.
*   **Typography**: I utilized bold, oversized headings to create a sense of scale and strength, reflecting the heavy-duty nature of the business.
*   **Layout**: The design avoids predictable card grids, instead using asymmetric sections, thin technical rules, and generous whitespace to create a premium, editorial feel that stands out in the local Lawton market.
*   **Memorable Motif**: I've integrated macro industrial textures (cables, steel hooks) alongside cinematic photography of their fleet to ground the high-end design in real-world grit.

### Features
*   **High-Visibility Contact**: The primary emergency line is persistent and clearly highlighted in safety amber.
*   **Technical Services Grid**: Services are presented as "System Capabilities" to emphasize precision and professional expertise.
*   **Production-Ready**: The site is fully responsive, including a mobile navigation system and a dedicated map slot for the Lawton headquarters.
