I have designed and built a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Tennessee Tanning**. This is not a template; it is a unique digital identity crafted to elevate the brand's local presence in Kingsport with a premium, luxury aesthetic.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Layout:** I moved away from standard card grids, opting for an asymmetric, high-contrast layout that uses generous whitespace and sophisticated typographic hierarchy to create a "Legacy of Light" feel.
*   **Sun-Drenched Palette:** The visual identity is anchored in a "Deep Obsidian and Burnt Copper" color story, accented with gold line motifs that reflect the radiance of the services offered.
*   **Cinematic Hero:** The entrance features a high-end, artistic hero image that immediately signals professional quality and spa-inspired luxury.
*   **Bespoke Sections:**
    *   **Curated Services:** Styled like a high-end spa menu rather than a generic service list.
    *   **The Legacy of Glow:** A storytelling section that emphasizes the brand's 20+ year history in Kingsport.
    *   **Premium Contact & Visit:** A clean, minimal approach to navigation and booking, including the requested interactive map slot and a sophisticated consultation form.

The code is fully production-ready, semantic, and responsive, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.
