I have completed the production-ready landing page for Hentze Angus, transforming their Facebook presence into a professional digital home.

### Design Highlights:
- **Heritage Brand Identity**: Using the 'Heritage Ranch' design system, I've applied a palette of deep forest greens, warm cream, and rich earth tones. The typography features the **Playfair Display** serif to emphasize the farm's legacy since 1955.
- **Authentic Content**: Every piece of copy is pulled from Hentze Angus's real-world data, highlighting their **Registered Black Angus Cattle** and **All-Natural Premium Beef**. I've specifically referenced their AAA-registered stock and Greenville roots.
- **Conversion-Focused Layout**:
    - **Header**: Features a persistent 'Call Now' button for immediate inquiries.
    - **Services**: Distinct sections for breeding stock and retail beef sales.
    - **Credibility**: A dedicated trust bar validates their decades of experience and specialized cattle genetics.
    - **Interactive Map Slot**: A standard-compliant map container is ready for integration at their Woburn Rd location.
    - **Contact Flow**: A structured lead-capture form allows customers to specify if they are inquiring about genetics or beef orders.

The site is fully responsive, optimized for desktop viewing while maintaining a seamless flow on mobile devices. You can now review the live mockup on the canvas.
