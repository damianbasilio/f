Our Story

A Legacy of Quality in Every Acre

For nearly seven decades, Hentze Angus has been a pillar of the Greenville agricultural community. What started as a small family passion in 1955 has evolved into a premier destination for Registered Black Angus genetics and all-natural beef.

Our commitment goes beyond the herd; it extends to the soil beneath our feet. We practice holistic land stewardship, ensuring that every acre of our ranch is managed to promote ecological health and sustainable production for generations to come.

When you choose Hentze Angus, you're not just buying a product, you're becoming part of a legacy built on integrity, hard work, and an uncompromising pursuit of quality.
