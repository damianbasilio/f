THE SEVENTH HOUSE ARTISANS

BEYOND
THE CHAIR.

Located in the heart of Portsmouth's historic district, Seventh House is more than a salon—it is a sanctuary for hair artistry. Our studio was founded on the belief that beauty should be as enduring as the architecture surrounding us.

Our team of master stylists continuously trains in London and New York to bring world-class techniques to our coastal community. We specialize in lived-in color and architectural cuts that grow out beautifully, honoring the natural movement of your hair.
