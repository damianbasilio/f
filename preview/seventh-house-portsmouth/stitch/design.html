Located at 40 Deer Street, Seventh House occupies a space where the industrial heritage of Portsmouth meets modern luxury. Our interior preserves the soul of the original architecture—exposed brick and timber—while introducing contemporary sharp lines and metallic accents.

It is a place designed for focused transformation. Every chair is a station for the alchemist's craft, situated under the warm glow of artisanal lighting.
