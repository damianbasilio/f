I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Seventh House** that captures the essence of "Modern Heritage"—blending the historic charm of Portsmouth with high-fashion salon expertise.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Layout**: I moved away from standard business templates, opting for an asymmetrical, high-contrast design that uses generous whitespace and bold typography to create a premium feel.
*   **Modern Heritage Palette**: The "Midnight Ink" and "Parchment" color story, accented with "Burnished Copper," establishes an immediate sense of sophistication and local roots.
*   **Signature Visual Motifs**: Thin rule lines and overlapping elements provide a "hair-like" delicacy and modern architectural structure throughout the page.
*   **Bespoke Sections**: 
    *   The **Hero** features a high-impact interior view of the Deer Street location.
    *   **Services** are presented in an intentional, non-grid list that emphasizes craftsmanship over volume.
    *   The **About** section integrates high-fashion portraiture to highlight the salon's editorial focus.
*   **Production-Ready**: The screen includes semantic HTML, responsive CSS, and the specific interactive requirements like the map slot and form validation UI.
