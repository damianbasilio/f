A Sanctuary on
Deer Street

Seventh House is more than a salon; it is a meticulously designed environment for transformation. Located in the historic Deer Street neighborhood, our space reflects the balance of Portsmouth’s rugged coastal beauty and the refined precision of haute couture.

Every visit is an invitation to pause, recalibrate, and emerge redefined. We prioritize a quiet, one-on-one boutique experience where your vision is the focus.
