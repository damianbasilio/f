The Story

A Letter to Fellow Pet Owners

Homebound was born from a simple realization: that for our pets, there truly is no place like home. I founded this practice in Dayton to provide the kind of care I would want for my own family—diligent, compassionate, and deeply rooted in the sanctuary of the house.

We aren't just pet walkers; we are curators of your pet's comfort and your home's security. Our team is trained to notice the small details—a slightly sluggish behavior, a half-empty water bowl, or an uncollected parcel—that others might overlook.

Thank you for trusting us with your most precious housemates.

Sarah Jennings Founder & Lead Caretaker
