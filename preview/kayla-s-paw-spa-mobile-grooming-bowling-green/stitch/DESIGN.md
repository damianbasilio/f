I have completed the bespoke landing page for **Kayla’s Paw SPA Mobile Grooming**. 

### Design Highlights:
- **Editorial Aesthetic:** The layout moves away from traditional small-business templates in favor of a high-end, editorial feel. I’ve used an asymmetrical grid, generous whitespace, and overlapping elements to create a premium first impression.
- **Custom Visual Identity:** Built around a 'Eucalyptus Mint' palette (deep forest greens and soft sage), the design uses sophisticated serif typography (**Playfair Display**) to signal luxury and care.
- **Memorable Visuals:** 
    - The hero section features an elegant Golden Retriever in a minimalist setting, setting a tone of "The Art of the Groom."
    - A secondary "Signature Standard" section uses a close-up of gentle brushing to emphasize the spa's commitment to detail and stress-free care.
- **Memorable Interaction:** Services are presented as a clean, list-based layout with subtle hover transitions, reinforcing the boutique nature of the business.
- **Production-Ready:** The screen includes a functional mobile menu, smooth scrolling, and the specific map slot requirement for Bowling Green.

The design is now live on the canvas for your review.
