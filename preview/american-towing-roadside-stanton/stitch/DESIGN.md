I have designed a bespoke, industrial-premium landing page for **American Towing & Roadside**. Moving away from generic local-business templates, I’ve established a high-fidelity visual identity that reflects the rugged reliability of heavy-duty recovery in West Texas.

### Key Design Decisions:
- **Visual Identity:** I utilized a high-contrast palette of **Asphalt Black** and **Warning Amber**, paired with bold, technical typography to create an "Industrial Premium" aesthetic. 
- **Editorial Layout:** Instead of standard card grids, I used a **Service Blueprint** layout and overlapping visual elements to give the site a custom, high-end feel.
- **Cinematic Assets:** I generated custom, professional-grade imagery, including a cinematic hero image of a heavy-duty rotator at dusk and detailed mechanical textures to emphasize technical expertise.
- **Memorable Motif:** Subtle "caution" accents and grid textures are used throughout the site to reinforce the industrial brand without feeling cluttered.
- **Functional Components:** The design includes a technical sticky header for rapid contact, a dedicated visit section with your exact address in Stanton, and a production-ready contact form.

The site is fully responsive, uses semantic HTML, and is engineered for a premium first impression.
