I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade digital home for **Gabby Nails & Spa Studio**. 

This isn't a template; it's a curated experience that elevates the brand from a local Facebook page to a high-end luxury destination in Atlantic City.

### Design Decisions:
- **Visual Identity:** I established a "Coastal Refinement" palette—midnight teals, warm sandstones, and pearl blushes—that feels both premium and rooted in its Atlantic City location.
- **Editorial Typography:** High-contrast serif headings paired with clean, wide-tracked sans-serif UI elements create a sophisticated, fashion-forward feel.
- **Asymmetric Layouts:** To break away from the generic "local business" look, I used an asymmetric grid for services and testimonials, giving the site a modern, bespoke architecture.
- **Macro Imagery:** The use of high-end editorial photography (interior and macro nail art) creates an immediate sense of quality and atmosphere.

The code is fully production-ready, featuring smooth scrolling, a responsive desktop-first layout, and the requested interactive map slot.
