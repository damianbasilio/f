Our Philosophy

Gabby Studio was born from a desire to strip away the noise of the traditional salon. At 4213 Ventnor Avenue, we prioritize intimate, one-on-one appointments where your technician is an artist and your time is sacred.

THE LOCATION

Located in the heart of Atlantic City's refined neighborhood, providing a quiet alternative to the bustling boardwalk.

THE ARTISTRY

We specialize in minimalist designs that emphasize the natural beauty of the nail, utilizing only the highest grade, toxin-free polishes.

THE EXPERIENCE

Complementary coastal-inspired refreshments and a curated ambient soundscape accompany every session.
