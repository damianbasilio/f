I have completed the bespoke homepage for **Paul's Towing & Recovery**. 

### Design Rationale:
- **Industrial Visual Identity**: I moved away from generic templates to create a "Heavy Duty" editorial feel. This is achieved through a high-contrast dark theme, bold industrial typography (Archivo Narrow), and the use of heavy 4px borders and hazard motifs.
- **Memorable Motif**: The design incorporates a repeating diagonal hazard pattern and thick structural lines that evoke the feeling of heavy machinery and roadside safety.
- **Editorial Layout**: The hero section features custom cinematic photography of the North Dakota landscape, paired with massive, aggressive headlines to establish Paul's as the "Heaviest Hitter" in Minot.
- **Functionality**: The site includes a dedicated "Request Dispatch" flow, a clean services grid, and the required industrial map slot for the Minot headquarters.

### Key Components Integrated:
- **Custom Brand Mark**: A new industrial logo generated specifically for this project.
- **Cinematic Assets**: AI-generated photography of a heavy-duty wrecker at dusk to set a moody, professional tone.
- **Production-Ready Code**: Full semantic HTML with responsive CSS and functional navigation.

The site is now ready for review on the canvas.
