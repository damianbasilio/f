THE PAUL IN PAUL'S TOWING

Family-owned and operator-led, Paul's Towing & Recovery was built from the ground up in the heart of Minot. We didn't start with a fleet; we started with a single truck and a commitment to never leave a driver stranded.

Today, we are the first call for heavy recovery across North Dakota. Our team is trained for the harshest conditions—from blizzard-buried semis to complex multi-vehicle wrecks. We bring the grit, the gear, and the expert knowledge required to handle the heaviest hitters on the road.
