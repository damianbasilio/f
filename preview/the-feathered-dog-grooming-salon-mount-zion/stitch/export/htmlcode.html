Behind the Blade

Founded by Rachel Austin, The Feathered Dog is the culmination of years spent mastering the intersection of pet psychology and artisanal grooming techniques.

Rachel believes that grooming should never be a conveyor-belt service. Instead, she approaches every companion with the patience of a specialist and the eye of an artist, ensuring that your pet leaves looking their absolute best and feeling deeply refreshed.
