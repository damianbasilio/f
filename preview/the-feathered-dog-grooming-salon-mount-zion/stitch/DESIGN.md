I've completed the bespoke homepage for **The Feathered Dog Grooming Salon**. 

As an award-winning designer, I moved away from generic pet-shop templates to create an **editorial, high-end experience** that reflects the "artisanal ritual" of the groom.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity**: Established a sophisticated "Feathered Narrative" design system using a **Deep Forest Green (#1B3022)** and **Soft Cream (#F9F7F2)** palette with **Playfair Display** for high-contrast, premium typography.
*   **Organic Motif**: Replaced standard rectangular grids with subtle, organic shapes and fine-line borders to evoke the delicacy of a feather and the flow of professional grooming.
*   **Custom Imagery**: Generated high-fidelity assets, including a custom single-line logo (The Feathered Dog Logo) and editorial photography of the salon environment (A high-end, editorial-style photograph of a well-groomed, happy dog (like a Golden Retriever or Poodle) in a bright, modern salon with natural wood accents and soft lighting. Focus on the texture of the clean fur. Professional, warm, and inviting. Deep depth of field.) and tools (Macro shot of grooming tools: high-quality scissors and a wooden brush resting on a soft cream linen surface. Soft, natural lighting. Aesthetic and professional.).
*   **Strategic Layout**: 
    *   The **Hero** section focuses on "The Art of the Groom," establishing an immediate premium tone.
    *   The **Services** section uses "intentional density" to present offerings like *Bespoke Styling* and *The Spa Retreat* as curated wellness experiences rather than just tasks.
    *   The **Visit** section includes the requested map placeholder and clear location details for the Mount Zion community.

The frontend is production-ready, featuring a mobile-responsive navigation, smooth scrolling, and a refined appointment request form.
