OUR PHILOSOPHY

"We don't just cut hair; we curate the confidence of the men who build this city."

Clipper City was founded on the belief that a barbershop should be a sanctuary of craft. Located in the heart of Yakima, we honor the timeless techniques of the masters while embracing the sharp aesthetics of today.

Every tool, every chair, and every stroke of the blade is chosen with intention. This is the Yakima standard of excellence.
