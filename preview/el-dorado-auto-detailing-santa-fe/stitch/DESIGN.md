I have completed the bespoke editorial homepage for **El Dorado Auto Detailing**. 

This design avoids the common "local business" template look by leaning into a high-end, editorial aesthetic I've called **'Desert Luxury'**.

### Design Decisions & Rationale:
*   **Visual Identity**: I've moved away from standard blue/red automotive palettes in favor of **Obsidian and Gilded Gold**. This reflects the premium nature of detailing high-end luxury vehicles and aligns with the sophisticated Santa Fe market.
*   **Typography**: I used **Bodoni Moda**, a classic high-contrast serif, for large-scale headlines. This creates an immediate "magazine" or "editorial" feel, signaling authority and precision.
*   **Asymmetric Layout**: Instead of a standard vertical stack of boxes, I implemented an asymmetrical grid. This creates visual interest and leads the eye through the service offerings (Ceramic Coating, Interior Restoration) more dynamically.
*   **The "Artisan" Focus**: I positioned Jaime not just as a business owner, but as an **Artisan**. Highlighting his 15+ years of experience with custom photography of him at work builds immediate trust and sets a personal tone that Facebook-only businesses often lack.
*   **Technical Excellence**: The site is fully responsive, features smooth scrolling for a premium feel, and includes a production-ready contact form with built-in validation UI.
