The Atelier

A Discipline
of Precision.

Located in the historic heart of Superior, WI, Rascal's was founded on the belief that grooming is an essential pillar of health and dignity for our companions.

Our studio is designed to be a calm harbor. We shun the frenetic pace of volume-based salons in favor of a one-on-one, bespoke approach where each pet is treated as a singular masterpiece.

Every stroke of the brush and every cut of the shear is executed with the discipline of a master craftsman, ensuring your pet leaves not just clean, but transformed.
