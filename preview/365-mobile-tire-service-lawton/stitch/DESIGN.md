I have designed a bespoke, premium homepage for **365 Mobile Tire Service** that establishes a bold "High-Contrast Industrial" identity. 

### Key Design Decisions:
- **Visual Identity:** I moved away from generic templates to create an editorial layout using a palette of **Deep Asphalt** and **Safety Orange**. This high-contrast pairing conveys both the rugged nature of the work and the urgency of a 24/7 service.
- **Memorable Motif:** A subtle "tread-pattern" motif is integrated throughout the site via background textures and industrial rule lines, reinforcing the brand's core business.
- **Editorial Typography:** I used a bold, condensed headline font to mimic industrial signage, paired with generous whitespace to elevate the brand from a local utility to a premium service provider.
- **Asymmetric Layouts:** The services and "About" sections use non-standard grid placements to create a modern, high-end feel that stands out from typical local business mockups.
- **Contextual Imagery:** 
    - A macro shot of tire tread (Close-up, high-detail macro shot of a rugged tire tread on a wet asphalt road at night, illuminated by orange streetlights. The aesthetic is industrial, premium, and moody. Deep shadows and sharp highlights on the rubber texture. 16:9 aspect ratio.) anchors the hero section for immediate impact.
    - Lifestyle photography (A professional mobile tire service van parked on a modern suburban street during the golden hour. The van is clean, with minimalist white and orange branding. A technician is seen from the side (no face) working with professional tools. High-end lifestyle photography, shallow depth of field. 3:2 aspect ratio.) highlights the human element and professional standard of service.
    - Custom-styled icons (A collection of three premium tire icons (all-season, performance, heavy-duty) rendered in a minimalist, thick-stroke orange line art style on a dark asphalt-textured background. 4:3 aspect ratio.) categorize the service offerings with visual consistency.

The site includes all required sections: a high-contrast header, credibility ticker, service offerings, about section, the map placeholder for Lawton, a functional service request form, and a modular footer.
