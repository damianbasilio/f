The Attitude Behind the Tail

Born in the heart of Creve Coeur, Attitudes With Tails was founded on a simple belief: every dog has a "red carpet" moment waiting to be unlocked. We moved away from the cold, clinical feel of traditional grooming to create an editorial salon experience.

Our stylists are artists who understand the nuances of personality. Whether your companion is a stoic senior or a high-energy pup, we tailor our approach to their emotional and physical needs, ensuring they leave not just looking better, but feeling empowered.
