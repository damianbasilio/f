I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Attitudes With Tails**. 

To move away from standard local business templates, I established a "Bold Boutique" identity that uses:
- **High-Contrast Asymmetry:** A non-traditional layout with organic shapes and overlapping elements that feel custom-crafted rather than assembled.
- **Editorial Typography:** A sophisticated Playfair Display serif paired with generous whitespace to convey a premium, high-fashion grooming experience.
- **Visual Motif:** Repeating 3px "frame" borders and bold color blocking in "Tailored Teal" and "Sunset Fur" coral to create a memorable brand rhythm.
- **High-Impact Imagery:** Custom-generated photography that positions the pets as style icons, matching the salon's spirited name.

The site is fully responsive, including the required map slot and a cohesive navigation/footer system.
