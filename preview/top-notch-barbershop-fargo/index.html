Our Story

Hardworking Heritage

Top Notch isn't just a barbershop; it's an extension of Fargo's industrial legacy. Founded on the principle that precision grooming should be accessible to those who build this city, we've made our home at 1015 Main Ave.

We respect the ritual of the morning shave and the power of a clean taper. Our space is built with the same grit and steel that defines North Dakota, refined for the modern professional.
