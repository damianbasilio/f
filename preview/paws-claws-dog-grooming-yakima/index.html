OUR PHILOSOPHY

Care over Speed. Precision over Volume.

At Paws & Claws, we believe that the ticking of a clock should never dictate the quality of a groom. We've intentionally moved away from the high-volume 'conveyor belt' model found in traditional pet salons.

Our atelier is a sanctuary for the discerning pet owner. We provide a calm, scent-neutral environment where our master stylists take the time required to understand the unique coat texture and temperament of every guest.
