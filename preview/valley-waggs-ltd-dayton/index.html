Our Philosophy

Elevated Pet Care for Discerning Owners

Founded on the belief that pets are family, Valley Waggs was established to fill the gap between industrial boarding and the boutique home experience. Our facility in Dayton is a haven of calm, designed with pet psychology at the forefront.

We don't just "watch" your pets. We engage them. From individual play schedules to customized nutritional planning, every aspect of our care is bespoke. Our team consists of certified professionals who share a deep reverence for the canine-human bond.
