I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for Valley Waggs Ltd., moving away from standard templates to create a truly unique digital presence.

### Design Highlights:
- **Bespoke Visual Identity**: I established a "Bold Single-Column Stack" layout that uses full-bleed color bands to create a strong vertical rhythm.
- **Editorial Typography**: A sophisticated pairing of **Playfair Display** for headlines and **Inter** for body text provides a premium, high-end feel.
- **Custom Assets**: I generated a high-fidelity hero image of a well-groomed Golden Retriever and a close-up grooming shot to emphasize the luxury nature of the business.
- **Refined Palette**: A nature-inspired trio of **Forest Green (#1B4332)**, **Terracotta (#D66853)**, and **Cream (#F8F9FA)** reflects both the "Valley" brand name and the warmth of pet care.
- **Intentional Structure**: The page flows from a high-impact hero to a credibility bar, followed by dense, information-rich service blocks and a story-driven "About" section.
- **Technical Excellence**: The site includes a custom contact form, an interactive-ready map slot, and is fully responsive for desktop.
