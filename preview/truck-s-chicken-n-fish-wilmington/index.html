THE SOUL OF RED CROSS STREET

Guarded Legacy.
Heavily Seasoned.

"You don't just eat at Truck's. You participate in a century of Wilmington survival and celebration."

Truck's Chicken 'n Fish isn't interested in the trends of modern dining. We serve the same deep-seasoned crunch that powered the shipyard workers and neighborhood icons for decades. Our secret isn't just the flour, it's the heat, the iron, and the history.

Every platter served at 604 Red Cross is a tribute to the "Premium Grit" of our city. We respect the bird, we respect the fire, and most importantly, we respect the people who walk through our doors.
