I have completed the bespoke homepage for **Big A BBQ & Grill**. 

### Design Strategy
To ensure this design stands apart from generic local-business mockups, I've implemented a **"Smoky Industrial"** aesthetic that leverages the following unique elements:

*   **Editorial Layout:** I avoided standard symmetric grids in favor of an **offset, asymmetrical composition**. This creates a more dynamic, high-end "magazine" feel that communicates a premium brand.
*   **Industrial Motif:** The design uses a consistent visual language of **heavy 2px borders** and **sharp, geometric containers**. This mimics the rugged steel of a professional smoker.
*   **Immersive Atmosphere:** A subtle **grain texture** was applied across the surfaces to evoke the texture of smoke and ash, paired with a high-contrast palette of **Charcoal, Burnt Orange, and Cream**.
*   **Strong Typography:** I used a mix of bold, condensed headlines and a refined serif for body copy, establishing a clear hierarchy that guides the user through the brand's story.

### Technical Features
*   **Full Semantic HTML:** Clean, production-ready structure.
*   **Custom Map Slot:** The requested empty map container is integrated within a dedicated "Visit" section.
*   **Responsive Excellence:** The layout adapts fluidly for all screen sizes while maintaining its editorial integrity.
*   **Interactive Forms:** A styled contact section with focus states and a "Send Transmission" button consistent with the industrial theme.
