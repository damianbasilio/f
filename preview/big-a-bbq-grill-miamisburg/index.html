The Big A
Legacy

"If it didn't touch wood and fire for at least twelve hours, we don't serve it. That's the promise."

Founded in Miamisburg, Big A BBQ was born from a garage project and a obsession with the perfect bark. Today, we're still small-batch, still local, and still waking up at 4 AM to tend the coals. We believe BBQ is a craft that can't be rushed, only respected.
