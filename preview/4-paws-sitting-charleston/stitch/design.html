Our Philosophy

Personalized, Boutique-Level Care.

Founded in the heart of Charleston, 4 Paws Sitting was born from a simple belief: every pet deserves the same level of care and attention they receive from their own family.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Our boutique approach means we take the time to learn your pet's favorite hiding spots, their specific "walking pace," and the exact way they like their ears scratched.

When you choose us, you're not just hiring a sitter; you're gaining a dedicated partner in your pet's happiness and well-being.
