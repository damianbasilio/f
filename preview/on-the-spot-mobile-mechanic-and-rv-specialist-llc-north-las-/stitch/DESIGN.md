I have built the professional homepage for **On the Spot Mobile Mechanic and RV Specialist, LLC**. 

This design was created specifically using the content and tone from their Facebook presence to ensure a tailor-made feel rather than a generic template.

### Key Design Decisions:
- **High-Impact Hero:** I used a custom-generated image of an RV being serviced in a Las Vegas setting to immediately establish the specialized nature of the business and its local context.
- **Trust-First Layout:** Given the trade industry, I prioritized the phone number and "Emergency Call" buttons, ensuring they are always accessible.
- **Brand Consistency:** I created a custom logo and applied a "Pro-Service Industrial" design system featuring deep navy blues and caution yellows to evoke professionalism and reliability.
- **Content Accuracy:** Every piece of information, from the address in North Las Vegas to the specialized services for travel trailers, was pulled directly from the provided source.
- **Interactive Elements:** The site includes a semantic structure with smooth scrolling, a functional map slot, and a validation-ready contact form.
