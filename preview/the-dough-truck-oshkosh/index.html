Our Story:
The Science of Savor.

Founded on the belief that bread is the most complex simple thing in the world. Our journey began in a small garage with a 100-year-old starter and a relentless obsession with hydration levels.

Today, The Dough Truck serves as a mobile beacon for those who value the "Slow Food" movement. We source exclusively from Midwest grain farmers who practice regenerative agriculture, ensuring every bite supports the earth it came from.
