I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Whisker Hollow Pet Sitting** that moves far beyond the standard local-business template.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Modern Forest Aesthetic**: I established a unique visual identity using a palette of **Deep Evergreen**, **Soft Moss**, and **Terracotta**, creating a mood that feels both professional and artisanal.
*   **Editorial Typography**: Leveraging **Playfair Display** for high-impact headlines to establish an immediate sense of premium quality and authority.
*   **Non-Standard Layout**: The design uses asymmetric compositions, overlapping elements, and purposeful color blocking to break the "template" feel.
*   **Credibility & Trust**: A dedicated "Whisker Standard" section highlights the business's credentials (Insured, First Aid Certified) in a minimal, sophisticated way.
*   **Bespoke Services Presentation**: Instead of a generic grid, I used a split-column layout with high-quality photo treatments to showcase their specialized care.
*   **Technical Readiness**: The code is fully responsive, features a functional mobile menu, includes smooth-scroll navigation, and contains the required semantic map-slot for the Richmond area.

## Brand & Style
The design system is built upon the **Modern Forest** aesthetic—a fusion of editorial luxury and organic warmth. It aims to evoke a sense of calm, professional reliability, and bespoke care. Unlike traditional high-energy pet services, this system prioritizes an artisanal, "slow-living" feel.

The style leverages **Minimalism** with a **Tactile** edge. It utilizes heavy whitespace to create a sense of breathability and premium positioning, while high-contrast color blocking between deep forest tones and soft neutrals provides a grounded, structural foundation. Organic, "hollow-inspired" curves are subtly integrated to soften the professional edge, reflecting the nurturing nature of the service.

## Colors
The palette is rooted in a natural, sophisticated spectrum that avoids industry clichés.

*   **Deep Evergreen (#0B2D26):** The primary anchor. Used for backgrounds in high-contrast blocks and primary typography to establish authority and depth.
*   **Soft Moss (#DDE6D5):** The secondary foundation. Used for large surface areas and containers to create a soothing, approachable atmosphere.
*   **Terracotta (#D97757):** The functional accent. Reserved for call-to-actions, notifications, and vital highlights, providing a warm, human touch.
*   **Off-White (#F9FBF8):** The base neutral. Used for main canvas backgrounds to maintain a clean, editorial look.

## Typography
The typography follows an editorial hierarchy. **Playfair Display** provides a sophisticated, literary character for headlines, featuring tight letter-spacing in larger formats to enhance the "bespoke" feel. 

**Hanken Grotesk** serves as the functional counterpart; its contemporary, clean proportions ensure high legibility for service details and body copy. For labels and small metadata, use uppercase Hanken Grotesk with increased letter-spacing to maintain clarity against rich color backgrounds.

## Layout & Spacing
This design system rejects standard dense grids in favor of a **Fluid Editorial Layout**. 

*   **Rhythm:** Use a base 8px unit. Section vertical spacing is intentionally generous (120px+) to emphasize the premium nature of the brand.
*   **Grid:** A 12-column system is used for desktop, but elements frequently break the grid or use asymmetrical offsets to mimic a high-end magazine layout.
*   **Margins:** Large outer margins (40px on tablet/desktop) ensure content feels framed rather than crowded.
*   **Mobile:** On mobile devices, gutters reduce to 16px and section gaps scale down to 64px to maintain momentum while preserving the "airy" feel.

## Elevation & Depth
Depth is achieved through **Tonal Layering** and **Organic Overlaps** rather than traditional shadows.

*   **Tiers:** Surfaces are defined by color shifts (e.g., a Soft Moss card sitting on a Deep Evergreen background). 
*   **Hollow Curves:** Use large-radius organic "cut-outs" or container shapes to create a sense of nested layers.
*   **Shadows:** When necessary for interaction (like a hover state on a primary button), use a very subtle, highly-diffused tint of the primary color: `rgba(11, 45, 38, 0.08)` with a 20px blur. 
*   **Outlines:** Use 1px solid borders in the primary color at 10% opacity for subtle definition on light surfaces.

## Shapes
The shape language is characterized by "Hollow Curves." While standard components use a **Rounded** (0.5rem) base, primary containers and hero sections should feature asymmetrical rounding—for example, a top-left and bottom-right corner at `rounded-xl` while others remain `rounded-md`. This creates a custom, organic silhouette reminiscent of natural burrows and comfort.

## Components
*   **Buttons:** Primary buttons are solid Deep Evergreen with white Hanken Grotesk text. Secondary buttons use a Terracotta outline (1.5px) to provide a warm but clear call to action.
*   **Cards:** Use Soft Moss (#DDE6D5) for card backgrounds. Avoid shadows; use a 1px border of #0B2D26 at 5% opacity for definition. Apply asymmetrical corner radii to card sets for an artisanal feel.
*   **Inputs:** Minimalist style. Transparent background with a bottom-only 1.5px border in Deep Evergreen. Focus states transition the border to Terracotta.
*   **Chips/Labels:** Small, pill-shaped elements using the Soft Moss background with Deep Evergreen text for categorization (e.g., "Cat Care," "Overnight").
*   **Lists:** Editorial style with custom "hollow" bullet points (a stroke-only circle in Terracotta) and generous vertical padding between items.
*   **Navigation:** A clean, centered top-bar with generous padding. The active state is indicated by a subtle Terracotta dot beneath the menu item.
