I have designed a bespoke, premium landing page for **Anna's A+ Pet Sitting** that captures the brand's commitment to excellence and its local Richmond charm.

### Design Rationale:
- **Visual Identity**: I moved away from generic pet-care tropes in favor of an **Editorial Warmth** aesthetic. This uses a sophisticated palette of **Forest Green**, **Warm Cream**, and **Burnt Orange**, paired with a high-contrast serif typeface (**Playfair Display**) to evoke trust and premium quality.
- **The "A+" Stamp**: A recurring "A+" stamp motif acts as a visual shorthand for the brand's name and high standard of care, appearing in the hero and credibility sections.
- **Layout & Imagery**: The design features an asymmetric hero layout and high-fidelity, sun-drenched photography to create a memorable, "Architectural Digest" feel for pet care.
- **Local Credibility**: Key trust markers, including Richmond-specific details and "Certified & Insured" badges, are placed prominently to build immediate confidence with local owners.
- **Technical Excellence**: The site is production-ready with semantic HTML, responsive layout for mobile and desktop, and a clean interface for the inquiry form and map panel.
