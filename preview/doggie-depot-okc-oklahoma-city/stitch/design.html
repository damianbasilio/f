BORN IN OKC, BUILT FOR THE BOLD.

Doggie Depot OKC was founded on a singular premise: that our dogs deserve a space as sophisticated and reliable as the city they live in. We rejected the neon plastics and noisy chaos of traditional kennels.

Our facility is an architectural tribute to Oklahoma City's industrial roots, designed from the ground up to minimize stress and maximize engagement. From our medical-grade air filtration to our curated staff of behavioral specialists, every element is a deliberate choice in favor of canine wellness.
