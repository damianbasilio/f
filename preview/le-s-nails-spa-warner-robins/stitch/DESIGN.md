I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Le’s Nails & Spa** that moves far beyond a standard template to create a premium, memorable first impression.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Layout**: Eschewing traditional grids, I used an asymmetric, staggered composition with generous whitespace to evoke the feeling of a high-end fashion magazine.
*   **Petal & Slate Palette**: A refined color story of warm off-white, deep charcoal, and soft rose highlights, inspired by the serene and luxurious atmosphere of the salon.
*   **Distinctive Typography**: High-contrast serif headings (Playfair Display) provide a sense of authority and luxury, paired with wide-tracked sans-serif body copy for clarity and modernism.
*   **Bespoke Assets**: I generated a custom minimalist monogram and high-fidelity editorial photography to ground the brand in a unique visual identity.
*   **Functional Elegance**: The site includes all requested sections—from a detailed service menu to a custom-styled contact form—wrapped in smooth, responsive code.

The code is production-ready, featuring semantic HTML, custom CSS variables for the color system, and smooth-scroll interactions.
