OUR HERITAGE

The Artisanal Approach

Rooted in State College, Pennsylvania, Mallory Devore brings a legacy of textile expertise to every project. We believe that window treatments should be an extension of a home's soul.

Our process is deliberate. From the initial tactile exploration of swatches to the final precision installation, we honor traditional craftsmanship while embracing modern engineering. Each piece is hand-sewn in our studio, ensuring a quality that mass-produced alternatives simply cannot replicate.
