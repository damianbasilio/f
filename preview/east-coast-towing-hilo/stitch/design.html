Big Island Heritage

For three decades, East Coast Towing has been the quiet force behind Hilo's roadside safety. Founded on the principles of reliability and community, we understand that an emergency is more than a mechanical failure—it's a disruption of your life.

From our headquarters at 21 Kalanianaole Street, our dispatchers manage a fleet that traverses the coastal roads and volcanic slopes of East Hawaii daily. We are more than just a towing company; we are your neighbors on the road.
