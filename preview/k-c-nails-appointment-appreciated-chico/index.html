The Narrative

Born in the heart of Chico, K C Nails was founded on the radical idea that nail care should be treated as high art. We've spent a decade refining the "K C Standard", a meticulous protocol that balances health and aesthetic.

Every technician at K C is an artisan. We don't just follow trends; we study anatomy, color theory, and chemical integrity to ensure your results are as durable as they are beautiful.
