I've designed a bespoke, editorial homepage for K C Nails that moves away from generic local business templates to create a truly premium, memorable brand presence.

### Design Highlights:
- **Visual Motif**: I utilized an "Artistry in Motion" theme, pairing high-contrast typography with macro textures of nail polish to evoke a sense of fine art rather than just a utility service.
- **Editorial Layout**: The design features an asymmetric grid and generous whitespace, mirroring the layout of high-end fashion magazines.
- **Custom Palette**: I developed a 'Polished Quartz' scheme—deep espresso, champagne gold, and soft ivory—to communicate a sense of quiet luxury and professional precision.
- **Brand Consistency**: The typography uses a high-contrast serif for an timeless feel, balanced by a modern, airy sans-serif for clarity.

The site includes all requested sections, from the artistic hero and service palette to the sanctuary-inspired about section and the visit/contact area with the integrated map slot.
