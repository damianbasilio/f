Our Legacy

A Jonesboro Original,
Driven by Passion.

Scalawags was born out of a simple realization: our local dogs deserved better than a corporate grooming line. Founded right here in Jonesboro, we set out to create a sanctuary where heritage barbering techniques meet modern pet wellness.

Every clipper stroke, every hand-picked collar in our boutique, and every warm greeting at our Parker Road door is a testament to our love for the canine community. We aren't just groomers; we're caretakers of your dog's comfort and style.

— The Scalawags Team
