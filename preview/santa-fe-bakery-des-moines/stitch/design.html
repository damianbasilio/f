OUR STORY

Blending Heritage Flavors with Iowa Warmth

Santa Fe Bakery began as a small family dream, rooted in the sun-drenched kitchens of New Mexico. We brought our heirloom recipes to Des Moines, finding a perfect harmony between the earthy spices of the Southwest and the rich, golden grains of the Midwest.

Every morning at 3:00 AM, our ovens come to life. We believe in the slow way—where bread isn't just a commodity, but a connection to the land and the people we serve.
