Beauty-Fur was born from a simple belief: our dogs deserve the same level of luxury and care that we afford ourselves.

Founded by expert stylists with over 15 years of experience in high-end canine aesthetics, we focus on the intersection of health and style. Every session is treated as a masterclass in grooming, using only premium, organic products that respect your dog's skin and the environment.

Whether in our boutique Greeley salon or through our mobile spa, we provide a tranquil, one-on-one experience that eliminates the noise and stress of traditional high-volume shops.
