I have designed a bespoke, award-winning-quality homepage for TLC Pet Sitting, moving far beyond a standard template to create a unique visual identity for Trish's business.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity:** Established a custom "Sunset & Sage" palette—deep forest greens, warm terracotta accents, and cream surfaces—to evoke a sense of professional care and local warmth.
*   **Editorial Layout:** Employed an asymmetrical design with high-contrast serif typography and generous whitespace to create a premium, "magazine-style" feel that stands out in the local market.
*   **Bespoke Sections:**
    *   **Hero:** Paired a sun-drenched lifestyle image of a dog in a Syracuse home with a bold, emotive headline.
    *   **Services:** Moved away from card grids in favor of an elegant, numbered list layout that prioritizes readability and sophistication.
    *   **About Section:** Integrated the photo of Trish with a textured terracotta block to create a personal, anchored connection for potential clients.
*   **Production-Ready:** Delivered clean, semantic HTML and CSS with the required functional map slot and form UI, all optimized for both desktop and mobile viewing.
