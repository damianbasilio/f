Our Story

Heritage in every stroke.

For over thirty years, All About The Dog has occupied the same corner on Post Road, serving as a sanctuary for the local canine community. We believe grooming is not a chore, but an act of stewardship.

Our founder established the salon with a simple mandate: treat every animal as a living heirloom. That philosophy continues today through our scrupulous attention to detail and unwavering commitment to the animals of Fairfield.
