I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Breezes Delight**, focusing on a "Coastal Editorial" aesthetic that feels both refreshing and premium.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Classic Centered Layout:** I chose a symmetrical, centered archetype for the hero and key sections to establish a sense of balance, elegance, and reliability.
*   **Visual Identity:** The palette uses "Breeze Blue" and "Sand" neutrals to reflect a sophisticated coastal vibe, paired with deep navy for professional contrast.
*   **Editorial Typography:** A refined serif (Playfair Display) for headlines provides a high-end, classic feel, while a clean sans-serif ensures readability.
*   **Local Charm & Credibility:** I’ve woven in specific details about Jacksonville to ground the brand in its community, using generous whitespace to give the content room to breathe.
*   **Interactive Elements:** The page includes a responsive navigation system, a dedicated "Visit" section with a map placeholder, and a polished contact form designed for high-end inquiries.
