I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Southern Paws** that moves far beyond the standard local-business template.

### Design Rationale:
- **Editorial Composition**: I opted for an asymmetric, split-screen hero layout that pairs high-density typography with large-scale, soulful photography. This creates an immediate sense of prestige and artisanal quality.
- **Visual Motif**: The design uses thin **Burnished Copper rule lines** and copper accents to frame content, serving as a subtle nod to high-end Southern architecture and craftsmanship.
- **Intentional Whitespace**: Instead of filling every corner, I used generous "buttermilk" gutters to let the brand's story breathe, emphasizing the calm, luxury environment they provide for pets.
- **Custom Assets**: I generated a series of bespoke images—an elegant parlor portrait, a detail-focused grooming shot, and a boutique suite lifestyle photo—to ensure the visual narrative is unique to Southern Paws.
- **Typography**: I leaned into a high-contrast serif (Playfair Display) to anchor the "Southern Hospitality" theme, using it for both headlines and a signature testimonial section.

The result is a digital experience that feels as intentional and cared-for as the estate itself.
