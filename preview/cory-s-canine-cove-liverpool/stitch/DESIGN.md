I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Cory's Canine Cove** that departs from generic local-business templates to create a truly premium first impression.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Nautical Editorial Identity:** I established a sophisticated "Cove" aesthetic using a palette of Deep Navy and Warm Sand, accented by a functional "Rescue Orange" for primary calls to action.
*   **Bespoke Layout:** Instead of a standard card grid, I utilized an asymmetrical, multi-layered layout for the services and brand story sections, creating a sense of custom craft and attention to detail.
*   **Intentional Typography:** High-contrast serif headlines (Playfair Display) provide a luxurious, established feel, while wide-spaced sans-serif body text ensures modern readability and an airy, editorial vibe.
*   **Authentic Content:** Every section—from the #credibility bar to the "Cove Story"—uses real business details to build immediate trust with local dog owners.
*   **Production-Ready:** The implementation includes a custom sticky navigation, a functional "Map Slot" for your future integration, and a sleek, validated contact form.
