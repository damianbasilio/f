From Kennel
to Retreat

Founded by Cory Burke, The Cove was born from a simple realization: dogs don't just need a place to stay—they need a place to thrive.

We transitioned from the traditional "kennel" model to a full-service retreat that emphasizes the mental and physical well-being of every guest. Our facility is designed to feel like a high-end coastal lodge, providing comfort for the pups and peace of mind for their humans.
