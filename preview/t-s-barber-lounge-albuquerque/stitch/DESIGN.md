I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **T's Barber Lounge** that moves far beyond the typical local business template. This design establishes a "Gentleman’s Industrial" aesthetic, using a sophisticated **Obsidian and Copper** color palette to reflect the premium, architectural precision of their grooming services.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Hero:** A cinematic, full-bleed hero section using custom-generated imagery that sets a moody, upscale tone immediately.
*   **The Ritual of Services:** Instead of a generic grid, services are presented with distinct typographic hierarchy, emphasizing the craftsmanship behind 'The Cut', 'The Shave', and 'The Ritual'.
*   **Local Credibility:** The "Located in the Heart of ABQ" section grounds the brand in Albuquerque, featuring a clean, minimalist layout with the requested functional map slot.
*   **Custom Brand Mark:** I generated a minimalist copper logo mark that integrates the 'T' with a subtle straight razor silhouette, reinforcing the brand's identity as both modern and traditional.
*   **Memorable Motif:** Generous white space (or "obsidian space" in this dark theme) and oversized, high-contrast serif typography create an editorial feel typically reserved for high-fashion or architecture journals.

The site is fully responsive, production-ready, and optimized for a memorable first impression. You can now explore the design on the canvas.
