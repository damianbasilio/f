I have completed the bespoke homepage for **Hungry Hound LLC**. 

### Design Rationale:
- **Editorial Aesthetic:** To differentiate from typical local business sites, I used a high-contrast serif typeface and a "Prince Street Editorial" design system. This creates a sense of authority and premium quality.
- **"Farm-to-Bowl" Palette:** The color scheme uses deep forest greens and soft ivory backgrounds to evoke a natural, sophisticated feel, accented by terracotta for call-to-action elements.
- **Asymmetric Layout:** I moved away from standard card grids in favor of an asymmetric "Offerings" section. This creates a more dynamic, designed feel that emphasizes the artisanal nature of their services.
- **Local Credibility:** The design prominently features their Prince Street heritage and Clovis roots, grounding the high-end look in local trust.

### Technical Details:
- **Production-Ready Code:** The screen is built with semantic HTML and utility-first CSS, ensuring accessibility and performance.
- **Responsive Design:** The layout is optimized for desktop while maintaining a clean, readable structure that scales elegantly.
- **Functional Components:** Includes a validated contact form UI and a dedicated map slot for the Prince Street flagship location.
