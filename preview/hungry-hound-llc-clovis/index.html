The Hungry Hound Story

Hungry Hound LLC was born from a simple realization: our canine companions deserve the same quality and care we afford ourselves. Established with a nod to Prince Street's heritage, we represent a "Quiet Luxury" for pets.

In the heart of Clovis, NM, we have built more than a boutique, we have built a community. Every recipe, every groom, and every interaction is infused with our commitment to artisanal excellence and local trust.
