I've designed a bespoke, premium homepage for **Dukes Glitzy Pawz**, moving away from standard templates to create an editorial-grade digital presence.

### Design Highlights:
- **Visual Identity:** I established a "Midnight & Gold" palette, pairing deep navy with metallic gold accents to evoke the brand's "Gold Standard" of pet care.
- **Typography:** A high-contrast pairing of *Playfair Display* for headings and *Montserrat* for body copy creates a sophisticated, trustworthy atmosphere.
- **Layout Archetype:** I utilized a classic centered hero section for a grand first impression, followed by a symmetrical, rule-based grid that emphasizes clarity and luxury.
- **Credibility & Services:** The design features custom-styled trust indicators and elegant service cards with thin-rule borders, ensuring the focus remains on the "Elite Grooming Collections."
- **Production-Ready:** The implementation includes semantic HTML, responsive CSS, and the required interactive elements like the map slot and contact inquiry form.

The result is a memorable, high-fidelity experience that positions Dukes Glitzy Pawz as the premier grooming destination in Decatur.
