OUR STORY

Elevating Grooming to
an Art Form

At Dukes Glitzy Pawz, we don’t just groom, we curate an experience. Founded on the belief that our companions deserve the same level of luxury and care as we do, we’ve created a sanctuary in Decatur that prioritizes wellness and aesthetics in equal measure.

Our master groomers are trained in the latest safety techniques and artistic styling, ensuring that every visit is as safe as it is transformative. We use only the finest botanical products, specifically chosen for their restorative properties and gentle touch.
