I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for Woof Pack Daycare and Boarding LLC, moving away from generic local-business templates to create a truly premium first impression.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Layout:** I utilized a high-contrast typographic hierarchy using the *Fraunces* serif for headings and *Inter* for body copy, paired with generous whitespace and organic, asymmetrical framing for images to create a "boutique hospitality" feel.
*   **Custom Visual Identity:** The palette centers on a sophisticated **Forest Green (#1B3022)** and **Sage (#8A9A5B)**, accented by a vibrant **Burnt Orange (#D45B12)** for key actions.
*   **Bespoke Sections:**
    *   **The Hero:** Features a high-impact headline and an organic-shaped image mask that immediately communicates high-end care.
    *   **Trust Stats:** Replaced standard icons with a sophisticated, fine-lined "Pack Stats" bar to build immediate credibility.
    *   **Asymmetrical Services:** Used staggered blocks and unique background treatments for Daycare and Boarding to ensure the layout feels designed, not assembled.
    *   **Visit & Map:** Includes the specific requested map slot in a clean, minimalist layout with prominent location details for Clarksville.
*   **Production-Ready:** The code includes responsive styling for mobile (375px), a custom navigation experience, and tactile interaction states for all buttons and forms.

The result is a unique digital home that reflects the artisanal quality of the service while remaining highly functional for conversion.
