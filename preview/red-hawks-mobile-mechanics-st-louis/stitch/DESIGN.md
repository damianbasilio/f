I've completed the bespoke homepage for **Red Hawks Mobile Mechanics**. 

This isn't a template—it's a high-precision digital environment designed to mirror the mechanical excellence of the business. 

### Design Rationale:
*   **The "Ignition" Split-Hero**: I moved away from traditional centered heroes for a high-contrast split layout. The left side uses massive vertical typography to establish authority, while the right side features a "Mechanical Scan" HUD overlay, making the user feel like they’re looking through a diagnostic lens.
*   **Industrial Precision Aesthetic**: To lean into the "Red Hawks" name and mechanical theme, I’ve used a sharp, 45-degree angled design language and a brutalist color palette (Ignition Red, Oil Slick Black, and Concrete Grey). Every element has 0px border-radius to emphasize rugged durability.
*   **Technical Hierarchy**: By pairing a condensed industrial sans-serif with monospace labels, I’ve given the site an "engineering schematic" feel. This reinforces the brand’s positioning as a premium, technical specialist rather than a generic repair shop.
*   **The "Flight Path" Services**: Instead of a standard card grid, I’ve designed a continuous timeline-style flow that guides the user through the mechanical lifecycle from diagnostic to rescue.

The result is a digital presence that feels like a piece of high-end automotive machinery—bold, precise, and impossible to ignore.
