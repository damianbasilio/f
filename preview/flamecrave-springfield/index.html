Our Heritage

RAW PASSION.
TRUE FIRE.

Founded on the belief that the most profound flavors are born from the simplest elements: wood, smoke, and heat. Our journey started in a small backyard in Springfield, experimenting with white oak and Oregon-grown produce.

"We don't just cook with fire; we listen to it. Every log tells a story of the season, and every spark is an invitation to taste the landscape."

Today, FlameCrave stands as a monument to that obsession. We source 90% of our ingredients within a 50-mile radius, ensuring that the soul of the Willamette Valley is present in every bite.
