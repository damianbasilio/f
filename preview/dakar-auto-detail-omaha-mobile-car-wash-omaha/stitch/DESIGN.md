I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade landing page for **Dakar Auto Detail**, tailored specifically for the Omaha market. This isn't just a site—it's a digital reflection of the meticulous care provided to high-end vehicles.

### Design Decisions:
- **Visual Identity**: I established a "Midnight Grand Prix" theme using a high-contrast dark palette (Asphalt #121417) with Racing Gold (#D4AF37) accents to evoke luxury automotive prestige.
- **Editorial Layout**: The design uses asymmetric grids, large-scale serif typography (Playfair Display), and generous whitespace to distance the brand from generic "mobile wash" competitors.
- **Memorable Motif**: Thin gold rule lines and "speed lines" serve as consistent structural elements, guiding the eye through the service tiers and credibility markers.
- **Omaha Focus**: Every piece of copy is localized to establish trust as Omaha's premier mobile collector care, including a custom-styled map area for service radius visualization.
- **Immersive Imagery**: I generated cinematic visuals focused on textures like fine leather, carbon fiber, and gloss ceramic coatings to communicate quality without words.

The technical foundation is a production-ready, semantic HTML document featuring smooth scroll interactions, a glassmorphism navigation bar, and a validated booking valet.
