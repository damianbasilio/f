I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for Dakar Auto Detail, moving away from standard templates to create a truly premium "Omaha Mobile" identity.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Motif**: I’ve utilized an "Asphalt & Gold" palette, pairing deep charcoal surfaces with metallic harvest gold accents to evoke a sense of high-end automotive luxury.
*   **Typographic Hierarchy**: High-contrast serif headings (Playfair Display) provide a premium, magazine-like feel, while clean sans-serif body text ensures absolute clarity.
*   **Bespoke Layout**: The hero section features overlapping typography on macro automotive photography, and the services section uses a numbered, staggered layout to emphasize the meticulous nature of the work.
*   **Omaha Mobile Focus**: Integrated Omaha-specific copy and professional credibility markers to establish immediate trust within the local enthusiast community.

The code is production-ready, featuring a sticky blur-effect navigation, smooth scroll behavior, and a custom-styled contact form.
