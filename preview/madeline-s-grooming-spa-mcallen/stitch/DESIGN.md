I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Madeline's Grooming Spa**, moving away from standard templates to create a unique "Boutique Minimalism" aesthetic.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Typography:** I used a high-contrast serif for headings to evoke a sense of high-end spa luxury, paired with wide-tracked sans-serif labels for a modern, clean feel.
*   **Asymmetric Layout:** The services and about sections use intentional offsets and varying column widths to create a visual rhythm that feels custom-designed rather than modular.
*   **Warm Boutique Palette:** The color story is grounded in "Parchment" and "Warm Gold," creating a serene and welcoming atmosphere that aligns with the business's high-end grooming philosophy.
*   **Memorable Visual Motifs:** Elegant hairline rules and vertical "Established" labels provide subtle, recurring details that reinforce the premium brand identity.
*   **Production-Ready:** The screen includes a functional mobile navigation, smooth-scroll links, and the requested interactive map container, all built with semantic HTML and tailored CSS.

The layout is fully responsive and optimized to provide a memorable first impression for pet owners in McAllen.

## Brand & Style
The design system for this pet grooming boutique is centered on "Warm Boutique Minimalism." It aims to evoke the serenity of a high-end human spa while maintaining a tangible connection to pet care. The brand personality is expert, pampering, and serene, targeting a discerning clientele who view their pets as family members deserving of world-class service.

The visual style blends **Editorial Minimalism** with **Tactile warmth**. It moves away from the sterile, clinical look of traditional veterinary services in favor of an aesthetic that feels like a luxury lifestyle magazine. Key characteristics include:
- **Generous Whitespace:** Giving content room to breathe to convey a sense of calm and order.
- **Asymmetric Balance:** Using off-center alignments and overlapping elements to create a bespoke, non-templated feel.
- **Thin Lineage:** Utilizing "hairline" rules to separate sections, mimicking the precision of professional grooming tools.

## Colors
The palette is rooted in organic, sophisticated tones that suggest both natural beauty and cleanliness.

- **Golden Retriever Gold (#D4A373):** Used as the signature accent for key calls to action, active states, and brand-defining highlights.
- **Parchment (#FAFAF5):** The primary background color. It provides a softer, more premium alternative to pure white, reducing eye strain and adding "editorial" warmth.
- **Deep Charcoal (#1A1A1A):** Used for all primary typography and structural lines to ensure high legibility and a grounded, authoritative feel.
- **Sage (#A3B18A):** A secondary accent used for success states, secondary buttons, or decorative organic shapes to reinforce the "spa" and "wellness" narrative.

## Typography
The typography strategy relies on the contrast between the authoritative, high-contrast Serif and a clean, wide-tracked Sans-Serif.

- **Headlines:** Set in *Playfair Display*. These should utilize optical sizing where possible. Use "Display" sizes for hero sections with tight kerning to emphasize the elegant stroke contrast.
- **Body & UI:** Set in *DM Sans*. This font was chosen for its geometric clarity and modern feel. Body text should be intentionally wide-tracked (letter-spacing: 0.01em) to enhance readability and the "airy" boutique aesthetic.
- **Labels:** Use uppercase *DM Sans* with generous letter-spacing (0.15em) for small captions, overlines, and navigation items to evoke luxury fashion branding.

## Layout & Spacing
The layout follows a **Fluid Asymmetric Grid**. Rather than standard 3-column cards, use overlapping containers and staggered image placements.

- **The Hairline Motif:** Use 1px "Deep Charcoal" lines with 20% opacity to create vertical and horizontal boundaries. These lines should often extend to the edge of the viewport.
- **Negative Space:** Content blocks should be separated by large vertical gaps (e.g., 120px or 160px on desktop) to maintain the premium spa feel.
- **Breakpoints:**
  - **Desktop (1024px+):** 12-column grid, 64px margins. Elements can span across columns and overlap (e.g., an image in columns 1-7 with text starting in column 6).
  - **Tablet (768px - 1023px):** 8-column grid, 40px margins. Reduce overlapping to maintain touch-target clarity.
  - **Mobile (Up to 767px):** 4-column grid, 20px margins. Stack elements vertically but maintain asymmetric text alignments (e.g., right-aligned subheaders).

## Elevation & Depth
Depth in this design system is achieved through **Tonal Layering** and **Organic Shapes** rather than traditional drop shadows.

- **Low-Contrast Outlines:** Use subtle "Deep Charcoal" hairline borders for interactive elements. Avoid heavy shadows.
- **Organic Containers:** Instead of rectangles, use large, subtle organic "blobs" or curved containers in "Sage" or "Golden Retriever Gold" (at 5-10% opacity) as background layers behind images.
- **Layering:** Images of pets should feel "cut out" or placed inside uniquely curved containers that overlap hairline rules, creating a 3D effect without needing shadows.
- **Glassmorphism:** Use very light backdrop blurs (4px-8px) on navigation bars only, maintaining the "Parchment" tint to ensure the UI feels integrated with the background.

## Shapes
The shape language is a mix of architectural precision and organic softness.

- **Primary Elements:** Buttons and input fields use a standard "Rounded" (0.5rem) corner radius to feel approachable yet professional.
- **Feature Containers:** Images and featured sections should utilize asymmetric rounding—for example, a container where the top-left and bottom-right corners have a 100px radius, while the others remain 8px. This echoes the fluid motion of grooming.
- **Icons:** Use thin-stroke (1px) icons that match the "Deep Charcoal" text color. Avoid filled icons unless in an active state.

## Components
Consistent styling for core interactive elements:

- **Buttons:**
  - **Primary:** "Golden Retriever Gold" background, "Deep Charcoal" text, 0.5rem radius. No shadow.
  - **Secondary:** "Deep Charcoal" 1px hairline border, transparent background, wide-tracked uppercase text.
- **Input Fields:** Bottom-border only (1px charcoal) to mimic a high-end stationery feel. Labels float above in small caps.
- **Cards (Service Blocks):** No background or shadow. Defined by a single vertical hairline on the left and generous padding. The service name is in "Playfair Display," and the price is in "DM Sans" small caps.
- **Chips:** "Sage" background at 15% opacity with "Deep Charcoal" text, fully pill-shaped (rounded-xl) for categories like "Dog Spa" or "Cat Grooming."
- **Interactive Motif:** On hover, primary buttons can subtly shift 4px up and to the right, leaving a faint 1px ghost border behind to reinforce the "asymmetric" theme.
