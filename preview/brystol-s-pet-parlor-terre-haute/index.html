Our Story & Your Family

At Brystol’s Pet Parlor, we believe in a simple truth: dogs are our babies. Whether they’ve been with you for ten years or ten days, we know they mean the world to you.

Our boutique salon was born from a passion for providing Terre Haute pets with a grooming experience that feels more like a spa day than a clinical appointment. We understand that fur babies are often our "first babies," and we take that responsibility to heart every single day.
