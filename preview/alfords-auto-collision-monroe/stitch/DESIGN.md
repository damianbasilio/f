## Brand & Style

The design system is built on the pillars of **Industrial Reliability** and **Local Craftsmanship**. It reflects 25+ years of automotive expertise by blending a rugged, mechanical aesthetic with high-end modern professionalism. The UI should evoke the feeling of a clean, well-organized workshop: precise, dependable, and durable.

The visual style is **Corporate Modern with a Industrial edge**. We utilize structured layouts, purposeful whitespace, and subtle tactile elements to bridge the gap between a high-tech repair facility and a friendly neighborhood business. 

**Target Audience:**
- Local Monroe residents seeking dependable vehicle repair.
- Insurance adjusters requiring clear, professional documentation.
- Performance automotive enthusiasts who value precision and detail.

**Emotional Response:**
- Confidence in technical skill.
- Ease of communication and transparency.
- A sense of local pride and long-standing reputation.

## Colors

The color palette is inspired by heavy-duty automotive materials and professional safety standards.

- **Primary (Midnight Chrome):** A deep, authoritative blue (#1A2B42) used for headers, primary actions, and brand reinforcement. It represents stability and years of experience.
- **Secondary (Caution Amber):** A high-visibility industrial orange (#F59E0B) reserved for "Call to Action" buttons, service alerts, and urgency. It draws the eye and prompts movement.
- **Tertiary (Steel Slate):** A neutral metallic blue-gray (#64748B) used for secondary icons and supportive information.
- **Surface & Background:** We use a very clean slate-white (#F8FAFC) for the main background to ensure the shop's photography and bold blue elements stand out without feeling cluttered.

## Typography

The typography strategy focuses on "Technical Clarity."

- **Headlines:** Uses **Hanken Grotesk**. Its sharp, geometric terminals and tight apertures give it a modern, engineered feel that communicates precision.
- **Body:** Uses **Inter** for maximum legibility across all digital interfaces. It remains neutral and professional, allowing the content to be the hero.
- **Labels & Data:** Uses **JetBrains Mono** for technical specs, phone numbers (318-512-4755), and service categories. This monospaced font reinforces the "industrial/diagnostic" aspect of the business.

Maintain high contrast between headlines (Primary Blue) and body text (Dark Gray) to ensure readability for all age groups in the local community.

## Layout & Spacing

This design system utilizes a **Fixed Grid** approach for desktop to maintain a structured, sturdy feel, transitioning to a fluid layout for mobile devices.

- **Desktop:** 12-column grid with a 1200px max-width. Gutters are set to 24px to provide enough breathing room between technical information blocks.
- **Mobile:** 4-column fluid grid with 16px side margins. 
- **Spacing Rhythm:** Based on an 8px linear scale. Large sections (like "Service Areas") should use 64px or 80px vertical padding to emphasize a "Clean Shop" aesthetic.

Align all text-heavy components to the left to maintain an organized, ledger-like appearance.

## Elevation & Depth

We avoid excessive layering to keep the UI grounded and "heavy" like automotive parts.

- **Tonal Layers:** Use subtle differences in background color (Slate 50 vs White) to define different content zones.
- **Shadows:** Use "Machine Shadows"—short, dark, and slightly more opaque than standard web shadows (e.g., `offset: 0 2px, blur: 4px, color: rgba(26, 43, 66, 0.12)`). They should make elements look like they are sitting firmly on a workbench rather than floating in air.
- **Borders:** Use 1px solid borders in a light gray-blue for cards and input fields to reinforce the structured, industrial feel.

## Shapes

The shape language balance "Rugged" and "Modern."

- **Primary Corners:** 0.5rem (8px) radius is the standard for cards and buttons. This provides a modern, approachable feel while remaining professional.
- **Interactive Elements:** Buttons use a slightly more pronounced rounding to feel "touchable" and friendly.
- **Icons:** Should be stroke-based (2px weight) with slightly rounded terminals to match the typography.

## Components

- **Buttons:**
    - *Primary:* Midnight Chrome background with white text. High-contrast, sharp, and authoritative.
    - *CTA (Estimate):* Caution Amber background with dark blue text. Used exclusively for "Get an Estimate" or "Call Now."
- **Cards:** White background, 1px Steel Slate border, and a subtle bottom shadow. Header area of cards should use a light slate tint to separate titles from content.
- **Input Fields:** Thick 2px bottom border when focused (using Caution Amber) to provide clear feedback during form completion.
- **Status Chips:** Use for "In-Progress," "Completed," or "Parts Ordered." These should use the monospaced label font.
- **Service List:** Use high-quality icons paired with bold Hanken Grotesk sub-headers. Each item should have ample vertical padding.
- **Contact Bar:** A persistent footer or header element featuring the address (2515 Washington St) and phone number in the monospaced technical font for quick scanning.
