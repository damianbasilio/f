I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Mike's Mobile Detailing LLC Santa Fe**. 

This design intentionally moves away from standard business templates, adopting a sophisticated "High Desert Editorial" aesthetic that aligns with Santa Fe's premium market.

### Design Rationale:
*   **Visual Motif:** Used architectural thin lines and large-scale serif typography (**Bodoni Moda**) to create an editorial feel, reminiscent of high-end lifestyle magazines.
*   **Color Palette:** A custom palette of "Obsidian," "Sandstone," and "Terracotta" reflects the local landscape and conveys luxury.
*   **Content Strategy:** Prioritized a "Curated Services" section with an elegant numbered layout rather than generic cards, emphasizing the artisanal nature of the work.
*   **Conversion:** A sticky navigation bar ensures the phone number is always accessible, while the "Start Your Transformation" contact form at the base provides a clear call-to-action after establishing credibility.

The technical implementation includes semantic HTML, a responsive layout (375px to Desktop), and smooth scroll transitions. The requested empty map slot is integrated into the "Visit" section as specified.
