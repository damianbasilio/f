The Santa Fe Standard

Est. 2012

In the high-desert landscape of New Mexico, the elements are as beautiful as they are punishing. Intense UV radiation, fine particulate dust, and shifting temperatures demand more than a standard wash. We view detailing through the lens of heritage and technical precision.

Our process is rooted in architectural discipline—stripping away the environmental toll to reveal the intended lines of your vehicle’s design. From vintage collectibles to modern performance machines, we apply a curated approach to preservation.

The Founder

The Hand Behind the Shine

For Mike Martinez, detailing isn't a task—it's a discipline. Born and raised in Santa Fe, Mike grew up respecting the unique challenges the desert poses to fine machinery.

With over a decade of experience in high-end automotive restoration, Mike founded Mike's Mobile Detailing LLC to bring a white-glove, gallery-quality finish directly to his clients. Every stroke of the cloth and every application of ceramic is performed with the eye of an artist and the hands of a craftsman.
