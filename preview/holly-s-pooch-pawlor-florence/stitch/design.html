Holly's Pooch Pawlor was born from a desire to redefine the grooming experience. We believe that grooming is more than a maintenance task; it is a ritual of wellness and a celebration of the bond between guardian and canine.

Our Florence salon is designed as a sanctuary—a modern heritage environment where the warmth of traditional English boutiques meets the precision of contemporary aesthetics.
