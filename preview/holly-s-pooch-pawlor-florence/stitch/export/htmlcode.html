Holly's Vision for Florence.

Founded on the cobbled streets of Florence, Holly's Pooch Pawlor was born from a desire to bridge the gap between traditional pet care and luxury hospitality.

After years of training in world-class grooming institutions, Holly returned to South Carolina with a singular mission: to create a sanctuary where the spirit of the animal is as nurtured as their coat. Every scissor snip and every drop of oil is a testament to this devotion.
