Our Provenance

A Story Written in Flour

Pan Y KaFe was born from a simple desire: to bring the uncompromising quality of Old World European boulangeries to the heart of the Midwest. Founded in 2022 in Webb City, our approach is strictly small-batch.

The Grain

We source organic, stone-milled flour to preserve the nutrients and complex flavors that industrial processing strips away.

The Roast

Our beans are roasted in micro-lots to highlight their unique terroir, from the slopes of Ethiopia to the volcanic soils of Guatemala.

The Community

We are more than a shop; we are a gathering place where the neighborhood finds its pulse over shared tables.
