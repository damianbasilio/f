I've designed and built a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Pan Y KaFe**. 

This design breaks away from generic local-business templates by adopting a **magazine-style layout** that emphasizes artisanal craft and the "Daily Ritual" of coffee and bread.

### Design Highlights:
- **Asymmetric Layout:** I utilized a split-screen hero and staggered content sections to create a premium, non-standard visual flow that guides the eye naturally.
- **Editorial Typography:** The pairing of *Playfair Display* for large, overlapping headlines with a clean, high-contrast sans-serif creates a sophisticated, high-end feel.
- **Visual Motif:** I introduced subtle vertical typography and thin, elegant rule lines to act as a recurring brand signature throughout the page.
- **Artisanal Palette:** The color story is built on deep Espresso browns, soft Flour creams, and golden Crust ambers, evoking the warmth of a morning bakery.
- **Functional Precision:** The site includes a dedicated map slot for your Webb City location, a responsive navigation system, and a refined contact form designed for high-conversion inquiries.

The code is production-ready, featuring semantic HTML, custom CSS properties for the brand colors, and smooth-scroll interactivity.
