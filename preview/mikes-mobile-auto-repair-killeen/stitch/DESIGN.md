I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Mike's Mobile Auto Repair** that moves away from typical local business templates in favor of a high-end "Industrial Chic" aesthetic.

### Key Design Decisions:
*   **Visual Identity**: Established a "Oil & Asphalt" theme using deep charcoal surfaces, heavy rule lines, and cautionary amber accents to convey reliability and premium service.
*   **Typography**: Leveraged **Bebas Neue** for massive, high-impact headlines that command attention, paired with technical monospaced details for a "master tech" feel.
*   **Editorial Layout**: Swapped generic card grids for an asymmetric, technical-manual style layout. The services section uses bold numbering and thick dividers to emphasize precision.
*   **Mobile Concierge Focus**: The copy and structure prioritize the convenience of on-site repair, highlighting Mike's service areas in Cuyahoga Falls and Killeen.
*   **Production-Ready Components**: Included a custom repair request portal, an interactive location section with the required map slot, and a comprehensive technical footer.

The site is fully responsive and built with semantic HTML/CSS to ensure a fast, memorable first impression.
