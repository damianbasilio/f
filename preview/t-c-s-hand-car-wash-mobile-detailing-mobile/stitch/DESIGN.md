I have designed a bespoke, premium homepage for **T.C.'s Hand Car Wash & Mobile Detailing**, following the "Warm Boutique" creative direction. 

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity**: I've established a warm, sophisticated palette using ivory, sand, and amber accents, which creates an artisanal and trustworthy feel.
*   **Editorial Layout**: The site moves away from generic templates, using rounded containers, generous whitespace, and a high-impact photo gallery strip to showcase the quality of work.
*   **Typography**: A pairing of *Libre Caslon Text* for headlines and a clean sans-serif for body text balances classic craftsmanship with modern efficiency.
*   **Core Sections**:
    *   **The Treatment Menu**: Elegantly presented service cards for Hand Washes, Interior Detailing, and Ceramic Protection.
    *   **"Driven by Detail"**: A narrative-led About section that emphasizes local pride in Mobile, AL.
    *   **Visit the Spa**: A dedicated location section with the requested interactive map slot and clear contact details.
    *   **Start Your Transformation**: A streamlined, validation-ready contact form for easy booking.

The result is a production-ready, unique digital presence that positions T.C.'s as the premier choice for automotive care in Mobile.

## Brand & Style
The design system is built to evoke the atmosphere of a premium, artisanal automotive spa. The brand personality is rooted in meticulous detail, professional warmth, and an approachable sense of luxury. It departs from the typical high-octane, aggressive aesthetic of automotive care, opting instead for a "Warm Boutique" style that emphasizes care, preservation, and high-end service.

The visual direction combines **Modern Minimalism** with **Tactile/Skeuomorphic** nuances. It utilizes soft gradients, organic shapes inspired by water and soap, and a sophisticated layering system to create a UI that feels as clean and polished as a newly detailed vehicle.

## Colors
The palette is a curated selection of warm neutrals designed to feel high-end and welcoming.
- **Primary (Soft Amber):** Used for primary calls to action, highlights, and active states. It suggests quality and a "golden hour" glow.
- **Secondary (Ivory/Sand):** Used for large surfaces and section backgrounds to maintain a bright, airy feel.
- **Neutral (Deep Charcoal):** Reserved for typography and structural borders to ensure maximum legibility and a grounded, professional feel.
- **Backgrounds:** Utilize subtle gradients from Ivory to Warm Sand to mimic the soft reflections found on polished automotive paint.

## Typography
This design system employs a high-contrast typographic pairing to balance tradition with efficiency. 
- **Headlines:** Use **Libre Caslon Text**. This classic serif conveys authority, history, and the "boutique" nature of the service.
- **Body & Interface:** Use **Hanken Grotesk**. This contemporary sans-serif provides a sharp, clean contrast that feels modern and highly legible, representing the precision of the detailing process.
- **Hierarchy:** Use generous line heights for body text to maintain an open, premium feel. Labels should use increased letter spacing and uppercase styling for a sophisticated, "branded" look.

## Layout & Spacing
The layout follows a **Fluid Grid** model with generous margins to evoke a sense of space and luxury. 
- **Desktop:** 12-column grid with 64px outer margins. Content should feel centered and breathable.
- **Mobile:** 4-column grid with 16px margins.
- **Philosophy:** Spacing should be used to create "islands" of content. Avoid crowding elements; the white space (or Ivory space) is as important as the content itself. 
- **Water-Droplet Texture:** Use subtle, non-repeating organic shapes as background decorative elements behind large content blocks to break up rigid verticality.

## Elevation & Depth
Depth in this design system is achieved through **Ambient Shadows** and **Tonal Layers**.
- **Shadows:** Use very soft, diffused shadows with a slight Amber tint (`rgba(212, 163, 115, 0.1)`) instead of pure gray. This maintains the "warm" atmosphere.
- **Glassmorphism:** Use subtle backdrop blurs (8px to 16px) for navigation bars and overlays to simulate the translucency of water and soap suds.
- **Depth Levels:**
  - **Level 0 (Base):** Subtle Ivory-to-Sand gradient.
  - **Level 1 (Cards):** Flat white or light cream surfaces with a 1px soft amber-tinted border.
  - **Level 2 (Floating):** Soft, wide-spread shadows for high-priority elements like "Book Now" buttons.

## Shapes
The shape language is organic and approachable, heavily inspired by the "soap-bubble" motif. 
- **Radii:** All primary containers use `rounded-lg` (1rem) or `rounded-xl` (1.5rem) to avoid harsh, industrial corners.
- **Interactive Elements:** Buttons and input fields should favor high roundedness to feel comfortable and "soft" to the touch.
- **Motifs:** Incorporate subtle rule lines that terminate in small circles or "droplets" to reinforce the car wash theme without being literal or kitschy.

## Components
- **Buttons:** Primary buttons should feature a subtle gradient and a soft shadow. Use `label-sm` typography. Hover states should increase the "glow" rather than darkening the color.
- **Chips/Tags:** Use rounded-pill shapes with a light Amber background and Deep Charcoal text for service categories (e.g., "Exterior," "Interior," "Ceramic Coating").
- **Cards:** Cards should have a 1px stroke in a slightly darker sand tone. Use organic, water-droplet shapes as subtle background masks for imagery within the cards.
- **Input Fields:** Use a soft-fill style (light cream) instead of a harsh white box, with a 2px bottom-border highlight when focused.
- **Service Lists:** Use high-contrast rule lines between items, with "droplet" icons as bullet points to maintain the visual motif.
- **Specialty Component (Service Gauge):** A custom circular progress or status indicator that mimics a clean, minimalist dashboard gauge or a soap bubble.
