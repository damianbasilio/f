Meet the Owner

Cradled in Love, Professional by Nature

At Cradled Critters, we believe that pet sitting isn't just a service, it's a sacred trust. For over a decade in Hollister, we have dedicated ourselves to the well-being of the community's most vulnerable family members.

Our approach combines medical expertise (Pet First Aid) with a deep, intuitive understanding of animal behavior. Every visit is documented, every walk is intentional, and every cuddle is genuine.

"Your peace of mind is my priority."
