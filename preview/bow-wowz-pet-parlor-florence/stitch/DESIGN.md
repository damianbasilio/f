## Brand & Style

The brand identity for this design system balances the meticulous care of a high-end medical facility with the warmth and joy of a luxury pet retreat. It targets discerning pet owners who view their animals as family members deserving of the finest experiences.

The design style is **Modern Minimalist with Editorial influences**. It prioritizes clarity and breathability, using generous white space to signal premium positioning. To avoid the sterility often associated with minimalism, the system introduces organic, "fluff-inspired" shapes and a sophisticated color palette that feels grounded yet energetic. Visuals should focus on high-resolution, close-up photography of groomed textures and calm, happy pets, avoiding over-saturated or staged-looking imagery.

## Colors

The palette is designed to evoke trust, warmth, and freshness:

*   **Midnight Fur (#1A1A2E):** Used primarily for typography, deep borders, and high-impact background sections. It provides the "professional" anchor.
*   **Poodle Cream (#F8F7F2):** The primary canvas color. It is softer than pure white, reducing eye strain and creating a "lifestyle" feel.
*   **Paw-print Terracotta (#D48166):** An energetic accent used for primary actions, notifications, and highlighting key services.
*   **Mint Groom (#B8D8BA):** A soothing secondary accent used for success states, secondary badges, or to represent hygiene and freshness.

## Typography

This design system employs a classic editorial pairing. **EB Garamond** provides an authoritative, literary feel for headlines, suggesting a "story-driven" approach to pet care. **Hanken Grotesk** is used for functional text, offering a sharp, modern contrast that ensures high legibility in data-heavy areas like booking forms or price lists.

Hierarchy is maintained through dramatic scale shifts rather than excessive weight changes. Large displays should utilize tighter tracking and slight italicization for a bespoke, "vogue" aesthetic.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout philosophy follows a **Fixed Grid** on desktop to maintain a contained, boutique feel, while transitioning to a fluid model for mobile. 

A 12-column system is used for desktop (64px margins), a 6-column system for tablet, and a 2-column system for mobile. Vertical spacing is intentionally loose (stack-lg) between major sections to emphasize the "spa" experience of relaxation and lack of clutter. Components should use an 8px base unit for internal padding to maintain geometric alignment with the clean typography.

## Elevation & Depth

To maintain a minimalist profile, this design system avoids heavy shadows. Depth is achieved through:

*   **Tonal Layers:** Using `Mint Groom` or subtle shades of `Midnight Fur` (at 5% opacity) to distinguish background cards from the `Poodle Cream` base.
*   **Thin Outlines:** Using 1px solid borders in `Midnight Fur` (at 10-15% opacity) for cards and inputs. This provides a "blueprint" or "architectural" crispness.
*   **Organic Masks:** Images and highlight sections are often clipped into asymmetrical, soft-radius "blobs" that float over the grid, creating depth through layering rather than drop shadows.

## Shapes

The shape language is a juxtaposition of structured containers and organic "fluff" motifs. 

While functional elements like buttons and inputs use a standard **Rounded (0.5rem)** radius for a friendly yet professional feel, larger containers and image masks should utilize highly irregular radii (up to 3rem or "blob" styles) to mimic the organic nature of pets. Dividers must be kept thin (0.5pt to 1pt) to maintain the editorial elegance.

## Components

*   **Buttons:** Primary buttons are `Midnight Fur` with `Poodle Cream` text. Secondary buttons use `Paw-print Terracotta` as a text color with a thin border. Hover states should involve a slight shift to the organic "blob" shape or a subtle scale-up.
*   **Cards:** Use minimal padding and thin outlines. Headlines within cards should be `EB Garamond` to maintain the premium feel.
*   **Inputs:** Clean, bottom-border-only or fully outlined with `0.5rem` rounding. Focus states should use the `Mint Groom` color for a calming effect.
*   **Chips/Badges:** Use `Mint Groom` with a high roundedness (pill-shaped) to represent "Service Features" (e.g., "Hypoallergenic", "Deep Clean").
*   **Dividers:** Vertical and horizontal lines should be `Midnight Fur` at 10% opacity, often interrupted by small, centered icons of a paw or a comb to break the mechanical feel.
*   **Specialty Component (Service Bubble):** An irregular, rounded container used for testimonials or featured service highlights, reflecting the "bubble/fluff" motif.
