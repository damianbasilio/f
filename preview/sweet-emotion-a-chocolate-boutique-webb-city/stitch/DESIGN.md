I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Sweet Emotion - A Chocolate Boutique**. 

This design intentionally breaks away from standard business templates, utilizing a high-fashion layout that mirrors the premium, artisanal nature of the brand.

### Key Design Decisions:
- **Editorial Typography:** I used a high-contrast serif (Playfair Display) for headlines to evoke luxury, paired with a wide-tracked sans-serif for a modern, airy feel.
- **Asymmetric Composition:** The hero and "The Collection" sections use an off-balance grid and overlapping elements to create a more dynamic, "magazine-style" visual interest.
- **Bespoke Visual Motif:** I’ve implemented organic "melt" shape masks and thin, gold-leaf vertical rules to reinforce the chocolate craftsmanship theme without relying on generic icons.
- **Indulgent Palette:** A custom "Midnight Cocoa" and "Silk Ganache" color story provides a sophisticated, high-contrast backdrop that makes the product photography (generated specifically for this brand) feel rich and tactile.
- **Functional Boutique Elements:** The header features an immediate call-to-action with the phone number, while the "Atelier" section focuses on the brand's local Webb City story.

The code is production-ready, featuring smooth-scroll interactivity, a mobile-responsive navigation system, and the required technical specifications for the map and contact modules.
