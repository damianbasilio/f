I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Beauty By Brittany** that departs from standard local-business templates to create a truly unique, premium impression.

### Design Highlights:
- **Editorial Layout**: The homepage uses an asymmetric structure inspired by high-end fashion magazines. Large-scale typography and a "split-screen" hero section create an immediate sense of exclusivity.
- **Modern Romanticism Palette**: I established a visual identity using **Deep Espresso**, **Dusty Rose**, and **Silk Cream**, providing a sophisticated and calming atmosphere that reflects the boutique salon experience.
- **Signature Visual Motifs**: 
    - **Architectural Lines**: Thin, elegant lines serve as both structural elements and "ribbon" motifs that weave through the sections, guiding the user's eye and adding a sense of curated movement.
    - **Large-Scale Serif Type**: Utilizing a high-contrast serif font for key headings establishes a commanding yet feminine brand voice.
- **Non-Standard Navigation**: The services are presented as a staggered, numbered list ("The Eye Atelier," "Skin Couture," "The Bridal Suite") rather than a generic grid, reinforcing the "curated" nature of the business.

This design was built specifically to leverage the aesthetic cues from the brand's Facebook presence while elevating it to a high-fidelity digital residency.
