OUR SPRINGFIELD ROOTS

Born from a Passion for Precision.

Stylin' Nails & Spa began as a vision to transform the standard salon visit into a restorative ritual. Located in the heart of Springfield, we believe that beauty is found in the details.

Our founders, seasoned in the luxury beauty industry, saw an opportunity to bring a "Soft Glam" aesthetic to Campbell Avenue, combining high-fashion sensibilities with the welcoming warmth of our Missouri community. Every chair in our studio is a sanctuary designed for your relaxation.
