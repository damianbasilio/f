I've designed a bespoke, premium homepage for Oakmont Towing & Recovery that moves away from generic local-business templates in favor of a high-impact, editorial visual identity.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Industrial Editorial Aesthetic:** I've used a "Heavy-Duty" layout characterized by thick industrial borders, grid-based alignment, and a high-contrast color story of deep charcoal and safety orange.
*   **Typography & Hierarchy:** Impactful, tight-tracked headings establish an immediate sense of reliability and strength, mirroring the nature of the business.
*   **Custom Visual Assets:** I generated a cinematic hero image of a heavy-duty tow truck set against the Frederick, MD skyline to anchor the local brand identity.
*   **Bespoke Sections:** 
    *   An asymmetrical **Services** grid that highlights technical capabilities.
    *   A high-visibility **24/7 Dispatch** header for immediate emergency utility.
    *   A minimalist **Strategic Map** slot ready for integration, styled to match the industrial theme.
*   **Production-Ready:** The screen includes responsive layout logic, smooth scrolling, and custom-styled form validation UI.

The result is a unique digital presence that feels as robust and professional as the equipment Oakmont operates.
