I've completed the bespoke homepage for **Golden Nails and Spa**, moving away from standard templates to create a high-end, editorial experience.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity**: Established an "Oyster & Champagne" color story using creamy neutrals, deep onyx for contrast, and champagne gold accents to reflect a premium salon atmosphere.
*   **Editorial Layout**: Utilized an asymmetrical grid with overlapping elements and generous whitespace to give the site a custom-designed, boutique magazine feel.
*   **Premium Typography**: Paired the sophisticated *Playfair Display* serif for headlines with a clean, widely-spaced sans-serif for a modern, luxurious hierarchy.
*   **Bespoke Imagery**: Integrated high-fidelity, macro-photography assets that highlight the artistry and serenity of the services, from minimalist manicures to stone therapy.
*   **Production-Ready Structure**: The design includes all required sections—from a credibility-building philosophy block to a functional concierge inquiry form and the specified map slot for the Warner Robins location.
