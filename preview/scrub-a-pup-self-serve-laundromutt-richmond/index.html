A Vision of Home-Grown Hospitality

Scrub-A-Pup was born from a simple observation: our dogs deserve the same level of care and aesthetic consideration that we expect for ourselves.

We've stripped away the chaotic, plastic environment of traditional pet stores and replaced it with a sophisticated, calming space inspired by the natural beauty of the Kentucky Bluegrass region.

Every decision, from the artisanal soaps we source to the ergonomic design of our wash stations, is guided by a commitment to quality and a love for the local community we call home.
