Our Story

Where the raw grit of East 55th meets the refined edge of a runway.

NAIL #1 was born from a desire to strip away the fluff of the traditional salon experience. We found beauty in the industrial architecture of Cleveland—the exposed brick, the weathered copper, the structural honesty.

Our philosophy is simple: we respect the anatomy of the nail as much as we respect the art of the finish. Every technician at NAIL #1 is trained in an editorial-first methodology, ensuring your signature look is both durable and avant-garde.
