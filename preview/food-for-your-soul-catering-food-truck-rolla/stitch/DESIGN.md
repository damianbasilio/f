I've designed and built a bespoke, editorial-quality landing page for **Food For Your Soul Catering**. 

### Design Rationale:
*   **Visual Identity**: I moved away from the "fast food" truck aesthetic, instead creating a "Modern Soulful Comfort" brand. This uses a sophisticated charcoal and gold palette paired with high-contrast serif typography to position the business as a premium catering service.
*   **Editorial Layout**: The homepage features an asymmetrical grid and generous whitespace, giving it the feel of a high-end food magazine rather than a template. 
*   **Memorable Motif**: I've used thin, elegant rules (1px borders) and subtle typographic details to create a consistent, handcrafted visual thread throughout the page.
*   **Strategic Sections**:
    *   **Hero**: A high-impact opening using your food truck imagery to establish immediate brand authority.
    *   **Credibility & Story**: Dedicated sections for "The Art of the Soul" and "Chef Catherine's Vision" to build trust and highlight the local Missouri roots.
    *   **The Experience**: A custom-designed service grid that clearly differentiates the truck, private catering, and signature platters.
    *   **Conversion-Ready**: A minimalist lead capture form and a prominent call-to-action in the header to drive bookings.

The site is fully responsive, uses semantic HTML, and includes the requested map container for the Rolla, MO location.
