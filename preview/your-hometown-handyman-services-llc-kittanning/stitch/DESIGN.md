## Brand & Style

The design system is built to evoke the feeling of a trusted neighborhood expert—someone who is as skilled with their hands as they are reliable with their word. The brand personality is **Professional, Grounded, and Approachable**. It balances the grit of manual labor with the precision of modern service management.

The visual style follows a **Modern Corporate** aesthetic with a **Tactile** edge. It avoids the clinical coldness of tech-heavy SaaS by using rich, wood-inspired accents and high-quality photography that captures real craftsmanship. The UI is characterized by clean lines, structured grids, and a sense of "sturdiness" that mirrors the physical work being performed.

**Key Visual Principles:**
- **Reliability over Flash:** Use solid blocks of color and clear borders to suggest stability.
- **Bespoke Craftsmanship:** Avoid generic layouts; use asymmetrical grid placements and varied container sizes to feel custom-made.
- **Local Connection:** Use photography that features human hands, real tools, and local architectural details.

## Colors

The palette is rooted in materials and trust. 
- **Sturdy Navy (#1e3a8a):** Used for primary actions, headers, and navigation to establish authority and reliability.
- **Warm Cedar (#92400e):** An accent color used sparingly for calls-to-action, highlights, and icons to provide a sense of warmth and natural craftsmanship.
- **Clean Slate (#475569):** Used for secondary text and decorative UI elements, providing a neutral balance to the more saturated primary tones.
- **Soft Accents:** A very light cedar tint (#fef3c7) is used for background sections to break up long pages without losing the warm, approachable feel.

## Typography

This design system utilizes a dual-typeface system to balance character with utility.
- **Headings (Montserrat):** Chosen for its geometric strength and professional "signage" feel. It should be used in bold weights to anchor sections and communicate confidence.
- **Body & Labels (Inter):** Chosen for its exceptional legibility and neutral, systematic tone. It handles dense information (like service lists and pricing) with clarity.

**Hierarchy Rules:**
- Use `display-lg` for hero sections with tight letter-spacing.
- All `label-md` elements should be in uppercase with slight letter spacing to differentiate from body text.
- Maintain generous line heights for body text to ensure readability for all age demographics in the community.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout philosophy is based on a **Fixed Grid** for desktop to maintain a "contained" and organized feel, and a fluid 4-column system for mobile.

- **Desktop (1200px max-width):** 12-column grid with 24px gutters. Use "offset" layouts where images span 7 columns and text blocks span 5 to create a bespoke, editorial feel.
- **Vertical Rhythm:** Use the `xl` (80px) spacing between major sections to provide "breathing room" and emphasize the premium nature of the service.
- **Bespoke Overlaps:** Elements like cards or images should occasionally overlap section backgrounds by 24-48px to break the "template" feel.

## Elevation & Depth

To convey professionalism and "sturdy" construction, the design system avoids heavy shadows in favor of **Tonal Layering** and **Subtle Depth**.

- **Surface Levels:** The main background is a light slate (#f8fafc). Content sits on pure white (#ffffff) containers.
- **Shadows:** Use a single, highly-diffused shadow for interactive elements like cards.
  - *Shadow Style:* `0px 4px 20px rgba(30, 58, 138, 0.08)` (A slight navy tint to the shadow to keep it integrated with the brand).
- **Borders:** Use 1px solid borders in Slate (#e2e8f0) for input fields and non-elevated containers. This reinforces the "clean and precise" brand value.

## Shapes

The shape language is **Soft (0.25rem)**. This provides enough rounding to feel modern and friendly without becoming "bubbly" or losing the professional edge associated with construction and craftsmanship.

- **Base Radius:** 4px (Buttons, Inputs).
- **Large Radius:** 8px (Cards, Photo containers).
- **Extra Large:** 12px (Modal overlays, Hero images).
- **Photography:** All photos should have 8px rounded corners. Avoid circles except for very small status indicators or avatars.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Sturdy Navy (#1e3a8a) background, white text. High contrast, 4px corner radius.
- **Secondary:** White background, 1px Navy border.
- **CTA:** Warm Cedar (#92400e) background. Reserve this strictly for "Book Now" or "Get a Quote" actions.

### Cards
- Use white backgrounds with 8px corner radius and the subtle navy-tinted shadow.
- Header images in cards should have no top radius if they are full-bleed within the card, or an 8px radius if padded.

### Input Fields
- Use Slate (#475569) for labels.
- Borders should be 1px solid (#e2e8f0), turning Navy (#1e3a8a) on focus.
- Backgrounds should be pure white for maximum legibility.

### Chips & Tags
- Use light Slate or Cedar backgrounds with dark text for categorizing services (e.g., "Plumbing," "Carpentry").

### Service Lists
- Use custom bullet icons (e.g., a small Cedar-colored square or checkmark) rather than standard browser bullets to reinforce the "bespoke" feel.
