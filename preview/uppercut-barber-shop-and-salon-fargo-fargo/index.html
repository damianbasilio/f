ETHOS

THE ETHOS

UPPERCUT was established with a singular objective: to strip away the vanity of the modern salon and return to the structural integrity of the master barber.

In a city defined by its grit and industrial roots, we believe grooming should reflect that same steadfast character. Our methodology is rooted in technical geometry, understanding the planes of the face and the architecture of the skull to provide a cut that maintains its form long after you leave the chair.

We are members of the Fargo community, supporting the trades and the workers who build this region. Precision is our baseline. Reliability is our promise.
