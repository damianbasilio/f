OUR HERITAGE

From White Linens to Open Roads.

For decades, brothers Marcus and Julian honed their craft in some of the most prestigious kitchens in the Southeast. They thrived on the precision, the heat, and the artistry of fine dining—but they missed the connection.

2 Bros Coastal Cuisine was born from a desire to bring that level of culinary rigor to the people. No reservations, no dress codes—just exceptional, chef-prepared coastal food served under the North Carolina sky.

Today, they combine their Michelin-star training with the rugged beauty of Wilmington to create an experience that is both accessible and extraordinary.
