Our Roots

TWO BROTHERS, ONE MISSION.

Raised between the salt marshes of Leland and the docks of Wilmington, we didn't just learn to cook—we learned to respect the water.

2 Bros was born from a simple realization: the best seafood doesn't need white tablecloths. It needs heat, heart, and the shortest distance possible from the net to the plate.

We've combined professional culinary training with the grit of a food truck to bring you a bespoke experience that tastes like the Atlantic feels.

Mastery. Sea-Salt. Grit.
