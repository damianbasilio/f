I've completed the bespoke homepage for **2 Bros Coastal Cuisine**. 

### Design Rationale:
- **Visual Identity:** I moved away from standard "food truck" templates in favor of a **Coastal Editorial** aesthetic. This uses a high-contrast serif font (Bricolage Grotesque) and a sophisticated palette of Ocean Deep and Sea Salt to position the brand as a premium, chef-driven experience.
- **Layout:** The design uses asymmetrical compositions and overlapping elements to create a magazine-like feel. For example, the Hero section features a massive typographic statement that breaks traditional container rules, while the "Offerings" section uses staggered text to avoid a generic grid look.
- **Photography:** I integrated the generated editorial images to highlight both the refined nature of the food and the scenic coastal vibe of their Wilmington/Leland service area.
- **Functionality:** The site is fully production-ready with semantic HTML5, responsive CSS, and a dedicated map slot for your live tracker.
