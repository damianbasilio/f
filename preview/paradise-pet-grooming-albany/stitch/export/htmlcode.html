OUR STORY

Where Luxury
Meets Love

Founded on the belief that every pet deserves the same level of care and aesthetic attention as their owners, Paradise Pet Grooming was born in the heart of Albany, Oregon.

We are not just a grooming studio; we are a retreat. Our team of certified artists combines years of technical expertise with a deep understanding of animal psychology to ensure every visit is peaceful and productive.

From our choice of sulfate-free, botanical shampoos to our hand-drying techniques that reduce anxiety, every detail is meticulously curated for the discerning pet.
