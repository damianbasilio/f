I have designed and built a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for Panda Foot & Spa. This design departs from standard local-business templates to create a premium, memorable first impression that reflects the brand's unique identity.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Visual Identity**: I established a "Serene Zen" aesthetic using a palette of Midnight Bamboo and Warm Stone, paired with a high-contrast typographic hierarchy (Playfair Display and Inter).
*   **Bespoke Layout**: The page features an asymmetrical, grid-breaking hero section and a custom service display that prioritizes visual storytelling over standard card grids.
*   **Signature Visual Motif**: Subtle circular motifs and intentional whitespace are used throughout to reinforce the theme of balance and stillness.
*   **Production-Ready Features**: 
    *   **High-End Imagery**: I generated custom, editorial-quality photography specifically for the spa's services.
    *   **Technical Compliance**: The code includes semantic HTML, custom CSS properties for easy brand management, and the required `#visit` map slot for your future integration.
    *   **Memorable Credibility**: A dedicated section highlights the brand's philosophy, positioning Panda Foot & Spa as Owensboro's premier wellness destination.

The site is fully responsive and ready for production, providing a professional and tranquil digital experience for your clients.
