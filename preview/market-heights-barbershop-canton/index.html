A Legacy Carved in Steel.

"The haircut is a formality. The conversation is the soul."

For over six decades, Market Heights Barbershop has stood as a bastion of traditional masculinity and artisanal grooming in Canton, Ohio. Founded in 1957, we have witnessed the evolution of style while remaining steadfast in our commitment to the tools that matter: the straight razor, the shear, and the steady hand.

Master Barber Scott

Scott & The Tradition.

Scott doesn't just cut hair; he stewards a legacy. Having taken the mantle from the generations before him, he views every chair session as a unique dialogue between history and the modern man.

The "Steel & Tobacco" philosophy is simple: use the best materials, take the time required for perfection, and treat every client as a member of the Market Heights family. In a world of fast-fashion and quick-cuts, Scott remains committed to the slow, deliberate art of the master barber.
