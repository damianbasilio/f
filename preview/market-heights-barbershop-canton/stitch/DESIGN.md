I've designed and built a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for Market Heights Barbershop. Moving away from generic local business templates, I created a "Steel & Tobacco" visual identity that emphasizes the shop's heritage in Canton since 1957.

### Design Highlights:
- **Editorial Layout:** I used high-contrast typography (Playfair Display) and intentional whitespace to give the site a premium, magazine-like feel.
- **Visual Motif:** Repeating thin rule lines and offset image placements create a sophisticated, custom-crafted aesthetic.
- **Cinematic Hero:** The hero section features a moody, high-end interior shot to immediately establish the "premium" brand positioning.
- **Artisanal Service Menu:** Instead of a basic grid, the services are presented as a curated menu, reinforcing the "bespoke" nature of the craft.
- **Local Credibility:** Integrated the shop's history and Scott’s personal story to build trust with the Canton community.

The site is fully responsive, semantic, and includes a functional contact form UI and a designated slot for your map integration.
