Our Journey

The Sanctuary at Sleigh Road

For Sheryl Legie, the path to healing was a return to the quietude of New Hampshire's landscapes. After years of clinical practice, she envisioned a space where the clinical met the ethereal.

Located on a historic route in Campton, MindSoulTouch is more than a studio, it is a sanctuary built on the foundations of presence, technical mastery, and the grounding energy of the White Mountains.

Every session is a conversation between practitioner and presence.
