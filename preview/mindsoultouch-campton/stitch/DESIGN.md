I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **MindSoulTouch** that captures the brand's unique "Earthy Modernism" identity. 

### Design Highlights:
*   **Unique Identity:** Moving away from generic wellness templates, the design uses a sophisticated palette of forest greens and copper accents, paired with high-contrast editorial typography.
*   **Asymmetrical Layout:** The services and hero sections use overlapping elements and generous whitespace to create a premium, rhythmic feel.
*   **Local Roots:** The New Hampshire location is woven into the narrative, from the professional practitioner portrait to the grounding studio imagery.
*   **Bespoke Interactions:** Includes a custom mobile navigation, smooth scrolling, and a refined inquiry form—all tailored to provide a memorable first impression.

The site is production-ready, fully responsive, and built with clean, semantic code.
