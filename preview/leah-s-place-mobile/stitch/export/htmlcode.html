The Story

A sanctuary where past and present coexist.

Leah's Place was born from a desire to create a physical narrative of Mobile's rich cultural tapestry. We believe that hospitality is an art form, and that the objects we surround ourselves with should carry the weight of craftsmanship and the light of memory.

From our historic storefront on McGregor Avenue to the curated collections within, every detail is an invitation to slow down and appreciate the enduring beauty of the South.

"Tradition is not the worship of ashes, but the preservation of fire."
