I have completed the bespoke homepage for **Da Wash Auto Spa**. 

This design intentionally moves away from standard automotive templates, adopting a high-end "Midnight & Chrome" editorial aesthetic. I’ve focused on the concept of "The Art of the Detail," treating vehicle maintenance as a restoration process rather than a simple utility.

### Key Design Decisions:
- **Visual Identity:** A palette of deep obsidian and metallic silver, accented by "Deep Sea Teal" to represent precision and high-end water treatment.
- **Editorial Layout:** I used asymmetrical grids, generous whitespace, and high-contrast serif typography (Playfair Display) to evoke the feel of a luxury fashion or architecture magazine.
- **Bespoke Motifs:** Thin silver rules (borders) and sharp, precise edges are used throughout to reinforce the business’s focus on meticulous detail and craftsmanship.
- **Custom Assets:** I generated cinematic, moody imagery focusing on texture and precision—water beading on obsidian paint, carbon fiber detailing, and macro product shots—to create a premium first impression.
- **Functional Sections:** The site includes a credibility bar with key metrics, a structured service atelier, an "About" section focused on philosophy over speed, and a minimalist contact inquiry form.

The code is fully production-ready, featuring semantic HTML, responsive CSS with custom properties, and smooth scroll interactions.
