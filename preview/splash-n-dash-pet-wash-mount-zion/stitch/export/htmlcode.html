Our Story

Friends First,
Always.

Founded in the heart of the Mount Zion community, Splash ‘N’ Dash was born from a simple belief: every pet deserves to be treated like family. We’ve traded clinical metal tables for comfortable, stress-free zones where wagging tails are our favorite metric of success.

Our team consists of lifelong animal lovers and certified professionals who understand that a great groom is about more than just a trim—it's about health, hygiene, and that incredible feeling of a fresh start.
