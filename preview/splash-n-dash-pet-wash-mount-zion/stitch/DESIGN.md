I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Splash ‘N’ Dash Pet Wash**. 

### Design Highlights:
*   **Unique Visual Identity:** Moving away from standard templates, I used a high-contrast typographic hierarchy (Playfair Display) paired with a "Bubble Bath White" and "Deep Reservoir" navy palette.
*   **Asymmetrical Services Grid:** Instead of a generic 3-column row, I used an overlapping, asymmetrical layout for the services to create a premium, curated feel.
*   **The "Splash Standard":** Dedicated a section to your unique value propositions (Filtered Water, Premium Shampoos) to establish immediate credibility.
*   **Local Focus:** The "Visit Our Studio" section includes your specific contact details and a custom-styled slot for your map.

The design is fully responsive and includes a mobile navigation menu, smooth scrolling, and interactive hover states for a polished, professional finish.

## Brand & Style

This design system establishes a high-end, editorial identity for a premium local service. It blends the tactile, approachable nature of organic shapes with the structured discipline of modern editorial layouts. The visual narrative centers on "Bespoke Care"—positioning pet hygiene not as a chore, but as a luxury ritual.

The style is **Modern-Organic**. It utilizes expansive white space (the "Bubble Bath" effect) to create a sense of cleanliness and calm. To prevent the design from feeling too clinical, we introduce "architectural" elements: thick, purposeful borders and a sophisticated typographic hierarchy. The emotional response should be one of immediate trust, professional excellence, and a touch of playful vibrance.

- **Minimalism:** Use generous margins to let high-quality photography of pets breathe.
- **Organic Accents:** Incorporate "blob" or "bubble" masking for secondary imagery to soften the rigid grid.
- **Architectural Framing:** Use 2px to 3px borders to define key containers, creating a "boutique shop" feel.

## Colors

The palette is anchored by high-contrast pairings that evoke water, cleanliness, and energy.

- **Deep Reservoir (#002B49):** Our primary brand color. Used for headings, heavy borders, and primary buttons. It provides the "architectural" weight and signals premium reliability.
- **Suds & Citrus (#FFB800 / #FF7A00):** A vibrant yellow-to-orange range used sparingly for calls to action, highlights, and interactive states. It adds the "playful" spark to the editorial foundation.
- **Bubble Bath White (#F9F7F2):** The primary canvas. This is a warm, sophisticated off-white that feels more bespoke and softer on the eyes than pure #FFFFFF.
- **Surface Accents:** Use subtle tints of Deep Reservoir at 5-10% opacity for secondary backgrounds and dividers.

## Typography

The typography strategy relies on the tension between the classic, high-contrast serif and the wide, airy sans-serif.

- **Headlines:** `Playfair Display` is set with tight leading and slight negative letter spacing for a dramatic, editorial look. Use Italic styles for emphasis or "pet names" to add personality.
- **Body:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` provides the "wide" modern feel. It should be set with generous line heights to maintain the breezy, premium atmosphere.
- **Labels:** Small caps or all-caps with increased tracking (0.08em) should be used for category labels, breadcrumbs, and eyebrow text to reinforce the architectural structure.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout utilizes a **Fixed Grid** system for desktop (1280px max-width) to maintain the editorial integrity, transitioning to a fluid model on mobile.

- **Vertical Rhythm:** Use large vertical gaps (`120px`) between major sections to emphasize a premium, "unrushed" user experience.
- **Architectural Borders:** Elements should often be "boxed" in 2px Deep Reservoir borders. Use these borders to create a clear separation between content types.
- **The "Bubble" Offset:** Occasionally break the rigid grid with organic, rounded image masks or floating "bubble" background elements that sit behind the grid, creating depth.
- **Mobile:** Margins shrink to `24px`, and the 12-column grid collapses to a single column. Maintain the thick borders even on mobile to keep the brand's structural identity.

## Elevation & Depth

This system avoids traditional shadows in favor of **Tonal Layering and Bold Outlines**. Depth is created through stacking and color contrast rather than light simulation.

- **Flat Depth:** Use 2px borders for all primary containers (cards, inputs). 
- **Active States:** When an element is interacted with, it does not "lift"; instead, it shifts 2px-4px down and right, with a "hard shadow" (a solid Deep Reservoir block) appearing behind it—mimicking a physical, tactile press.
- **Glassmorphism (The "Steam" Effect):** In specific overlays (like a booking modal), use a high-blur (20px+) backdrop filter with a 70% opacity "Bubble Bath White" fill to simulate a steamy, spa-like environment.

## Shapes

The shape language is a deliberate mix of **Hard Geometry** and **Soft Organics**.

- **Containers:** All primary structural elements (buttons, input fields, cards) use `0.5rem` (Rounded) corners to keep them feeling modern and friendly.
- **The "Suds" Motif:** Decorative elements, image masks, and background accents should use highly irregular "bubble" shapes (variable border-radius, e.g., `60% 40% 30% 70% / 60% 30% 70% 40%`).
- **Icons:** Use medium-stroke icons with rounded terminals to match the `plusJakartaSans` weight.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Deep Reservoir background, Bubble Bath White text. 2px solid border. Bold, all-caps labels.
- **Secondary:** Transparent background, Deep Reservoir 2px border and text.
- **Accent:** Suds & Citrus background. Reserved for "Book Now" or "Emergency Wash."

### Cards
- Use a 2px Deep Reservoir border. 
- Headers inside cards should use `headline-md`. 
- Padding should be generous (`stack-lg`). 
- On hover, cards can display a solid offset shadow in Suds & Citrus.

### Input Fields
- Underlined style or fully boxed with 2px borders. 
- Placeholder text in a lightened tint of Deep Reservoir.
- Focus state: Border changes to Suds & Citrus.

### Chips & Badges
- Used for "Service Tags" (e.g., "Nail Trim", "Organic Shampoo"). 
- Pill-shaped (`rounded-xl`) with a light Deep Reservoir tint and dark text.

### Interactive "Bubble"
- A unique component for this system: A floating, organically shaped action button or floating stat-counter that gently "wobbles" or drifts, reinforcing the water/bubble motif.
