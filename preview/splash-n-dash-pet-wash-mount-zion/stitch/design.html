NOT JUST A WASH,
A MOVEMENT.

At Splash 'N' Dash, we believe grooming should be an extension of the love you give your pet every day. Our mission was born out of a desire to create a space that MT. Zion pet owners could trust implicitly.

We combine the rigor of professional techniques with a playful, editorial environment that reduces stress for pets and their humans alike. No cold clinic vibes—just bubbles, treats, and clean coats.
