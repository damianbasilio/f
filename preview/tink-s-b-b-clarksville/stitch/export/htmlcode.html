HERITAGE NOTES

Constructed in 1894, the residence of Tink's B&B has stood as a silent witness to the evolution of Clarksville. Restored with surgical precision, we have preserved the original heart pine flooring and hand-carved crown moldings while integrating a discreet, modern technological infrastructure.

The Heart

Tink’s B&B is more than a destination; it is a personal passion project born from a love for Clarksville’s architectural history and the art of Southern hospitality.

Named after the founder’s grandmother, whose porch was always open and whose tea was always sweet, we strive to recreate that same sense of effortless belonging in a contemporary, elevated setting.
