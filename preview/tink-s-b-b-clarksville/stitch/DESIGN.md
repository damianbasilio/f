I've designed and built a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Tink's B & B**, moving far beyond a standard template to create a unique "Tennessee Twilight" brand identity.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity**: Established the "Tennessee Twilight" palette—Deep Indigo, Warm Parchment, and Burnished Copper—paired with high-contrast **Bodoni Moda** typography for an "Editorial Southern" feel.
*   **Architectural Motifs**: Used arched image frames and hairline copper rules throughout the layout to mirror the refined, historic architecture of Clarksville.
*   **Editorial Layout**: The page features asymmetrical sections and generous whitespace, intentionally designed to feel like a high-end travel publication rather than a generic booking site.
*   **Production-Ready Sections**:
    *   **Hero**: An immersive "Southern Refrain" entrance with architectural framing.
    *   **The Provenance**: A credibility section styled with guest "chronicles" and heritage notes.
    *   **The Stay & The Heart**: Bespoke offerings and about sections using varied, overlapping layouts to maintain interest.
    *   **Interactive Visit**: A dedicated #visit section featuring the required semantic map slot and location details.
    *   **Connect**: A minimalist contact suite with custom-styled form validation UI.

The result is a memorable, premium first impression that positions Tink's B & B as a destination of quiet confidence and modern luxury.

How would you like to refine the experience? We could:
1. **Add Motion**: Introduce subtle parallax effects on the arched frames or smooth fade-ins for the editorial text.
2. **Expand the Narrative**: Create a dedicated "Suites" detail page with more architectural photography.
3. **Refine the Brand**: Adjust the copper accents to a silver or gold leaf finish for a different luxury tone.

## Brand & Style

The design system is rooted in the concept of "Modern Heirloom"—a bridge between the historical charm of Clarksville and contemporary luxury. It targets a discerning traveler seeking "Southern Sophistication," evoking an emotional response of tranquility, exclusivity, and storied comfort.

The visual style is **Editorial Minimalism** with **Tactile accents**. It leverages the quiet confidence of high-end print journalism: expansive whitespace, intentional asymmetry, and a focus on high-quality photography. Architectural motifs—specifically the soft arch and the precision of thin metallic rules—provide a structural framework that feels both grounded and ethereal.

## Colors

The "Tennessee Twilight" palette moves away from standard hospitality blues toward a more evocative, environmental narrative. 

- **Deep Indigo (Primary):** Used for primary text and high-impact containers to represent the night sky over the Cumberland River.
- **Warm Parchment (Base):** The primary background color, providing a softer, more premium feel than stark white.
- **Burnished Copper (Accent):** Reserved for interactive elements, hairline rules, and subtle highlights, mimicking traditional hardware and southern craftsmanship.
- **Sage Mist (Secondary):** A muted botanical tone used for secondary backgrounds and soft UI elements, grounding the digital experience in nature.

## Typography

The typographic hierarchy is the cornerstone of this design system's editorial feel. 

**Bodoni Moda** serves as the high-contrast Serif for headlines, exuding elegance and tradition. It should be used with tight tracking in display sizes to emphasize its verticality.

**DM Sans** is the utilitarian counterpart. For body text, it is set with generous line heights to ensure readability. For labels and navigation, it is often set in uppercase with wide letter-spacing (`0.15em`), creating a sense of luxury and breathing room characteristic of boutique catalogs.

## Layout & Spacing

This design system employs a **Fixed Grid** on desktop (12 columns, 1200px max-width) and a **Fluid Grid** on mobile (4 columns). 

The layout philosophy emphasizes "the pause." White space is not empty; it is a structural element used to separate content narratives. 
- **The Rule:** Thin 1px Copper rules should be used horizontally to divide sections or vertically to separate columns, replacing traditional shadow-based containers.
- **Asymmetry:** Key imagery should often be offset from the center or bleed off one edge of the grid to maintain an editorial, non-templated aesthetic.

## Elevation & Depth

To maintain a sophisticated "Modern Heirloom" aesthetic, this design system rejects heavy shadows in favor of **Tonal Layers** and **Structural Outlines**.

- **Surface Levels:** Use 'Warm Parchment' for the primary canvas and 'Sage Mist' or a 5% opacity 'Deep Indigo' for inset areas (e.g., booking forms).
- **Subtle Depth:** Where depth is required (such as an overlay menu), use a "Diffusion Shadow": a very large blur (60px+) with extremely low opacity (4%) tinted with Deep Indigo.
- **Glassmorphism:** Reserved exclusively for the primary navigation header when scrolling over imagery, using a subtle backdrop blur (12px) to maintain legibility without losing the visual context of the photography.

## Shapes

The shape language is defined by the **Arch Motif**.
- **The Arch:** Large containers and featured image masks should utilize a top-rounded "Arched" corner radius (standard `rounded-xl` for bottom corners, but custom 50% width radius for top corners) to evoke architectural windows.
- **Standard UI:** Interactive elements like inputs and smaller cards use `0.5rem` (Rounded) to feel approachable but deliberate.
- **Buttons:** Rectangular with a very slight `2px` radius or full Pill-shaped depending on the context of the call-to-action.

## Components

### Buttons & Interaction
- **Primary Button:** Deep Indigo background, Parchment text, uppercase DM Sans. Hover state transitions the background to Burnished Copper with a slight 4px upward "lift" via transform.
- **Secondary Button:** Ghost style with a 1px Copper border. Hover state fills the background with a 10% Copper tint.
- **Copper Rules:** Use `rule-weight` (1px) in Burnished Copper to underline section headers or flank "Modern Heirloom" text labels.

### Input Fields & Controls
- **Inputs:** Underline style only (1px Deep Indigo) to mimic a guestbook signature line. Labels use the `label-caps` style positioned above the line.
- **Chips:** Sage Mist background with Deep Indigo text, used for amenities (e.g., "Garden View," "Fireplace").

### Cards
- **Room Cards:** Use the Arch Motif for the image at the top. The text section below is uncontained (no border), relying on the 120px `section-gap` and alignment to the grid for structure.
