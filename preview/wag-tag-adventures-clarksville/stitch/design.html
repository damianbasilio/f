Our Roots

Born from the Trail

Wag & Tag Adventures was founded on a simple realization: your dog's happiness is directly proportional to the size of their world. As a lifelong resident of Clarksville and an avid hiker, I saw too many high-energy dogs limited to the same four corners of a fenced yard.

We believe every dog has an "inner explorer." Our mission is to safely unlock that side of them, providing professional supervision, GPS tracking, and pure outdoor engagement that standard dog-walking services simply can't match.

When you trust us with your pet, you're not just hiring a walker—you're booking a curated experience designed for their health, wellness, and adventure-loving soul.

Sarah J., Founder
