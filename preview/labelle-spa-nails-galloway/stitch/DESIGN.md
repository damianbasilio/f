I’ve designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **LaBelle Spa & Nails**, specifically tailored to establish a premium, unique online presence that transcends a standard local business mockup.

### Design Highlights:
- **Quiet Luxury Identity**: I established a sophisticated visual system using an 'Ivory Silk' palette with 'Deep Orchid' accents, conveying a sense of calm and exclusivity.
- **Editorial Layout**: The design moves away from generic grids, employing generous whitespace and a high-contrast typographic hierarchy (Playfair Display) to create a "sanctuary" feel on the screen.
- **Custom Visual Motifs**: I integrated thin, elegant gold lines and asymmetric photo placements (featuring custom-generated high-fashion spa imagery) to maintain a bespoke, professional atmosphere.
- **Production-Ready Features**:
    - A smooth-scrolling experience with a mobile-responsive navigation.
    - A dedicated **#visit** section with the requested functional map slot and local contact details.
    - A minimalist, high-fidelity contact form and refined service highlights.

This design is crafted to make a memorable first impression for Galloway residents, positioning LaBelle as the premier destination for professional beauty and serenity.
