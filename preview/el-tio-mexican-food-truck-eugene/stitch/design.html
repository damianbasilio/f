Nuestra Historia

The Soul of El Tio

Born from the vibrant markets of Oaxaca and the hardworking streets of Eugene, El Tio is more than a food truck—it is a culinary bridge.

Our journey began with a single mission: to elevate the perception of Mexican street food. Every tortilla is hand-pressed daily, every salsa is roasted over open flame, and every recipe is a chapter of family heritage passed through generations.
