The Standard of Care

Founded in the heart of Laramie, Fluffy Puppy Precision Grooming represents a departure from the chaotic environments of traditional pet shops. We operate on a principle of focused attention, treating every animal with the anatomical respect and clinical precision they deserve.

Our shop at 363 West Garfield Street is a sanctuary of cleanliness and technical excellence. By maintaining a controlled environment, we reduce pet anxiety and ensure that our Certified Hygienists can perform their craft with unerring accuracy.
