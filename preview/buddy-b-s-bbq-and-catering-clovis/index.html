THE ART
OF THE PIT

Where the raw intensity of the fire meets the surgical precision of the craft. We don't just cook; we curate a sensory journey through smoke and time.

The Wood

We exclusively burn seasoned Post Oak. It provides a clean, consistent heat and a subtle sweetness that respects the integrity of the protein rather than masking it.

The Smoke

Our smoke is blue and nearly invisible. It is a vapor, not a particulate, ensuring a delicate infusion that develops a profound depth of flavor over sixteen grueling hours.

The Patience

There are no shortcuts in the pit. Every cut is monitored by hand, rested for hours, and served only when the collagen has fully surrendered to the heat.
