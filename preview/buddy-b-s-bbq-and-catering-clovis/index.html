Rooted in Fire, Refined by Tradition

Buddy B’s isn't just a kitchen; it’s a living legacy. Born in the heart of Clovis, NM, our methods haven't changed since the first pit was lit. We believe that true barbecue is a slow conversation between wood, fire, and meat.

Led by family recipes and a relentless pursuit of the perfect smoke ring, we’ve transformed a local staple into a destination for those who appreciate the finer details of the pitmaster's craft.
