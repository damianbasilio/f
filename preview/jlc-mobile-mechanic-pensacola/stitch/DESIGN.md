I have designed a bespoke, high-fidelity landing page for **JLC Mobile Mechanic**, specifically tailored to the Pensacola market with a premium, editorial aesthetic.

### Design Highlights:
- **Visual Identity:** Built around the **"Asphalt & Amber"** design system, the site uses a high-contrast dark mode with industrial amber accents (#FFB800) to evoke a sense of mechanical precision and rugged reliability.
- **Editorial Layout:** I’ve moved away from standard templates, using a structured grid-line motif, large-scale typography, and overlapping elements to create a memorable brand impression.
- **Bespoke Imagery:** 
    - A cinematic hero image (**A high-end, professional close-up of a modern car engine being worked on by a mechanic's hands with clean tools. The lighting is cinematic and moody, with deep shadows and warm amber highlights reflecting off metallic surfaces. Shot in a clean, professional garage setting. No people's faces visible. Industrial, premium, high-resolution photography.**) captures the "hands-on" precision of elite diagnostics.
    - An environmental shot (**A rugged, professional mobile mechanic van parked in a scenic Pensacola, Florida location during golden hour. The van is clean and features subtle branding. High-end, editorial automotive photography style. Wide shot showing the environment.**) features a professional van at golden hour, grounding the service in its Pensacola location.
- **Custom Components:** 
    - **Header/Footer:** Integrated the JLC wordmark (**JLC Logo**) and clear utility-focused navigation.
    - **Interactive Services:** A minimalist vertical list designed for high-impact readability of core capabilities.
    - **Map Integration:** A structural map slot ready for API integration, framed to match the industrial theme.
- **Craftsmanship:** Every detail, from the sharp 90-degree edges to the condensed Oswald typography, was chosen to reflect the "Engineered for Reliability" brand promise.

The code is fully production-ready, semantic, and responsive for desktop and mobile devices.
