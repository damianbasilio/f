I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for Vests Greenhouse that transitions the brand from a social-only presence to a premium digital destination. 

### Design Highlights:
*   **Artisanal Identity**: I established a "Botanical Atelier" design system using a refined palette of **Greenhouse Deep**, **Petal Cream**, and **Earth Ochre** to reflect the nursery's rooted, artisanal heritage.
*   **Editorial Layout**: The page utilizes intentional whitespace and an asymmetrical layout to move away from generic templates, creating a curated feel that highlights their rare botanicals.
*   **High-Fidelity Imagery**: I generated custom editorial photography, including a moody greenhouse interior and macro botanical textures, to anchor the visual narrative.
*   **Interactive Foundations**: The technical implementation includes a dedicated `#visit` section with a production-ready map slot and a minimalist inquiry form for bespoke consultations.

The result is a unique, memorable first impression that positions Vests Greenhouse as a sophisticated pillar of the Johnson City community.
