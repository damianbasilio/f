Our Story

Deeply Rooted in Johnson City

We are a locally owned and operated greenhouse. Est. in 1968. For over five decades, Vest Greenhouse has been a cornerstone of the Laurels Road community.

What started as a modest local plant nursery has blossomed into a hybrid sanctuary where visitors can enjoy the beauty of nature and the comfort of a home-cooked meal. Our history is etched into every leaf and every recipe.
