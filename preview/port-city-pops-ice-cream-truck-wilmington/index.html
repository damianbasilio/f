Hand-Scooped Joy in Every Stick

Born from a love of long summer days and the crisp breeze of the Atlantic, Port City Pops began in 2014 with a simple mission: to create a premium popsicle experience that captures the essence of Wilmington.

What started as a single humble cart has grown into the coast's favorite vintage-style ice cream truck. We still do things the "old way", slow churning, hand-pouring, and using real ingredients you can recognize. No shortcuts, just pure, frosty bliss.
