The Brush
Story

Founded on the belief that beauty should be as enduring as it is breathtaking, Brush Nails is more than a salon—it is a sanctuary for the modern individual. Our artists are trained not just in technique, but in the history of design and color theory.

We believe in the "Precision Pulse"—the steady hand, the thoughtful line, and the perfect finish. Every appointment is an intimate collaboration between artist and client, resulting in a transformation that feels uniquely yours.
