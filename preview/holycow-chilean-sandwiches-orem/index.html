OUR STORY

The Flame &
The Bread

Holycow was born from a singular obsession: the perfect crust. We brought the smokey, intense flavors of Chilean street food to the heart of Orem, UT, blending traditional wood-firing techniques with premium local ingredients.

Every sandwich is a tribute to the open fires of the central valley, a marriage of charred bread and succulent meats that tells the story of our heritage with every bite. This isn't just fast food; it's a culinary ritual.
