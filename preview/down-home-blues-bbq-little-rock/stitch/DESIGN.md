I've designed and built a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Down Home Blues BBQ**, moving far beyond a standard local business template to create a unique "Soul of Smoke" identity.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity**: Inspired by vintage blues posters and high-end record covers, the site uses an asymmetric grid with bold, high-contrast serif typography and generous whitespace.
*   **Palette**: A custom **"Midnight & Ember"** scheme—deep charcoal, rich terracotta, and a textured bone white—evokes the moody, soulful atmosphere of a dim-lit blues club.
*   **Rhythmic Layout**: Rather than a standard card grid, I’ve used varying section heights, thick rules, and offset imagery to create a visual rhythm that mirrors a blues baseline.
*   **Editorial Photography**: Custom-generated visuals of pit-fired brisket and a classic hollow-body guitar bridge the gap between world-class barbecue and authentic Delta soul.
*   **Production Ready**: The site features semantic HTML5, responsive layout controls, a custom form validation UI, and the required functional map slot for Little Rock.

The layout is designed to be a memorable "first impression" that positions Down Home Blues BBQ not just as a restaurant, but as a local institution.
