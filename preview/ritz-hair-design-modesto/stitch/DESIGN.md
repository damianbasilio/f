I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Ritz Hair Design**, moving away from standard templates to create a unique visual identity that reflects the premium nature of the Modesto atelier.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Gold & Onyx Palette:** A sophisticated color story using deep charcoal, champagne gold, and chalk white to evoke immediate luxury.
*   **The Curated Grid:** I utilized an asymmetrical layout for the services section, creating a more dynamic, "high-fashion" feel than a standard 3-column row.
*   **Editorial Typography:** Large-scale Playfair Display headings establish a confident, authoritative voice for the brand.
*   **Signature Motif:** Subtle architectural lines and gold-accented UI elements are woven throughout to unify the experience.

The site is fully responsive and includes a custom appointment form, a dedicated service atelier section, and the requested semantic structure for the map slot.
