I've completed the bespoke editorial homepage for **Beautiful Nails**. 

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Layout:** Moving away from standard business templates, I've implemented a high-fashion magazine aesthetic with intentional asymmetry and generous whitespace to elevate the brand's perception.
*   **Visual Motif:** The design uses a sophisticated "Thin-Rule & Texture" motif, featuring 1px borders and a soft champagne/deep plum color story that feels both tactile and premium.
*   **Artistic Imagery:** I've integrated custom-generated photography, including an editorial hero visual and craftsmanship shots, to anchor the salon's identity in artistry rather than just utility.
*   **Bespoke Typography:** By pairing a high-contrast serif for headlines with a clean, wide-spaced sans-serif for body copy, I've established a typographic hierarchy that reflects the precision of their services.
*   **Interactive Foundations:** The page includes a custom contact form, smooth scrolling, and the required interactive map container, all built with semantic, production-ready code.

The result is a memorable first impression that positions **Beautiful Nails** as Manchester's premier sanctuary for elevated nail artistry.
