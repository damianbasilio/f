I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Tans By Steph**, moving away from standard templates to create a high-end, boutique digital presence.

### Key Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Layout:** I used an asymmetrical, split-screen hero and generous whitespace to create a sense of luxury and calm, reflecting the "sanctuary" of the Portland studio.
*   **"Golden Hour" Palette:** The visual identity is anchored in sun-drenched terracotta, oatmeal silk, and burnished gold accents—a color story crafted to feel as warm as a fresh tan.
*   **Sophisticated Typography:** I paired a high-contrast serif for headings with a clean, wide-tracked sans-serif to achieve that "fashion-magazine" feel.
*   **Unique Motifs:** Thin rule lines and oversized typographic initials provide a distinct visual anchor for each section, ensuring the site feels designed rather than assembled.
*   **Production-Ready:** The code is semantic, fully responsive, and includes the required map slot and form validation UI.
