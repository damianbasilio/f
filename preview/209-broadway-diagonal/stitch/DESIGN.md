I have completed the production-ready landing page for **209 Broadway** (The Honey Hole). 

### Design Rationale:
*   **Brand Identity:** I established a rugged, warm, and energetic aesthetic using a "Honey Gold" (#DAA520) and deep charcoal palette to reflect the "real country roadhouse" vibe described in their Facebook content.
*   **Content-First Approach:** Every headline and copy block was pulled directly from their recent Facebook posts and "About" section to ensure authenticity.
*   **Asymmetric Layout:** I used an intentional, non-template grid for the services section (Live Bands, Cold Drinks, etc.) to give the site a bespoke, hand-crafted feel that matches a local neighborhood favorite.
*   **Conversion Focused:** The "Call Now" CTA and phone number are prominently featured in the header and hero sections to drive immediate inquiries.
*   **Technical Compliance:** The page includes a semantic HTML structure, a functional mobile navigation menu, smooth-scroll links, and the specific map slot requirement for your deployment.
