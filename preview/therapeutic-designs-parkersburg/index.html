The Hands Behind the Craft

Where Industrial Precision Meets Organic Warmth.

Based in our Ohio Valley studio in Parkersburg, West Virginia, Therapeutic Designs represents a legacy of meticulous attention to detail.

We believe that the spaces we inhabit are silent participants in our well-being. By combining surgical-grade precision with the warmth of natural materials, we create artifacts and environments that heal the soul as much as they please the eye.
