I've completed the bespoke homepage for **Sun Rayz Tan & Wellness Center**. 

The design moves away from generic local-business templates, opting for a **"Sunset Editorial"** aesthetic that balances luxury wellness with high-intensity tanning technology.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity:** A premium palette of *Afterglow Amber*, *Evening Shadow Navy*, and *Beach Sand Cream* creates a warm, sophisticated atmosphere that reflects the Pensacola coast.
*   **Editorial Layout:** I used an asymmetrical hero section and arched visual motifs to break the traditional grid, giving the site a curated, high-end feel.
*   **Custom Typography:** Playfair Display headlines provide a sense of luxury and authority, while Inter ensures modern readability.
*   **Bespoke Sections:**
    *   **Credibility:** An understated social proof ticker that focuses on longevity and expertise.
    *   **Services:** A unique arched card grid featuring the specific data from your service menu.
    *   **The Studio:** A clean, minimal visit section with the requested functional map slot and detailed hours.
    *   **Inquiry Within:** A minimalist contact form using refined underlined inputs rather than standard boxes.

The code is fully production-ready, featuring semantic HTML5, responsive CSS with custom properties, and smooth-scroll interactions.
