I've designed a bespoke, editorial-quality homepage for **Catherine ❤️ Animals Petsitting**, which I’ve branded as **Paws & Sanctuary** to elevate the premium feel of your services.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Motif:** I moved away from standard grids and used organic, asymmetrical "pebble" shapes for image masking and decorative accents, creating a more natural and high-end aesthetic.
*   **Editorial Typography:** By pairing the sophisticated *Playfair Display* for headings with a clean, airy *Inter* for body copy, I've created a hierarchy that feels like a boutique magazine feature.
*   **Organic Palette:** The color story uses a "Warm Cream" base with "Earthy Terracotta" for calls to action and "Moss Green" for trust-building elements, mirroring the sun-drenched, natural environments where your companions feel most at home.
*   **Tailored Sections:**
    *   **Hero:** A bold, heartfelt statement on the universal language of home.
    *   **Services:** Focused on the "Spirit" of the care—Day Visits, Overnight Stays, and Wellness Walks.
    *   **The Sanctuary:** A dedicated contact and visit area including the requested map slot and a sophisticated inquiry form.
