Parisian Spirit,
High Desert Heart.

Madame Matisse is a sanctuary where the saturated colors of the Fauves meet the sun-bleached textures of New Mexico. We celebrate the intersection of refined European sensibility and the rugged, authentic soul of the West.

Our studio overlooks the Sangre de Cristo Mountains, serving as a quiet witness to the ever-changing light that inspired our name and our philosophy.
