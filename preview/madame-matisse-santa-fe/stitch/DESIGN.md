I have designed a bespoke, award-winning digital home for **Madame Matisse** in Santa Fe. 

This design departs from standard business templates to offer an **editorial-quality experience** that bridges the gap between French modernism and Southwestern soul.

### Design Highlights:
*   **The 'Terra & Indigo' Narrative**: A unique color story that pairs the warm, grounded tones of Santa Fe adobe with a rich, intellectual indigo—a nod to the artistic heritage of Henri Matisse.
*   **Artisanal Layout**: I moved away from rigid grids in favor of **asymmetric balance** and **overlapping elements**, reflecting the organic, "cut-out" shapes found in Matisse's later work.
*   **Typography as Identity**: High-contrast serif headlines in *Playfair Display* provide a sophisticated, editorial rhythm, while generous whitespace ensures a quiet, premium feel.
*   **Bespoke Visuals**: I generated custom imagery—including a premium hero and a sun-drenched studio shot—to establish an immediate sense of "place" and high-fashion craftsmanship.
*   **Production-Ready Structure**: The site includes a dedicated #visit section with your specific map panel, a responsive "Inquire" form with custom validation UI, and a mobile-optimized navigation system.
