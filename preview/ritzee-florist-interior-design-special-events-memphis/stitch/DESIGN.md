I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade landing page for **Ritzee Florist | Interior Design & Special Events** that moves away from generic layouts to create a truly unique brand presence.

### Design Highlights:
- **Editorial Aesthetic:** I've utilized an asymmetric grid and fine-line borders to mimic high-end design monographs, ensuring the site feels curated rather than assembled.
- **Memphis Soul:** The "Midnight Bloom & Gold" palette combines deep charcols with warm golds, evoking a sophisticated yet soulful local heritage.
- **Strong Typography:** A high-contrast pairing of *Playfair Display* and *Jost* establishes a clear visual hierarchy, elevating the brand's prestige.
- **Custom Visual Motifs:** I've incorporated signature image treatments—such as overlapping text and offset borders—to create a memorable, non-standard visual rhythm.
- **Production-Ready Code:** The delivered code is fully semantic, responsive, and includes the requested map slot and inquiry form.

The design features high-fidelity photography generated specifically for the brand, including a moody floral arrangement, a macro botanical shot, and a luxury interior detail to showcase the breadth of Sharon Coleman's expertise.
