I've completed the bespoke homepage for **Spa for Paws**. This design breaks away from standard local business templates, adopting a high-end editorial aesthetic that reflects the premium, sanctuary-like quality of the brand.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Typography:** I used a high-contrast serif (Playfair Display) for headlines to create a sophisticated, magazine-style feel that communicates "Master-level artistry."
*   **Asymmetric Layout:** By utilizing staggered image placements and overlapping elements, the site feels designed and intentional, moving away from generic grid systems.
*   **Custom Brand Assets:** I generated three unique editorial images—a serene hero shot, a macro tool close-up, and an architectural interior view—to establish a cohesive visual story of calm and luxury.
*   **"Serene Paws" Palette:** The color story is grounded in Deep Moss Green and Cream, providing a calming backdrop that emphasizes the premium nature of the spa services.
*   **Production-Ready:** The page is fully responsive, includes a custom map slot as requested, and features a refined inquiry form to begin the client journey.
