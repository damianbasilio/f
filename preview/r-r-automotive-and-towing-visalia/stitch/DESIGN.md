I have designed a bespoke, premium landing page for R&R Automotive and Towing. Moving away from standard local-business layouts, I've implemented an **Industrial Editorial** aesthetic that emphasizes the business's heritage and reliability in Visalia.

### Design Highlights:
- **Visual Identity**: A high-contrast "Oil & Steel" palette using deep charcoal and safety orange accents.
- **Typographic Authority**: Large-scale, industrial-grade headlines that establish "Visalia's Iron Standard."
- **Editorial Layout**: Asymmetric grids and heavy structural borders provide a custom, high-end feel that differentiates R&R from typical Facebook-only competitors.
- **Narrative Photography**: I've integrated cinematic, high-contrast imagery of heavy-duty recovery and mechanical precision to build immediate trust.
- **Strategic Utility**: The "Emergency 24/7" action is prioritized in the navigation, while the service and location sections provide clear, authoritative information for both routine and urgent needs.

The site is fully responsive, including a dedicated map slot for your Visalia headquarters and a functional service request interface.
