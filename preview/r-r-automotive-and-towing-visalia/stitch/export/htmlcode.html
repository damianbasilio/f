Grit &
Integrity

Established in 1992, R&R Automotive and Towing was built on a simple premise: do the job right, no matter how heavy the load. We aren't just mechanics; we're the backbone of Visalia's infrastructure, ensuring that the wheels of commerce and community keep turning.

Our facility at 1912 East Mineral King Avenue serves as a sanctuary for machines and a source of relief for drivers. We've weathered the storms, the heatwaves, and the shifting technologies of the automotive world, always coming out stronger and more capable.

When you call R&R, you're not getting a faceless dispatch. You're getting local experts who know every backroad in Tulare County.
