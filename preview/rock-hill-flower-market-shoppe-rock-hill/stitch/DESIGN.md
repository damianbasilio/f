I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade digital home for **Rock Hill Flower Market Shoppe**. 

This design breaks away from generic local-business templates to create a high-fashion, "Rustic High-Fashion" identity. It features a sophisticated serif-led typographic hierarchy, generous whitespace, and a custom color palette of Stem Green, Petal Cream, and Pressed Rose.

### Key Design Highlights:
- **Asymmetric Hero Section**: Features large-scale botanical imagery paired with elegant serif typography to establish immediate premium credibility.
- **Editorial Layout**: Non-standard sectioning and overlapping elements mirror the organic yet precise nature of professional floral design.
- **Custom Visual Identity**: A unique "floating leaf" motif and thin hairline borders provide a modern, architectural feel that distinguishes the brand from competitors.
- **Production-Ready**: The site includes all required sections, including a specialized map slot, a minimalist contact form, and high-fidelity floral imagery.
