I've built a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **D & D Wrecker Inc.** that establishes a premium "Industrial Prestige" identity.

### Design Rationale:
- **Visual Identity:** I moved away from common towing tropes, choosing a sophisticated **Midnight and Industrial Ochre** palette. This reflects the scale and professionalism of their heavy-recovery and police impound operations.
- **Editorial Layout:** The site uses oversized high-contrast serif typography (**Playfair Display**) and an asymmetric grid to create a "unique, memorable first impression" rather than a standard service template.
- **Intentional Density:** I chose a layout with generous whitespace balanced by bold, dense typographic blocks to convey the "Heavy Lift" authority of the brand.
- **Memorable Motif:** A recurring vertical line and "cable" motif act as structural dividers throughout the page.
- **Production-Ready:** The page includes the required emergency dispatch header, a specialized services list, a legacy-focused about section, and the mandated empty map slot for the facility location.

### Key Sections:
1. **Hero:** A high-impact introduction featuring heavy-duty recovery imagery.
2. **Credibility:** Bold metrics highlighting their 24/7 readiness and specialized police impound status.
3. **Services:** A modern, list-style layout focused on mechanical authority.
4. **Visit & Contact:** A clean, industrial interface for dispatch and location details.

The site is fully responsive and ready for production use.
