OUR LEGACY

A LEGACY OF RECOVERY

What began in 1974 as a modest local service has evolved into the Quad Cities' most sophisticated specialized recovery operation. D&D Wrecker Inc. doesn't just tow vehicles; we manage the heavy logistics of Davenport’s arterial flow.

Our evolution has been defined by a relentless commitment to engineering precision. Every rigging operation is a calculation, every recovery is a project. We provide the mechanical authority required to handle the region's most challenging industrial and municipal scenarios.
