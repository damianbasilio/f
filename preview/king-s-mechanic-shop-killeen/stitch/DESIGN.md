I have designed and built a bespoke, editorial-style homepage for **King's Mechanic Shop**. 

This design breaks away from standard local-business templates by adopting a **"Precision & Grit"** visual identity. It balances the raw industrial nature of automotive repair with the high-precision surgical approach King's is known for.

### Design Highlights:
- **Visual Identity**: A sophisticated "Heavy-Duty Editorial" aesthetic using a dark mode palette with Texas Ochre (#D4AF37) accents.
- **Typographic Hierarchy**: Bold, high-impact headings that mirror vintage shop signage while maintaining a modern, premium feel.
- **Imagery**: I've integrated high-end, moody photography including technical engine details and master-technician close-ups to emphasize expertise.
- **Layout**: An asymmetrical, grid-based layout that provides a unique rhythm as users scroll through services like "Transmission Surgery" and "Vintage Restoration."
- **Production-Ready Features**: The site is fully responsive, features a sticky emergency contact bar, a dedicated "Visit" section with the requested map slot, and a streamlined service request form.
