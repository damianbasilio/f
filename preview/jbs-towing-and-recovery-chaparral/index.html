Our Story

Built in Chaparral.

Founded on the principles of reliability and grit, JBS Towing and Recovery was born out of the necessity for a truly professional heavy-duty service in the Southern New Mexico region.

We aren't just a towing company; we are recovery specialists. Our team is trained to handle the unique challenges of the high desert, from shifting sands to extreme temperature shifts that affect heavy machinery.

Every rig in our fleet is maintained to the highest industrial standards, ensuring that when you call for help, we arrive with the hardware and expertise to resolve the situation safely and efficiently.
