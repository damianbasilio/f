I've designed a bespoke, premium landing page for **JBS Towing and Recovery** that captures the rugged, high-performance nature of their work in Chaparral, NM.

### Design Rationale:
*   **Visual Identity:** Established a "Desert Midnight" palette using deep charcoal, hazard orange, and sandstone to create an authoritative and established feel.
*   **Typography:** Utilized bold, condensed neo-grotesk headings to echo industrial machinery and professional reliability.
*   **Layout:** Moved away from generic templates in favor of a cinematic, high-contrast hero section and an asymmetrical service showcase that highlights their specialized recovery capabilities.
*   **Content:** Integrated their actual contact details and local New Mexico roots into the copy to build immediate trust with local customers.

### Key Features:
*   **Production-Ready:** Full semantic HTML5, CSS3 with custom variables, and vanilla JS for interactivity (mobile menu, smooth scroll, form UI).
*   **Industrial Imagery:** Custom-generated cinematic assets featuring heavy machinery and desert landscapes to ground the brand in its specific environment.
*   **Conversion Focused:** A prominent, fixed "Dispatch" CTA ensures emergency service is always one click away.
