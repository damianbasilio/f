OUR HERITAGE

Stay Sharp isn't just a name; it's a command. Founded on the principles of traditional barbering, we believe that a man's appearance is his signature. In our Cedar Rapids studio, time slows down. We honor the tools of the trade, the weighted steel of the razor, the density of the brush, while embracing modern techniques that ensure every cut is contemporary.

Our founder, 3D, established this space to be more than a shop. It is a sanctuary of style and a community cornerstone where excellence is the only standard.
