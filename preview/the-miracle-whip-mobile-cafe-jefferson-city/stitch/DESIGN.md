I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **The Miracle Whip Mobile Cafe**, crafted to deliver a premium first impression that feels distinctly "Jefferson City."

### Design Highlights:
*   **Artisan Aesthetic:** I established an "Espresso & Cream" visual identity, pairing deep charcoal and rich coffee tones with warm, whipped-cream surfaces and burnt orange accents.
*   **Editorial Layout:** Instead of a standard grid, the site uses an asymmetrical, non-linear layout with generous whitespace, thin rule lines, and large-scale typographic motifs (like the "MO" watermark) to evoke the feel of a high-end food magazine.
*   **Signature Imagery:** I integrated high-fidelity photography including a sleek Airstream cafe in an urban setting, moody "whipped" latte art close-ups, and vibrant lifestyle food shots.
*   **Bespoke Sections:**
    *   **Hero:** Features a sophisticated overlapping layout and bold "Crafted in Missouri" branding.
    *   **Services:** An asymmetrical grid highlighting Daily Stops and Event Catering with intentional density.
    *   **Visit:** A clean, minimal section with the exact location and a production-ready interactive map slot.
    *   **Inquiry Form:** A custom-styled contact form designed for high conversion, utilizing the brand's accent colors.
