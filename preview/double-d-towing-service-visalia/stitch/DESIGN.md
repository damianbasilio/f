I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Double D Towing Service** that departs from the standard local business template. 

### Design Rationale:
*   **Editorial Authority**: I utilized a high-contrast dark theme with an industrial palette of Asphalt, Zinc, and Caution Gold to establish a premium, professional presence that feels unique in the towing industry.
*   **Structural Asymmetry**: The layout uses intentional whitespace and oversized, condensed typography (Archivo Narrow) to create a "magazine spread" feel, moving away from generic card grids.
*   **Industrial Motif**: Heavy horizontal rules and structural numbering (01, 02, etc.) for the services section emphasize mechanical precision and reliability.
*   **Visual Storytelling**: The hero section features cinematic automotive photography to immediately signal the scale and capability of the Double D fleet.

The site is production-ready with semantic HTML, responsive CSS, and smooth-scroll interactivity.

### Key Features:
*   **Distinctive Services Layout**: A structured vertical list that prioritizes readability and hierarchy.
*   **The "Double D Standard"**: A dedicated editorial section highlighting the business's roots in Visalia and its technical expertise.
*   **Functional Infrastructure**: Includes a custom contact inquiry form and a dedicated slot for map integration.
