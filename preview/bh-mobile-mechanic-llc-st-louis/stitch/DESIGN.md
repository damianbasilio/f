I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **BH Mobile Mechanic LLC** that departs from standard local-business templates in favor of a high-end "Industrial Precision" aesthetic.

### Design Rationale:
- **Visual Identity**: I established a "blueprint" motif using a dark, high-contrast palette (St. Louis Asphalt and High-Vis Amber) paired with a technical grid and oversized condensed typography. This positions the brand as a premium engineering service rather than just a local garage.
- **Asymmetrical Layout**: The layout uses intentional whitespace and non-traditional alignment to create a memorable, editorial feel that stands out from competitors.
- **Custom Imagery**: I generated three high-fidelity editorial assets: a cinematic hero shot of the service van in an industrial St. Louis setting, a macro precision shot of tools for technical services, and a gritty warehouse portrait for the brand manifesto.
- **Technical Integrity**: The page is built with semantic HTML, CSS custom properties for scalability, and includes the specific map-slot requirements and St. Louis-based content.

The result is a production-ready, mobile-responsive landing page that makes a powerful and unique first impression.
