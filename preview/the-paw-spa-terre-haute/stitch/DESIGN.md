I've completed the production-ready homepage for **The Paw Spa**, transforming its Facebook presence into a professional digital storefront.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Brand-Specific Strategy**: I built a custom design system using a soft "Spa Mint" and warm neutral palette to evoke the luxury and care of a professional pet spa.
*   **Split-Hero Layout**: The hero section features high-fidelity photography of a clean, happy "pup" to immediately establish a premium but welcoming tone for Terre Haute residents.
*   **Real Data Integration**: Every detail—from the names of the groomers (Brianna, Taylor, Kaylee, and Edward) to the 4.8-star rating and the specific location near Haute City Center—was pulled directly from their social context.
*   **Conversion-Focused**: A sticky header ensures the "Call Now" button and phone number are always accessible, while the service cards clearly outline the "Spa Day" experience.
*   **Visit & Contact**: I included the requested map slot and a validated contact form to streamline the booking process for local pet owners.

The site is fully responsive and uses semantic HTML/CSS to ensure a fast, high-quality experience on any device.
