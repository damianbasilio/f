I've designed a bespoke, "Precision Craft" homepage for Ward Wash Mobile Detailing that elevates the brand beyond its Facebook roots into a premium Omaha automotive institution.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity**: Built on a palette of **Omaha Midnight** (deep charcoal) and **Turbo Orange** accents, creating a high-contrast, mechanical aesthetic that reflects technical precision.
*   **Editorial Layout**: The design breaks away from generic templates with generous whitespace, sharp technical rule-lines, and immersive, high-contrast photography of ceramic coatings and meticulous interior work.
*   **Interactive Credibility**: A dedicated "Omaha Trusted" section highlights the brand's local authority with live statistics and a 5-star standard.
*   **Precision Details**: Every service tier is presented with technical specifications, treated as "maintenance tiers" rather than simple washes, reinforcing the luxury positioning.

The site is production-ready with semantic HTML, a distinctive CSS token system, and a custom map slot for your Omaha service radius.
