I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Royalty Nail Spa & Lounge**, crafted to deliver a premium first impression that moves far beyond standard local business templates.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Identity:** Established a "Midnight Velvet & Gold" aesthetic, utilizing deep navy backgrounds and rich gold accents to evoke a sense of exclusivity and royalty.
*   **Editorial Layout:** I moved away from common grid structures in favor of an asymmetric, magazine-style layout. This includes overlapping typographic elements, thin geometric rules, and generous whitespace to create a high-fashion feel.
*   **Bespoke Sections:**
    *   **Hero:** A cinematic introduction using high-fidelity imagery of bespoke nail artistry.
    *   **Curated Rituals:** A stylized services menu that treats each offering as a "ritual" rather than just a price list.
    *   **The Sanctuary:** A dedicated about section that focuses on the sensory experience and atmosphere of the lounge.
*   **Production-Ready:** The site is fully responsive, features smooth-scroll navigation, and includes a specialized map container ready for integration.

The result is a digital presence that reflects the "sanctuary-grade" experience of your physical location in Cleveland Heights.
