*   **Typographic Hierarchy**: A high-contrast Serif (Playfair Display) for headlines provides an editorial feel, paired with a wide-tracked Sans-serif for a modern, airy UI.

## Brand & Style

The brand personality is rooted in the concept of "The Sanctuary." It departs from the clinical, sterile aesthetic of traditional grooming services to embrace a boutique, editorial lifestyle feel. The target audience is discerning pet owners who view their animals as family and value artisanal care over industrial speed.

The design style is **Editorial Minimalism** with a **Tactile** edge. It utilizes generous whitespace (the "Warm Cream" canvas) and sophisticated typography to create a sense of calm and exclusivity. The emotional response should be one of immediate relief and trust—an intimate, professional service that arrives at the doorstep. The visual narrative is supported by organic "bubble" geometries and soft wave patterns that reference water and lather without being literal or cartoonish.

## Colors

The palette is organic and grounded, eschewing "utility blue" for high-end interior tones.

- **Sage Green (#4A5D4E):** Used for primary actions, navigation headers, and core branding elements. It evokes nature and tranquility.
- **Warm Cream (#F9F7F2):** The primary surface color. It provides a softer, more premium feel than pure white, reducing eye strain and feeling more "residential."
- **Deep Charcoal (#2C2C2C):** Reserved for typography and high-contrast iconography. It ensures legibility and adds an authoritative weight.
- **Terracotta (#D68C78):** A soft accent used sparingly for notifications, specific CTAs (like "Book Now"), or highlighting artisanal details.

The color system relies on high-contrast pairings: Deep Charcoal text on Warm Cream backgrounds, and Warm Cream text on Sage Green components.

## Typography

The typography system creates an editorial rhythm. **Playfair Display** provides the sophisticated, "serif-first" look required for a lifestyle brand, while **DM Sans** offers a clean, modern contrast.

For display styles, the serif is used to anchor the page, often in Deep Charcoal. For body text, the sans-serif is given generous tracking (letter-spacing) to improve readability and evoke a sense of "breathable" luxury. The `label-caps` style is specifically designed for small navigational cues or category headers, using wide tracking to maintain the high-end feel.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout follows a **Fluid Grid** model with generous padding to prevent content crowding. 

- **Desktop:** A 12-column grid with 64px side margins. Elements often span the center 8 columns for an editorial, focused reading experience.
- **Mobile:** A 4-column grid with 20px side margins.
- **Rhythm:** An 8px base unit drives all spacing. For this design system, "Space is Luxury." Use larger gaps (48px, 64px, 80px) between sections to allow the organic shapes and typography to stand out. 

The "Wave" pattern should be used as a divider between major vertical sections, moving slightly to create a dynamic but subtle sense of water flow.

## Elevation & Depth

This design system avoids heavy shadows. Depth is created through **Tonal Layers** and **Subtle Outlines**.

1.  **Surfaces:** The primary background is Warm Cream. Secondary surfaces (like cards) use a slightly lighter tint or a very thin (0.5px) Sage Green border at 20% opacity.
2.  **Shadows:** When necessary for interactivity (e.g., a floating booking button), use a "Botanical Shadow"—a soft, diffused drop shadow with a slight Sage Green tint (#4A5D4E at 8% opacity) instead of pure black.
3.  **Glassmorphism:** Use "Lather Blurs" (backdrop filters) for navigation bars, allowing the organic background patterns to peek through with a soft-focus effect.

## Shapes

The shape language is defined by the **Organic Circle**. While standard UI components follow a `Rounded` (0.5rem) logic for usability, the decorative elements use varying radii to simulate bubbles.

- **Primary Containers:** Use the `rounded-lg` (1rem) for most cards.
- **Decorative Masks:** Images of pets should often be clipped into imperfect circular "bubble" shapes or soft-edged squircles rather than sharp rectangles.
- **Buttons:** Fully pill-shaped (rounded-full) to echo the "lather" motif.

## Components

- **Buttons:** Primary buttons are Sage Green with Warm Cream text, pill-shaped, and use `label-caps` typography. Secondary buttons use a Sage Green outline.
- **Cards:** Use a Warm Cream surface with a very subtle Sage Green 0.5px border. No heavy shadows. Top-right corners of cards can feature a decorative "bubble" accent.
- **Input Fields:** Minimalist. Only a bottom border in Sage Green, becoming thicker on focus. Labels sit above in `label-caps`.
- **Chips/Badges:** Used for service types (e.g., "Full Groom," "Bath Only"). These are Terracotta with white text, or Sage Green with a light tint, always pill-shaped.
- **Lists:** Traditional bullets are replaced by small, soft Sage Green dots or custom "paw" icons in the brand's secondary color.
- **Floating Action:** The "Book Now" button should be a prominent Terracotta circle in the bottom right of the mobile view, appearing as a floating bubble.
