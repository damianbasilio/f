I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Diamonds Nail Salon** that positions the business as Lancaster's premier luxury destination for nail artistry.

### Design Highlights:
- **Editorial Aesthetic:** Moving away from standard business layouts, I've used an asymmetric mosaic approach for the services and a high-fashion hero section to establish an immediate "premium" brand identity.
- **Sophisticated Palette:** The "Obsidian & Pearl" theme uses deep blacks, crisp whites, and gold accents to mirror the luxury of the services provided.
- **Visual Motif:** I've integrated thin gold rule lines and generous whitespace to create a sense of breathability and high-end craftsmanship.
- **Bespoke Content:** All copy is tailored to the Lancaster location and the salon's specific focus on "precision" and "bespoke art."
- **Technical Excellence:** The site includes a custom map slot, a fully responsive sticky navigation with a dedicated booking CTA, and a production-ready contact architecture.
