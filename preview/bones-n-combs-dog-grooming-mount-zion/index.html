The Founder

Rickey's Story

Bones n Combs was born from a singular vision: to treat every canine guest as a living masterpiece. Founded by Rickey, our atelier moves away from the chaotic, high-volume models of commercial salons. We believe in the luxury of time.

By focusing on one guest at a time, we build a foundation of trust that transcends a simple haircut. It is a one-on-one bond that ensures your pet feels seen, respected, and truly cared for in a serene, boutique environment.
