I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Nails & Spa Center**, specifically tailored for a premium Roanoke audience. 

### Key Design Decisions:
- **Editorial Layout**: I moved away from standard "box-and-grid" templates, opting for an asymmetrical composition with generous whitespace that mimics a high-end wellness magazine.
- **"The Floating Rule" Motif**: To create a memorable visual identity, I implemented thin, elegant 1px lines and dividers that frame the content and overlap imagery, providing a structured yet airy feel.
- **Terra-Cotta & Silk Palette**: I utilized a sophisticated color story of cream, deep charcoal, and muted terra-cotta to ground the brand in the Roanoke landscape while maintaining a sense of luxury.
- **Typographic Hierarchy**: By pairing a high-contrast serif for headlines with a wide-tracked sans-serif for functional text, I've established an authoritative and refined brand voice.
- **Bespoke Assets**: I integrated custom-generated photography for the salon interior and manicured artistry to ensure the visuals are as unique as the service.

The site is production-ready with semantic HTML5, CSS custom properties for easy branding updates, and responsive navigation for mobile visitors.

How would you like to refine this design? We could explore a dark-mode "after hours" variant or add subtle scroll-triggered animations to enhance the "Floating Rule" motif.
