I've designed a bespoke, "Heavy-Duty Editorial" homepage for Saber Towing & Recovery LLC. 

### Design Strategy:
*   **Visual Identity**: I established a "Force of Service" aesthetic, moving away from generic towing templates. The design uses a high-contrast dark theme (Night Road) paired with "Impact Amber" highlights—mirroring the visibility and reliability of emergency hazard lights.
*   **Typography**: I utilized **Archivo Narrow** for headlines to create a condensed, mechanical feel that conveys industrial strength and precision. 
*   **Layout**: Instead of a standard card grid, I implemented an "Operational Manifest" for services and a "Technical Spec" hero sidebar. This reinforces the brand's positioning as a professional recovery operation rather than just a local service.
*   **Production Readiness**: The code is fully semantic, including a mobile-responsive navigation system, smooth-scroll anchors, and the specific technical requirements for the map slot and contact form.
