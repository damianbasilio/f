I've completed the bespoke homepage for **Patron Barbershop Salon**. 

This design avoids the common "local business" template in favor of an **Editorial Density** aesthetic—inspired by high-end magazine layouts and boutique hospitality.

### Design Highlights:
- **Typographic Authority**: Large, overlapping serif headings create a sense of legacy and confidence, moving away from standard grid-based layouts.
- **Atmospheric Palette**: A rich combination of 'Midnight Slate' and 'Burnished Gold' communicates premium craft and a refined, masculine environment.
- **Cinematic Photography**: I've integrated custom-generated imagery that focuses on the tools of the trade and the sharp, technical precision of the haircuts.
- **Minimalist Credibility**: Instead of generic icons, I've used thin gold hairline separators and structured typography to highlight their history and Winston-Salem roots.
- **Bespoke Services Menu**: A clean, list-style service menu that focuses on readability and high-end positioning.

The site is fully responsive and built with production-ready semantic HTML and CSS. You can find the required map slot and contact details integrated seamlessly into the layout.
