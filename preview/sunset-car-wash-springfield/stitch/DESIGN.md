I have designed and built a bespoke, production-ready homepage for **Sunset Car Wash** that moves away from generic templates to establish a premium, editorial brand identity.

### Design Highlights:
- **Visual Identity:** I developed the "Twilight Horizon" theme, using a deep indigo base contrasted with warm amber and sunset orange accents to reflect the business's name and high-end positioning.
- **Editorial Typography:** High-contrast serif headlines create a sophisticated, magazine-like feel, establishing an immediate sense of quality.
- **Bespoke Layout:**
    - **Hero:** An immersive, full-width section with cinematic imagery of the facility at golden hour.
    - **Narrative Flow:** Instead of a standard grid, I used asymmetrical layouts and large-scale photography to tell the story of "local craftsmanship" and "master detailing."
    - **Service Tiers:** Clearly defined packages with high-fidelity macro photography to emphasize the attention to detail.
- **Functional Precision:** Included a custom appointment request form, a dedicated location section with the required map slot, and a fully responsive structure optimized for a premium desktop experience.

The site is built with semantic HTML and modern CSS, ensuring it's not just a mockup but a high-performance foundation for the brand's digital presence.
