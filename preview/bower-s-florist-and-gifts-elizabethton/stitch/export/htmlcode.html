OUR STORY

Crafting Memories with Every Stem.

What began as a small boutique in 1977 has flourished into Elizabethton’s premier floral destination. Founded by the Bower family, our mission has remained unchanged: to treat every arrangement as a unique piece of sculpture.

We source our blooms from local growers whenever the seasons permit, ensuring that every bouquet reflects the vibrant spirit of the Tennessee landscape. Our designers don't just follow trends—they create them through a deep understanding of botanical form and color theory.
