The Vision

Crafted with Care: Adrian's Story

Founded by Adrian, K-9 Kleaners was born from a simple observation: pets are happiest and most relaxed in their own environment. Traditional salons can be loud, stressful, and overwhelming for many dogs.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between high-end spa aesthetics and the convenience of mobile service. We use only the finest organic products and maintain a quiet, climate-controlled environment where your pet is the sole focus.
