I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Dog Gone Dirty Mobile Dog Grooming** that breaks away from the standard local-business template.

### Design Rationale:
- **'Mud & Suds' Visual Identity**: I established a high-contrast palette of 'Mud' (deep charcoal) and 'Suds' (peppermint teal and cream) to create a premium, spa-like atmosphere that still feels playful and true to the brand name.
- **Editorial Layout**: The homepage features a sophisticated split-screen hero and asymmetrical sections that favor generous whitespace and bold typographic hierarchy. This "magazine-style" approach elevates mobile grooming from a chore to a luxury experience.
- **Custom Visual Motif**: I used "soap-bar" rounded shapes for buttons and floating UI elements, paired with macro-photography of soap bubbles to emphasize the sensory, artisanal quality of your services.
- **Intentional Density**: Rather than a standard card grid, the services are presented in a vertical, overlapping stack, creating a narrative flow that guides potential clients through your process.

This design is unique, memorable, and built to position Dog Gone Dirty as the premier mobile grooming service in your area.
