OUR HERITAGE

From the Streets of Athens to Eugene

Cafe Agora wasn't born in a boardroom; it was born in the bustling markets of Athens and the sun-bleached kitchens of Crete. Our founder brought his grandmother’s secret spice blends and a passion for artisanal pita across the Atlantic to the Willamette Valley.

We believe in the 'Slow Food' philosophy—even when served from a truck. That means 24-hour marinades, hand-kneaded dough, and sourcing our produce from local Eugene farmers who share our respect for the earth.
