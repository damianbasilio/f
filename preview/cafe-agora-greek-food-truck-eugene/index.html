OUR HERITAGE

The Greek Heart of Olive Street.

Born from a family's journey from the rugged coast of Crete to the lush valleys of Oregon, Cafe Agora is more than a food truck, it's a tribute to the ritual of the Agora: the gathering place.

We believe in the power of wood fire, hand-pressed oils, and herbs dried in the Aegean sun. Every gyro served is a piece of our history, hand-crafted with the same intensity as the street vendors of Athens.
