Our Story

Gentle Care by Melissa

With over a decade of experience in pet wellness, Melissa's passion has always been the comfort of our four-legged companions. Watching pets struggle with the loud, chaotic environment of traditional salons led to the creation of Paw & Petal.

Our mobile spa is more than just a convenience; it's a sanctuary. We believe every pet deserves personalized, one-on-one attention without the stress of cages or transport. Our mission is to leave your pet feeling refreshed, loved, and looking their absolute best.
