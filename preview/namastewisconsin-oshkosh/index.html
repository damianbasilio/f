Our Legacy

The Namaste Story

NamasteWisconsin was born from a simple yet profound realization: the quiet power of the Wisconsin landscape mirrors the inner stillness we seek in our practice. Founded in the heart of Oshkosh, our sanctuary is more than just a studio; it is a community anchor for those navigating the complexities of modern life.

We believe that wellness is not a destination, but a rhythmic return to oneself. Our instructors are curated for their deep expertise and their ability to guide with humility and grace. Every class, every session, and every interaction is designed to honor the lineage of yoga while grounding it in the unique tranquility of our Midwestern home.
