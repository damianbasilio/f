## Brand & Style
The design system is built to evoke the warmth of a trusted local neighbor combined with the expertise of a 20-year veteran. The brand personality is "The Compassionate Professional"—deeply personal, calm, and reliable. 

The aesthetic style is **Minimalist-Tactile**. It utilizes heavy whitespace to suggest a clean, stress-free environment for pets, while incorporating soft, layered cards and organic shapes to feel approachable. The focus is on the single-groomer experience, emphasizing the safety and quiet of Brittney’s dedicated care.

**Key Brand Attributes:**
- **Local Identity:** Integration of Hobbs, NM as a community anchor.
- **Legacy:** Highlighting two decades of experience through authoritative but warm typography.
- **Safety:** Using soft teals and creams to lower the heart rate of both the pet owner and the pet.

## Colors
The palette is inspired by natural landscapes and domestic comfort, moving away from "clinical" blues toward "caring" teals.

- **Primary (Deep Teal):** Used for primary buttons, headings, and key brand moments. It represents professionalism and stability.
- **Secondary (Warm Cream):** The main background color. It is softer on the eyes than pure white and feels more domestic and inviting.
- **Tertiary (Chocolate Brown):** Used for body text and iconography. It provides a grounded, organic feel that contrasts beautifully with the cream.
- **Accent (Muted Clay):** Used sparingly for notifications or special call-outs, like the 20-year experience badge.

## Typography
The typography strategy balances the "Family-Run" feel with modern legibility. 

**Plus Jakarta Sans** is used for headings. Its slightly rounded terminals and geometric proportions feel optimistic and friendly without losing professional weight.

**Inter** is utilized for all body copy and UI labels. It provides a clean, systematic contrast to the rounded headings, ensuring that detailed information about grooming services and safety protocols is easily digestible.

- **Emphasis:** Use the Chocolate Brown for body text to maintain a soft, low-contrast reading experience that feels high-end yet accessible.
- **Contact Details:** The phone number (575) 441-7588 should frequently appear in `headline-md` weight to remain highly accessible.

## Layout & Spacing
The layout employs a **Fluid Grid** with generous padding to create a sense of "breathing room." 

- **Full-Bleed Imagery:** Sections highlighting the grooming environment or pets should span the full width of the viewport to create an immersive, emotional connection.
- **Vertical Rhythm:** Large `section-gap` values (120px+) prevent the UI from feeling cluttered. This mimics the "single-groomer" philosophy: one thing at a time, focused and unhurried.
- **Mobile First:** On mobile, margins shrink to 20px, and full-bleed images remain to maximize the visual impact of the pet photography.

## Elevation & Depth
This design system avoids harsh shadows. Depth is communicated through **Tonal Layers** and subtle **Ambient Shadows**.

- **Surface Levels:** The base background is the Secondary Cream. Cards and containers use a pure white background to "pop" slightly forward.
- **Shadows:** When used, shadows should be extremely diffused (e.g., `box-shadow: 0 10px 30px rgba(78, 54, 41, 0.05)`). The shadow color is tinted with Chocolate Brown rather than black to keep the warmth.
- **Interactive States:** Subtle scale-up effects (1.02x) on cards are preferred over heavy shadow changes to maintain the gentle aesthetic.

## Shapes
The shape language is consistently soft. Angularity is avoided to ensure the brand feels safe for pets.

- **Components:** Standard cards and buttons use a 0.5rem (8px) radius. 
- **Large Elements:** Featured containers and full-bleed image masks use a `rounded-xl` (1.5rem / 24px) radius to emphasize the "soft corner" directive.
- **Icons:** Icons should feature rounded caps and corners to match the Plus Jakarta Sans typeface.

## Components
- **Buttons:** Primary buttons are Solid Deep Teal with white text, using `rounded-lg` corners. Ghost buttons use the Deep Teal outline for secondary actions like "View Gallery."
- **Cards:** White backgrounds on the Cream surface. They contain a 1px solid Chocolate Brown border at 10% opacity to provide definition without harshness.
- **Service Chips:** Small, rounded labels for service types (e.g., "Full Groom," "Nail Trim") using a light version of the Deep Teal background with dark Teal text.
- **Experience Badge:** A circular or organic "blob" shape containing the text "20 Years of Experience," using the Accent Clay color to draw the eye immediately.
- **Contact Bar:** A persistent footer or header element featuring the Hobbs, NM location and (575) 441-7588, styled with the Chocolate Brown text for high visibility.
- **Lists:** Bulleted lists use custom "paw" or simple soft-dot icons instead of standard browser bullets.
