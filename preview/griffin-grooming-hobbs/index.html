Consistency is Comfort.

Griffin Grooming is a family-run business born from a lifelong passion for animal care. We believe that pet grooming shouldn't be a stressful production line.

Unlike larger shops where your pet might see a different person every time, here they only ever see Brittney. This build-up of trust leads to a calmer, safer experience, especially for senior pets or those who are "grumpy" about their grooming day.
