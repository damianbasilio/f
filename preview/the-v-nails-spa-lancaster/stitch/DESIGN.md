I've designed a bespoke, editorial-inspired landing page for **The V Nails Spa**, focusing on a premium and memorable first impression.

### Key Design Decisions:
- **Visual Identity:** I established a sophisticated "Obsidian & Silk" palette—deep plum tones paired with champagne and rose gold—to convey luxury and exclusivity.
- **Editorial Layout:** The design uses an asymmetrical grid and generous whitespace, moving away from generic templates to create a high-fashion, boutique feel.
- **Typography:** A high-contrast serif font (Bodoni Moda) was chosen for headlines to provide an authoritative yet elegant voice, complemented by a clean sans-serif for readability.
- **Immersive Imagery:** I integrated high-fidelity, architectural and fashion-focused photography that highlights the atmosphere of the salon and the precision of the artistry.
- **Functional Luxury:** The header and footer are streamlined to prioritize contact and booking, while the "Rituals & Treatments" section uses a curated layout to showcase services as experiences rather than just menu items.

The site is fully responsive, featuring a custom mobile navigation and smooth scroll interactions to ensure the premium experience translates across all devices.
