I have designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **601 Hair - Nails & Spa**. 

### Design Rationale:
*   **Visual Identity**: I established an "Obsidian & Champagne" palette, using deep charcoal surfaces to create a sense of exclusivity and high-contrast serif typography to evoke the feel of a luxury fashion editorial.
*   **Asymmetric Layout**: To avoid a templated look, I utilized overlapping images and text, thin rule lines, and rhythmic whitespace. This creates a curated, gallery-like experience for the visitor.
*   **Editorial Imagery**: I generated custom photography—including a high-end salon interior and detailed hair/nail styling shots—to populate the design with brand-specific visuals.
*   **Signature Details**: The service menu is styled like a high-end ritual list, and the "601 Philosophy" section uses bold pull-quotes to communicate the brand's commitment to intentional beauty.
*   **Production-Ready**: The site includes a sticky minimalist header, a functional contact form, and the requested interactive map slot for the Lancaster, PA location.

The design is fully responsive and uses modern CSS properties for consistent branding across all screen sizes.
