Our Story

Community Roots in Campton

Located at 236 Main St, Halsey's Country Store has been the heartbeat of Campton, KY for generations. We believe a store is more than just a place to buy goods, it's where neighbors meet, problems get solved, and the coffee is always fresh.

Whether you're fixing a leak or looking for a hearty lunch, our staff brings decades of local expertise to every interaction. We're proud to support the growth and maintenance of our beautiful mountain home.
