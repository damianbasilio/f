I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Jeff’s Jamaican Cuisine** that moves far beyond the standard local-business template.

### Design Rationale:
- **Visual Identity**: I established the **"Jungle Gold & Obsidian"** theme—a high-contrast, moody palette using deep blacks, sun-scorched yellows, and tropical greens to evoke the smoky, vibrant atmosphere of a premium Jamaican grill.
- **Editorial Layout**: Rather than a generic grid, the site uses **intentional density and asymmetrical compositions**. The "Menu Edit" section uses vertical typographic anchors, and the "Jeff's Story" section features a sophisticated, layered text-over-image layout.
- **Memorable Motif**: I've implemented a **"Rhythmic Border"** motif—thick, architectural lines that define sections and create a sense of movement and utility-chic, mirroring the structure of a modern food trailer.
- **Premium Hero**: The landing experience is anchored by a high-fidelity, top-down shot of authentic jerk chicken (A premium, editorial-style hero shot of authentic Jamaican jerk chicken with vibrant garnishes, served on a textured charcoal plate. Moody lighting, high contrast, professional food photography, shallow depth of field. Top-down slightly angled view. Tropical accents.), establishing immediate credibility and appetite appeal.
- **Production-Ready**: The code is semantic and fully responsive, featuring a floating navigation bar, a high-energy "Credibility Ticker," and the requested functional map slot for the Lewiston location.

The site is built to serve as a definitive digital anchor for the business, translating its Facebook presence into a premium, memorable first impression.
