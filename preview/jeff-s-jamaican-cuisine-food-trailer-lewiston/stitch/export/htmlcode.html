THE STORY OF JEFF

Jeff's Jamaican Cuisine isn't just about food; it's a culmination of a lifelong journey from the vibrant parishes of Jamaica to the heart of Maine. Our founder, Jeff, believes that every dish should tell a story—one of resilience, rhythm, and spice.

Using recipes passed down through generations and refined in the heat of his kitchen, Jeff brings an uncompromising dedication to authenticity. Every piece of chicken is marinated for 24 hours, every spice blend is handcrafted, and every customer is treated like family.
