I have built a professional, community-rooted homepage for Waggin Tails Pet Care, using the real content and voice from their Facebook presence. 

### Design Rationale:
- **Brand Identity**: I created a custom design system using a soft sage green and warm cream palette to evoke feelings of trust, warmth, and nature.
- **Storytelling**: I integrated the business's unique story—transitioning from 20 years of part-time care to a full-time "dream business"—as a central piece of the "About" section to build an emotional connection with local pet owners.
- **Conversion-Focused**: The layout features a prominent phone number in the header and a "Schedule a Visit" CTA above the fold to drive bookings.
- **Service Highlights**: Dedicated sections for "Snuggle Time" and "Professional Dog Walking" use custom-generated imagery to reflect the business's core values.
- **Local Credibility**: The footer and contact sections prominently display the Brazil, IN location and "Always Open" hours to reinforce their accessibility.

The website is fully responsive and includes a semantic structure with a map slot and contact form ready for your team to integrate.
