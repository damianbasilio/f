I've designed a bespoke, editorial-style homepage for The Food Trucks Plaza that captures the "Industrial Gourmet" essence of the brand.

### Key Design Choices:
- **Visual Identity**: I established a high-contrast dark theme using Deep Charcoal, Burnt Ochre, and a crisp Paper White. This palette, combined with sharp 90-degree corners and a subtle grain texture, creates a modern, urban aesthetic that feels custom-built.
- **Editorial Layout**: The homepage breaks away from standard templates with a bold, asymmetric grid. The hero section uses a split-screen layout with overlapping typography to create depth and a high-fashion editorial feel.
- **Typography**: I used a high-contrast pairing of *Playfair Display* for striking headlines and *Inter* for clean, functional body copy, reinforcing the "Raw Artistry & Urban Utility" theme.
- **Memorable Motif**: A signature "Border & Rule" motif is used throughout the sections to frame images and content, echoing the industrial architecture of the plaza itself.

The site is fully responsive, features smooth scrolling, and includes a production-ready contact form and map placement.
