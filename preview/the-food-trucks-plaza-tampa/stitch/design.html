OUR STORY

STREET FOOD,
ELEVATED.

Born in the heart of Tampa’s industrial corridor, THE FOOD TRUCKS PLAZA was conceived as a rejection of the ordinary. We believe street food shouldn't just be convenient—it should be an event.

We’ve curated a rotating collective of the region's most innovative culinary minds, housing them in a space that respects Tampa's raw architectural heritage while providing a refined dining environment.
