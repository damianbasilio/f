RECLAIMING THE
CONCRETE

The Food Trucks Plaza isn't just a food court. It's an urban reclamation project. We took a dormant industrial lot on West Hillsborough and turned it into a high-voltage hub for culinary entrepreneurs who reject the traditional restaurant model.

Our mission is simple: Aggressive quality. High-contrast atmosphere. Zero compromise. This is where Tampa eats when the sun goes down.
