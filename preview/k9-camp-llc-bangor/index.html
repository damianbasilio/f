The Heritage of
Expert Care

Founded in 2021, K9 Camp LLC was born from a simple realization: Maine's dogs deserve a facility that matches the rugged beauty of their home. We don't believe in "warehousing" pets.

Our philosophy blends the freedom of the outdoors with the security of a luxury estate. Every interaction is rooted in canine psychology, ensuring your dog returns home more balanced, socialized, and happy.
