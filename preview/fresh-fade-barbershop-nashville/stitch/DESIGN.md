I've completed the bespoke homepage for **Fresh Fade Barbershop**, delivering a "Nashville Noir" editorial experience that balances vintage grit with modern refinement.

### Design Highlights:
- **Nashville Noir Aesthetic**: A custom high-contrast palette of Deep Charcoal, Warm Ochre, and Bone creates a premium, moody atmosphere that stands out from generic local business templates.
- **Editorial Layout**: I implemented an asymmetrical grid with generous whitespace and 1px border motifs, ensuring the site feels like a high-end magazine feature rather than a standard landing page.
- **Custom Visuals**:
    - **Hero & Services**: Integrated architectural photography of the Nashville interior and editorial-style portraiture to showcase the craft.
    - **Shared Components**: A custom-built navigation bar and footer ensure consistency and easy access to the **"Book Now"** action.
- **Interactive Elements**:
    - **Visit Section**: Includes the required `map-slot` for your future map integration alongside clear location and hours.
    - **Contact Form**: A minimalist, industrial-style form at the base of the page for direct inquiries.
    - **Technical Excellence**: Full semantic HTML, responsive design for desktop and mobile, and smooth-scroll navigation.

The site is production-ready and designed to leave a memorable first impression for every Nashville client.
