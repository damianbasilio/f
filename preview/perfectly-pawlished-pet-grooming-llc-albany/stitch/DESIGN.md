I have designed a bespoke, premium homepage for **Perfectly Pawlished Pet Grooming LLC**. 

This design breaks away from the typical "local business" template to establish a **Quiet Luxury** visual identity. By combining high-contrast editorial typography (**Bodoni Moda**) with a warm **Champagne and Charcoal** palette, the site positions the business as an award-winning boutique rather than a standard grooming shop.

### Key Design Decisions:
- **Editorial Layout**: I moved away from standard grids to an asymmetrical hero and staggered service sections, creating a more rhythmic, high-end feel.
- **Visual Motif**: Subtle gold hairline rules and circular motifs are used throughout to echo the "polishing" and precision of their work.
- **Custom Assets**: 
    - **Logo**: A minimalist, single-line mark that balances elegance with the pet industry.
    - **Imagery**: High-detail studio photography of a groomed Samoyed and macro shots of professional tools to convey craftsmanship.
- **Functional Boutique Elements**: The navigation features a high-visibility contact number and a "Book Appointment" primary action, tailored for a service-based business.

The site is fully responsive, semantic, and ready for production with custom CSS variables for easy future styling.
