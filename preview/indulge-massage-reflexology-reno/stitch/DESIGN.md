I've completed the bespoke homepage for Indulge Massage & Reflexology. 

### Design Choices & Rationale:
- **Editorial Identity**: I moved away from the standard "spa" aesthetic (blues and teals) and instead used a sophisticated palette of **Keystone Slate, Oatmeal, and Eucalyptus**. This gives the brand a more exclusive, boutique feel that stands out in the Reno market.
- **Asymmetrical Layout**: The hero and service sections use intentional whitespace and an asymmetrical grid to create a sense of movement and modern luxury, moving away from the "boxy" feel of standard templates.
- **Visual Motifs**: I introduced a soft **arch motif** in the hero image and subtle rule lines throughout the site to provide a unique, consistent visual thread that feels high-end and curated.
- **Typography**: The high-contrast pairing of **Playfair Display** (serif) and **Inter** (sans-serif) establishes a strong typographic hierarchy that balances restorative warmth with clinical professionalism.
- **Production-Ready Code**: The design is implemented as a semantic, responsive HTML5 document using CSS Custom Properties for easy maintenance and smooth-scroll interactivity.

### Key Features:
- **Curated Offerings**: A service grid that highlights Therapeutic Massage, Advanced Reflexology, and Holistic Body Rituals.
- **Studio Narrative**: A section dedicated to the "Stillness" of your Reno studio, grounding the brand in its physical location.
- **Production-Ready Visit Section**: Includes a dedicated map slot and a refined booking inquiry form with built-in validation UI.
