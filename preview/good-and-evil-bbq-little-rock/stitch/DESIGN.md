I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Good and Evil BBQ** that captures the unique duality of their brand: the soulful, community spirit of Little Rock ("The Good") and the intense, charred mastery of the pit ("The Evil").

### Design Highlights:
*   **Visual Duality:** The layout uses a high-contrast theme, moving between deep charred blacks and warm bone whites, accented by "Divine Gold" to reflect both the grit and the premium quality of the smokehouse.
*   **Editorial Layout:** I’ve moved away from standard grids in favor of an asymmetric, narrative-driven structure that feels like a premium food magazine.
*   **Atmospheric Photography:** I generated custom, cinematic imagery including a low-key study of glistening smoked brisket and a warm, nostalgic scene of the Little Rock community pit at sunrise.
*   **Authentic Typography:** Headlines use the bold, authoritative **Bodoni Moda** serif, providing a sophisticated slab-serif feel that balances the industrial nature of BBQ with a high-end dining experience.
*   **Functional Polish:** The site includes a fixed navigation bar for easy access to ordering and contact info, a responsive mobile menu, and a custom-styled slot for your map visualization.
