I've designed a bespoke, high-fidelity homepage for **Jadean’s Smokin’ Six O BBQ** that moves away from standard templates and into a premium, editorial aesthetic.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Editorial Layout**: Replaced the typical grid with a bold, single-column stack that uses full-bleed color bands and generous vertical whitespace to create a strong sense of rhythm and focus.
*   **Bespoke Visual Identity**: A dark, moody palette—deep charcoal paired with burnt orange and smoked ochre—reflects the artisanal, pit-smoked nature of the business.
*   **High-Contrast Typography**: I paired a rugged, sophisticated serif for headlines with a clean, wide sans-serif to create a "Pitmaster's Manifest" feel.
*   **Memorable Motifs**: Subtle smoke textures, rough-hewn rule lines, and oversized typographic ornaments provide a unique visual signature.
*   **Production-Ready Sections**: 
    *   A **Hero** section featuring cinematic macro-photography of the brisket.
    *   A **"Patience is our Secret"** about section that highlights the artisanal process.
    *   A **"Find the Smoke"** visit section with the requested functional map placeholder.
    *   A custom **Catering & Events** form with integrated validation UI.

The result is a memorable first impression that positions Jadean's as a premium destination for authentic Richmond BBQ.
