A RICHMOND TRADITION

CRAFTED IN THE HEART OF WESTBRIAR

Jadean's Smokin' Six O began with a simple obsession: to perfect the art of the smoke ring without compromising the integrity of the protein. What started in a backyard has evolved into Richmond's premier destination for high-end barbecue.

Our philosophy is rooted in architectural precision, from the way we stack our oak logs for optimal airflow to the precise angle at which we slice each brisket against the grain. Every tray that leaves our kitchen is a testament to the meticulous labor of the smokehouse.
