I've designed a bespoke, high-fidelity landing page for B&G TowingLLC that breaks away from standard local-business templates to establish a premium "Editorial Industrial" identity.

### Design Highlights:
- **Visual Identity:** Developed a high-contrast palette of Deep Onyx and Safety Orange, paired with bold, condensed typography (Archivo Narrow) to communicate professional reliability and strength.
- **Memorable Motif:** Used architectural "mechanical" details like 1px steel rules and oversized, numbered service blocks (01-04) to create a structured, engineered feel.
- **Editorial Layout:** The hero section uses cinematic photography and asymmetrical spacing to grab attention, while the "By the Numbers" credibility section uses scale to build instant trust.
- **Production-Ready:** Includes the required map slot for Kenosha, a functional emergency dispatch CTA, and a responsive contact form.

The design is optimized for desktop and provides a powerful first impression for the Kenosha community.
