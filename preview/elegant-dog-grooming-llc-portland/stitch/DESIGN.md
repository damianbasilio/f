I have built the production-ready homepage for **Elegant Dog Grooming LLC**, specifically tailored to their Portland location and Facebook presence.

### Design Highlights:
- **Tailored Branding:** Integrated the 'Golden Sage' design system to evoke a warm, professional, and "Elegant" feel that matches the business name.
- **Conversion Focused:** Placed the phone number and "Call Now" CTA prominently in the header and hero sections to drive local bookings.
- **Real Business Context:** Used the exact address, contact info, and 4.3-star review score from their Facebook page to establish immediate trust.
- **Service Clarity:** Dedicated sections for Full Grooming, Bath & Brush, and their popular Self-Wash stations with realistic "Starts at" pricing.
- **Portland Identity:** The layout and copy emphasize their commitment to the Portland community, including a dedicated "Visit Our Studio" section with a placeholder for a local map.

The site is fully responsive, semantically structured, and ready for deployment.
