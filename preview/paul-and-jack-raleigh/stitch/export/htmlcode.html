A LETTER FROM THE FOUNDERS

"We believe that objects should outlast their owners."

Paul & Jack was born from a singular frustration: the vanishing of the permanent. In an era of mass-produced disposability, we chose the path of the slow, the deliberate, and the difficult.

Based in the heart of Raleigh, North Carolina, our workshop is more than a production space. It is a laboratory of legacy. Each piece that leaves our doors carries the weight of its making—the smell of the tannin, the grain of the white oak, and the precision of the hand-turned joint.

Our commitment is simple. We design for the future by honoring the methods of the past. The Raleigh Standard isn't just a tagline; it's a promise of durability that spans generations.

P. & J.
