I have crafted a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Paul & Jack**, designed to establish a premium and memorable first impression. 

### Design Rationale:
*   **Visual Identity:** The "Heritage Editorial" system uses a high-contrast palette of **Midnight Graphite** and **Parchment**, accented by an **Electric Copper** motif. This creates a "modern heritage" feel that separates the brand from generic local business templates.
*   **Typographic Hierarchy:** I paired a dramatic, high-contrast serif (Playfair Display) for headlines with a clean, wide-tracked sans-serif (Inter) for utility, mirroring the craftsmanship of the business itself.
*   **Memorable Motifs:** The layout features 1px architectural copper rules and a recurring "numbered step" (01, 02, 03) system that guides the user through the brand story.
*   **Intentional Density:** I chose to commit to generous whitespace to let the high-fidelity imagery and bold typography breathe, creating an atmosphere of exclusivity and focus.
*   **Production Ready:** The code is fully semantic, includes the required map slot, and features responsive adjustments for a seamless experience on any device.

### Technical Details:
*   **Map Slot:** Implemented exactly as specified: `<div class="map-slot relative overflow-hidden min-h-[400px]" data-map-slot aria-label="Map"></div>`.
*   **Contact Info:** Integrated throughout the header, visit section, and footer for maximum accessibility.
*   **Interactive:** Added smooth scroll and subtle form validation UI previews.
