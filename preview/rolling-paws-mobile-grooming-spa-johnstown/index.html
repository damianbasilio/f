The Visionary

Meet Chris & the
Rolling Spa

I started Rolling Paws with a simple belief: every pet deserves a grooming experience that is as peaceful as it is beautiful. After years in traditional salons, I saw the stress that cages and wait times put on both pets and their parents.

Our mobile spa is an extension of my home, a place of artisanal care where we take our time, use only the finest natural products, and focus entirely on the companion in front of us. It’s not just a service; it’s a standard of care.

Chris Baranowski

Founder & Master Groomer
