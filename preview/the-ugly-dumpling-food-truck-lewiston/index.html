Our Philosophy

GRITTY BUT
GOURMET

We started The Ugly Dumpling because we were tired of "refined" dining. We wanted the steam, the noise, and the absolute chaos of the Asian night markets brought to the heart of Maine.

Authentic Chaos

We don't over-process. Our dumplings look raw because they are real. Every fold is unique, every bite is a statement of culinary independence.

Lewiston Bound

We're committed to our community. We source 80% of our ingredients from within a 50-mile radius of Canal Street. This is local fuel.
