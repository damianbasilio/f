Where Your Family is Treated Like Our Own.

Founded in 2014 by lifelong canine enthusiasts, Bonesboro was born from a simple realization: the pets of Jonesboro deserved more than just a cage. They deserved a heritage—a place where the care was as robust as the bond they share with their humans.

Our facility combines "Dog-Friendly Brutalism"—functional, safe, and indestructible architecture—with the warmth of a premium home environment. Every staff member is certified in canine behavioral science, ensuring that your pet's emotional well-being is prioritized alongside their physical comfort.
