I've designed a bespoke, editorial-grade homepage for **Bonesboro Dog Daycare & Boarding**, moving far beyond a standard local business template to create a premium, memorable brand presence.

### Design Highlights:
*   **Modern Heritage Aesthetic**: I established a custom visual identity using a "Dog-Friendly Brutalism" approach. This combines a sophisticated palette—**Bone Cream**, **Bark Charcoal**, and a punchy **Fetch Ochre**—with sharp, 2px borders and a signature "bone-notched" corner motif on cards and sections.
*   **Editorial Typography**: Massive, high-contrast headings in *Playfair Display* establish immediate authority and a premium feel, while tight, wide-set sans-serif body copy ensures readability.
*   **Bespoke Visuals**: I generated custom, editorial-style photography to ground the design: a majestic hero portrait of a Golden Retriever, a dynamic "daycare in action" shot, and a high-detail craftsmanship close-up for the brand's premium positioning.
*   **Jonesboro Identity**: The copy is tailored specifically to the local community, emphasizing Bonesboro as the premier destination for discerning pet parents at County Road 712.
*   **Production-Ready Structure**: The site is built with semantic HTML, CSS custom properties for easy theme management, and includes a dedicated, accessible map slot and a refined contact system.

The result is a unique, high-fidelity experience that communicates luxury, expertise, and a genuine love for dogs.
