OUR STORY

A HERITAGE
OF HEAT

Sweet Bonez started in a backyard off 15th Street with nothing but a rusted offset smoker and a family recipe for white sauce that dates back four generations.

We believe BBQ is the ultimate equalizer. In the pit, there are no shortcuts. You can't rush fire, and you certainly can't rush history. Every rack of ribs we serve carries the weight of Alabama's smoke-stained legacy.
